# Voz Pasiva en Ingles

## Que es la voz pasiva

En la **voz activa**, el sujeto hace la accion. En la **voz pasiva**, el sujeto recibe la accion. En ingles se usa MUCHO mas que en espanol, especialmente en noticias, ciencia y contextos formales.

| Activa | Pasiva |
|--------|--------|
| The dog bit the man. | The man was bitten by the dog. |
| They make cars in Germany. | Cars are made in Germany. |
| Someone stole my phone. | My phone was stolen. |

> **Nota:** Usamos pasiva cuando no sabemos quien hizo la accion, cuando no importa, o cuando queremos enfatizar el objeto.

---

## Estructura

**Sujeto + BE (conjugado) + Participio Pasado (+ by + agente)**

El verbo "be" cambia segun el tiempo verbal. El participio pasado no cambia.

---

## Pasiva en todos los tiempos

| Tiempo | Activa | Pasiva |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Present Simple | They clean the office every day. | The office is cleaned every day. |
| Present Continuous | They are cleaning the office now. | The office is being cleaned now. |
| Past Simple | They cleaned the office yesterday. | The office was cleaned yesterday. |
| Past Continuous | They were cleaning the office. | The office was being cleaned. |
| Present Perfect | They have cleaned the office. | The office has been cleaned. |
| Past Perfect | They had cleaned the office. | The office had been cleaned. |
| Future Simple | They will clean the office. | The office will be cleaned. |
| Future Perfect | They will have cleaned the office. | The office will have been cleaned. |
| Modal | They can clean the office. | The office can be cleaned. |
| Modal Perfect | They should have cleaned the office. | The office should have been cleaned. |

## Ejemplos por tiempo verbal

### Present Simple Passive

1. **English is spoken in many countries.** (El ingles se habla en muchos paises.)
2. **Coffee is grown in Colombia.** (El cafe se cultiva en Colombia.)
3. **The museum is visited by thousands of people.** (El museo es visitado por miles de personas.)
4. **Emails are sent every day.** (Se envian correos todos los dias.)
5. **This car is made in Japan.** (Este coche se fabrica en Japon.)

### Past Simple Passive

1. **The book was written by Cervantes.** (El libro fue escrito por Cervantes.)
2. **America was discovered in 1492.** (America fue descubierta en 1492.)
3. **The window was broken by a ball.** (La ventana fue rota por una pelota.)
4. **Three people were injured in the accident.** (Tres personas fueron heridas en el accidente.)
5. **The house was built in 1950.** (La casa fue construida en 1950.)

### Present Perfect Passive

1. **The report has been finished.** (El informe ha sido terminado.)
2. **Two suspects have been arrested.** (Dos sospechosos han sido arrestados.)
3. **The email has already been sent.** (El correo ya ha sido enviado.)
4. **Nothing has been decided yet.** (Nada ha sido decidido todavia.)
5. **The project has been approved by the manager.** (El proyecto ha sido aprobado por el gerente.)

### Future & Modal Passive

1. **The meeting will be held on Friday.** (La reunion se celebrara el viernes.)
2. **The issue must be resolved immediately.** (El problema debe ser resuelto inmediatamente.)
3. **New employees will be trained next week.** (Los nuevos empleados seran formados la proxima semana.)
4. **This can be done in five minutes.** (Esto puede hacerse en cinco minutos.)
5. **The results should be published soon.** (Los resultados deberian publicarse pronto.)

## Cuando usar la pasiva

| Usa pasiva cuando... | Ejemplo |
|----------------------|---------|
| No sabes quien hizo la accion | My bike was stolen. (No se quien lo robo.) |
| No importa quien lo hizo | The bridge was built in 1920. (No importa quien.) |
| Es obvio quien lo hizo | He was arrested. (Obvio que la policia.) |
| Quieres ser formal/impersonal | Mistakes were made. (Tipico de politicos.) |
| En ciencia y noticias | The experiment was conducted. / Five people were killed. |

## Errores comunes de hispanohablantes

| Incorrecto | Correcto | Por que |
|------------|----------|---------|
| The house was build in 1990. | The house was **built** in 1990. | Build es infinitivo, built es participio |
| It was happened yesterday. | It **happened** yesterday. | Happen es intransitivo, no tiene pasiva |
| The movie was enjoyed by me. | **I enjoyed** the movie. | No uses pasiva cuando la activa suena mas natural |
| Was stolen my phone. | My phone **was stolen**. | El sujeto va primero en ingles |
| The book is write by her. | The book is **written** by her. | Participio, no infinitivo |

> **Consejo practico:** Si puedes decirlo en activa de forma clara y natural, usa activa. La pasiva existe para cuando el "quien" no importa o no se sabe.
