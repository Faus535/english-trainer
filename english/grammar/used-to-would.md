# Used to / Would — Habitos y Estados del Pasado

## Used to

Describe habitos o estados que existian en el pasado pero **ya no**. Es como decir "solia" en espanol.

### Estructura

| Tipo | Estructura | Ejemplo |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Afirmativa | Sujeto + used to + verbo base | I used to smoke. (Yo solia fumar.) |
| Negativa | Sujeto + didn't use to + verbo base | I didn't use to like coffee. (No me gustaba el cafe.) |
| Pregunta | Did + sujeto + use to + verbo base? | Did you use to play football? (¿Solias jugar futbol?) |

> **Atencion con la ortografia:** En negativa y pregunta se escribe "use to" (sin d) porque el auxiliar "did" ya lleva el pasado.

### Ejemplos — Habitos pasados

1. **I used to walk to school every day.** (Solia caminar al colegio todos los dias.)
2. **She used to play the piano when she was young.** (Solia tocar el piano cuando era joven.)
3. **We used to live in a small town.** (Soliamos vivir en un pueblo pequeno.)
4. **They used to go to the beach every summer.** (Solian ir a la playa cada verano.)
5. **He used to work at a restaurant.** (Solia trabajar en un restaurante.)

### Ejemplos — Estados pasados

1. **I used to be very shy.** (Solia ser muy timido.)
2. **She used to have long hair.** (Solia tener el pelo largo.)
3. **This building used to be a hospital.** (Este edificio solia ser un hospital.)
4. **I used to love chocolate.** (Me encantaba el chocolate. / Solia adorar el chocolate.)
5. **He used to know the answer.** (El solia saber la respuesta.)

## Would (para habitos pasados)

"Would" tambien describe habitos repetidos del pasado, pero con una diferencia importante:

| Used to | Would |
|---------|-------|
| Habitos Y estados | SOLO habitos (acciones repetidas) |
| I used to **be** shy. ✓ | I would **be** shy. ✗ |
| I used to **walk** to school. ✓ | I would **walk** to school. ✓ |
| I used to **have** a dog. ✓ | I would **have** a dog. ✗ |
| I used to **play** every day. ✓ | I would **play** every day. ✓ |

> **Regla:** "Would" NO se usa con verbos de estado (be, have, like, love, know, live, believe). Solo con verbos de accion repetida.

### Ejemplos con Would

1. **Every summer, we would go to my grandmother's house.** (Cada verano, ibamos a casa de mi abuela.)
2. **He would always tell the same joke.** (Siempre contaba el mismo chiste.)
3. **She would read for hours before bed.** (Leia durante horas antes de dormir.)
4. **On weekends, we would play football in the park.** (Los fines de semana, jugabamos futbol en el parque.)
5. **My dad would wake up at 5 AM every day.** (Mi padre se levantaba a las 5 AM todos los dias.)

## Used to vs Would — Cuando usar cada uno

| Situacion | Usa | Ejemplo |
|-----------|-----|---------|
| Habito pasado (primera mencion) | used to | I **used to** go fishing with my dad. |
| Habito pasado (continuando la historia) | would | We **would** leave at dawn and **would** spend the whole day at the lake. |
| Estado pasado | used to (nunca would) | I **used to** live in Madrid. |
| Habito con emocion/nostalgia | would | She **would** always laugh at my jokes. |

> **Consejo de estilo:** "Used to" introduce el tema. "Would" continua con los detalles. Es comun usarlos juntos:
> "I **used to** live near the beach. Every morning, I **would** walk along the shore and **would** watch the sunrise."

## Be used to / Get used to (NO confundir)

Estas estructuras son completamente diferentes a "used to":

| Estructura | Significado | Ejemplo |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| used to + verbo base | Solia (pasado) | I **used to** smoke. (Ya no fumo.) |
| be used to + -ing/nombre | Estar acostumbrado a | I **am used to** smoking. (Estoy acostumbrado.) |
| get used to + -ing/nombre | Acostumbrarse a | I'm **getting used to** waking up early. (Me estoy acostumbrando.) |

### Ejemplos de contraste

1. **I used to drive on the right.** (Solia conducir por la derecha — ya no lo hago.)
2. **I am used to driving on the right.** (Estoy acostumbrado a conducir por la derecha — lo hago sin problema.)
3. **I'm getting used to driving on the left.** (Me estoy acostumbrando a conducir por la izquierda — aun me cuesta.)

## Errores comunes de hispanohablantes

| Incorrecto | Correcto | Por que |
|------------|----------|---------|
| I use to play football. | I **used to** play football. | En afirmativa siempre "used to" con d |
| I didn't used to like it. | I didn't **use to** like it. | Negativa: "use to" sin d (did lleva el pasado) |
| I would be thin when young. | I **used to be** thin when young. | Would no va con verbos de estado |
| I'm used to wake up early. | I'm used to **waking up** early. | Be used to + gerundio (-ing) |
| I used to living here. | I used to **live** here. | Used to + verbo base (infinitivo sin to) |
