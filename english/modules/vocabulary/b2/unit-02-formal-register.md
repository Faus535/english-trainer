# Unit 02 — Formal Register

## Objetivo

Aprender 10 conectores y adverbios de registro formal que transforman tu escritura y discurso de "correcto" a "profesional". Estas palabras aparecen en informes, correos formales, textos legales y presentaciones. No las usas en una cerveza con amigos, pero son imprescindibles para emails de trabajo, ensayos y reuniones formales.

## Palabras

| English | IPA | Spanish | Example | Trampa/Note |
|---------|-----|---------|---------|-------------|
| furthermore | /ˌfɜːr.ðɚˈmɔːr/ | ademas, es mas | **Furthermore**, the data supports our initial hypothesis. | Mas formal que "also" o "besides". Usado para anadir argumentos en cadena. |
| consequently | /ˈkɑːn.sə.kwent.li/ | en consecuencia, por consiguiente | Sales dropped; **consequently**, we had to reduce staff. | Causa → efecto. Mas formal que "so" o "therefore". |
| notwithstanding | /ˌnɑːt.wɪðˈstæn.dɪŋ/ | no obstante, a pesar de | **Notwithstanding** the challenges, the project was completed on time. | Muy formal. Puede ir antes o despues: "notwithstanding the risks" o "the risks notwithstanding". |
| henceforth | /ˌhensˈfɔːrθ/ | de ahora en adelante, en lo sucesivo | **Henceforth**, all employees must use the new system. | Muy formal/legal. En conversacion diria "from now on". |
| whereby | /werˈbaɪ/ | por el cual, mediante el cual | They created a system **whereby** employees can work remotely. | Relativo formal. Reemplaza "by which" o "through which". Comun en contratos. |
| albeit | /ɔːlˈbiː.ɪt/ | aunque, si bien | He accepted the offer, **albeit** reluctantly. | Pronunciacion: /ɔːlˈbiː.ɪt/ (tres silabas). Siempre va seguido de adjetivo o adverbio, NO de clausula completa. |
| thus | /ðʌs/ | asi, por lo tanto | The experiment failed; **thus**, we modified our approach. | Mas formal que "so". Puede significar "de esta manera" o "por lo tanto". |
| hence | /hens/ | de ahi, por eso | The roads were icy; **hence** the accident. | Puede ir sin verbo: "Hence the delay" = de ahi el retraso. Tambien: "hence" = por eso. |
| nonetheless | /ˌnʌn.ðəˈles/ | sin embargo, no obstante | The plan was risky. **Nonetheless**, they decided to go ahead. | Sinonimo de "nevertheless". Mas formal que "but" o "however". |
| pursuant | /pɚˈsuː.ənt/ | conforme a, en virtud de | **Pursuant to** our agreement, payment is due within 30 days. | Muy formal/legal. Siempre "pursuant TO". Solo en documentos legales o corporativos. |

## Ejercicios

### Fill in the blank

1. The budget was cut by 40%. _______, several projects were cancelled.
2. _______, the candidate has extensive experience in machine learning.
3. _______ the difficulties, the team delivered the project on time.
4. _______, all communication must go through the official channels.
5. They developed a platform _______ users can share resources.
6. The results were modest, _______ encouraging.
7. The evidence was insufficient; _______, the case was dismissed.
8. He's young; _______ his lack of experience.
9. It was a controversial decision. _______, it proved to be the right one.
10. _______ article 5 of the contract, the tenant must pay a deposit.

1. Consequently
2. Furthermore
3. Notwithstanding

5. whereby
6. albeit

### Match formal → informal equivalent

| Formal | Informal |
|--------|----------|
| 1. furthermore | a) but / still |
| 2. consequently | b) from now on |
| 3. nonetheless | c) also / besides |
| 4. thus | d) so / as a result |
| 5. henceforth | e) so / that's why |

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1-c, 2-d, 3-a, 4-e, 5-b

</details>

### Rewrite in formal register

1. "Also, we found more problems." →
2. "So, we changed the plan." →
3. "It was hard, but we finished." →
4. "From now on, everyone has to use the new system." →
5. "The weather was bad. That's why we stayed home." →

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. "Furthermore, additional issues were identified."
2. "Consequently, the plan was modified."
3. "Notwithstanding the difficulties, the project was completed." / "It was challenging; nonetheless, it was completed."
4. "Henceforth, all personnel are required to utilize the new system."
5. "The weather was inclement; hence, we remained at home." / "The weather was poor; thus, we stayed home."

</details>

## Repaso

| Front | Back |
|-------|------|
| ademas (formal) | **furthermore** — /ˌfɜːr.ðɚˈmɔːr/ — Furthermore, we found new evidence. |
| en consecuencia | **consequently** — /ˈkɑːn.sə.kwent.li/ — Consequently, the project was delayed. |
| aunque (+ adj/adv) | **albeit** — /ɔːlˈbiː.ɪt/ — A good result, albeit unexpected. |
| sin embargo (formal) | **nonetheless** — /ˌnʌn.ðəˈles/ — Nonetheless, we proceeded. |
| de ahi, por eso | **hence** — /hens/ — Hence the confusion. |
