## Mini-Test (20 ejercicios)

### Section A — Fill in the blank (8 points)

1. There's a _______ difference between the two wines — most people can't tell them apart.
2. The project failed; _______, the team had to start over with a new approach.
3. It suddenly _______ _______ me that I had been wrong all along.
4. Nobody wants to mention _______ _______ _______ _______ _______ — the CEO is the problem.
5. She felt _______ (power + less) to change the situation.
6. He's not frugal — he's _______ (negative: tacano).
7. We'll _______ the new version to production tonight.
8. The design team needs to _______ on the prototype based on user testing.

2. consequently
3. dawned on
4. the elephant in the room

</details>

### Section B — Choose the correct option (6 points)

9. She did a very (thorough / through) investigation.
    - a) thorough (exhaustivo)
    - b) through (a traves de)

10. The results were good, (albeit / although) slightly below expectations.
    - a) albeit (+ adj/adv)
    - b) although (+ clause)

11. We can't (rule out / iron out) the possibility of delays.
    - a) rule out (descartar)
    - b) iron out (resolver detalles)

12. Losing that job was (a blessing in disguise / back to square one).
    - a) a blessing in disguise (algo malo que resulto bien)
    - b) back to square one (volver al principio)

13. "She always asks about my personal life." → She's (curious / nosy).
    - a) curious (+)
    - b) nosy (−)

14. We don't have the (bandwidth / throughput) to take on another project.
    - a) bandwidth (capacidad disponible)
    - b) throughput (capacidad de procesamiento)

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

9. a) thorough
10. a) albeit (seguido de adjetivo "slightly below")
11. a) rule out (descartar posibilidad)
12. a) a blessing in disguise
13. b) nosy (connotacion negativa, entrometida)
14. a) bandwidth (sentido figurado: capacidad personal)

</details>

### Section C — Translate to English (6 points)

</details>
