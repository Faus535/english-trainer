# Unit 05 — Word Formation

## Objetivo

Aprender los prefijos y sufijos mas productivos del ingles para que puedas decodificar palabras desconocidas sin diccionario. Dominar la formacion de palabras multiplica tu vocabulario: si conoces "help" y el sufijo "-ful/-less", ya conoces "helpful" y "helpless". Esta unidad te da las herramientas para descifrar miles de palabras nuevas.

## Prefijos principales

| Prefix | Meaning | Examples | Trampa/Note |
|--------|---------|----------|-------------|
| un- /ʌn/ | no, opuesto | unhappy, unable, uncertain, unfair, unlikely | El mas comun. NO todos los opuestos usan "un-": "possible → impossible" (no "unpossible"). |
| re- /riː/ | otra vez, de nuevo | redo, rewrite, rebuild, reconsider, rethink | Cuidado: "recover" no es "re-cover" (cubrir de nuevo). "Recover" = recuperarse. |
| mis- /mɪs/ | mal, incorrectamente | misunderstand, mislead, misjudge, misplace, misbehave | "Misunderstand" = malinterpretar. Muy util y muy usado. |
| over- /ˈoʊ.vɚ/ | demasiado, en exceso | overwork, overreact, overestimate, overlook, overcome | "Overlook" = pasar por alto (no "mirar por encima"). "Overcome" = superar. |
| under- /ˈʌn.dɚ/ | insuficiente, por debajo | underestimate, underpay, undervalue, underperform, undergo | "Underestimate" = subestimar. Opuesto de "overestimate". |

## Sufijos principales

| Suffix | Creates | Examples | Trampa/Note |
|--------|---------|----------|-------------|
| -ness /nəs/ | sustantivos (de adjetivos) | happiness, sadness, weakness, kindness, awareness | Adj → Noun. "Happy → happiness". Duplica la consonante si hay "y": happy → happiness. |
| -ment /mənt/ | sustantivos (de verbos) | development, improvement, achievement, agreement, management | Verb → Noun. "Develop → development". No todos: "decide → decision" (no "decidement"). |
| -able /ə.bəl/ | adjetivos (capacidad) | affordable, manageable, achievable, remarkable, comfortable | "Afford → affordable" (asequible). Algunos usan "-ible": visible, possible, flexible. |
| -ful /fəl/ | adjetivos (lleno de) | helpful, powerful, successful, meaningful, grateful | "Help → helpful". Siempre UNA "l": "ful" no "full". |
| -less /ləs/ | adjetivos (sin) | helpless, careless, useless, homeless, meaningless | Opuesto de "-ful": helpful ↔ helpless. "Careless" = descuidado. |

<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. unaffordable = no asequible, demasiado caro (un- + afford + -able)
2. mismanagement = mala gestion (mis- + manage + -ment)
3. overreact = reaccionar en exceso (over- + react)
4. underpaid = mal pagado (under- + paid)
5. rethink = repensar (re- + think)
6. carelessness = descuido (care + -less + -ness)
7. powerless = impotente, sin poder (power + -less)
8. achievable = alcanzable (achieve + -able)
9. unmanageable = inmanejable (un- + manage + -able)
10. meaningless = sin sentido (meaning + -less)

### Fill in the blank with a formed word

1. The project was delayed due to _______ (mis + manage + ment).
2. This schedule is _______ (un + manage + able) — I need help.
3. She felt _______ (power + less) to change the situation.
4. His _______ (care + less + ness) caused the accident.
5. We should _______ (re + consider) our strategy.

<details>

1. mismanagement
2. unmanageable
3. powerless
4. carelessness
5. reconsider
