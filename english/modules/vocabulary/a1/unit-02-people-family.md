# Unit 02 — People & Family

## Objetivo

Activar 10 sustantivos de personas y relaciones que reconoces al leer pero que no te salen de forma automatica cuando hablas de tu entorno. Muchas de estas palabras las confundes o evitas porque no estas seguro de la pronunciacion.

## Palabras

| English | IPA | Spanish | Example |
|---------|-----|---------|---------|
| friend | /frend/ | amigo/a | My best **friend** lives in another city, so we talk on the phone every week. |
| husband | /ˈhʌz.bənd/ | esposo, marido | Her **husband** works as an engineer. |
| wife | /waɪf/ | esposa, mujer | My **wife** and I have been married for ten years. |
| son | /sʌn/ | hijo | Their **son** is starting university next year. |
| daughter | /ˈdɔː.tər/ | hija | My **daughter** wants to be a doctor when she grows up. |
| boss | /bɒs/ | jefe/a | My **boss** is very demanding, but fair. |
| colleague | /ˈkɒl.iːɡ/ | companero/a de trabajo | I'm having lunch with a **colleague** today. |
| neighbor | /ˈneɪ.bər/ | vecino/a | Our **neighbor** has a very loud dog. |
| customer | /ˈkʌs.tə.mər/ | cliente | The **customer** complained about the service. |
| stranger | /ˈstreɪn.dʒər/ | desconocido/a, extrano/a | Don't open the door to **strangers**. |

### Trampas y notas

- **son** /sʌn/ vs. **sun** /sʌn/: Suenan EXACTAMENTE igual. Solo el contexto los diferencia.
- **daughter**: La pronunciacion es traicionera — NO se dice "dau-g-ter". La "gh" es muda: /ˈdɔː.tər/.
- **colleague**: No es "colega" en el sentido informal espanol. En ingles es estrictamente "companero de trabajo". Para "colega/amigo" se usa "mate" (UK) o "buddy" (US).
- **stranger** vs. **foreigner**: "Stranger" = alguien que no conoces. "Foreigner" = alguien de otro pais. No los mezcles.
- **customer** vs. **client**: "Customer" compra productos o servicios puntuales. "Client" tiene una relacion profesional continua (abogado, consultor).

## Ejercicios

### Fill in the blank

1. My _______ and I are going on vacation next month. We've been married for five years.
2. Is she your _______? — No, we just work together. She's my _______.
3. Their _______ is only two years old. He's learning to walk.
4. A _______ knocked on my door and tried to sell me something.
5. The _______ asked for a refund because the product was damaged.
6. I can't complain to my _______. He decides if I get a raise.
7. Our _______ plays loud music every Saturday night.
8. They have two children: a _______ and a _______.
9. She's my best _______. We've known each other since school.
10. I don't feel comfortable talking to _______ at parties.

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. wife / husband
2. friend / colleague
3. son
4. stranger
5. customer
6. boss
7. neighbor
8. son / daughter (o al reves)
9. friend
10. strangers

</details>

### Translate to English

1. Mi vecino tiene tres hijos.
2. Mi jefa quiere hablar conmigo manana.
3. No hables con desconocidos.
4. Un companero de trabajo me recomendo este restaurante.
5. Mi hija tiene los mismos ojos que su madre.

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. My neighbor has three children (sons).
2. My boss wants to talk to me tomorrow.
3. Don't talk to strangers.
4. A colleague recommended this restaurant to me.
5. My daughter has the same eyes as her mother.

</details>
