## Mini-test (20 ejercicios)

### Seccion A: Fill in the blank (10 puntos)

1. I _______ at 6 am but I don't _______ until 6:30.
2. My _______ and I have been married for 15 years. We have a _______ and a _______.
3. I need to go to the _______ to return some books. Then I'll stop at the _______ to get cash.
4. Have you finished _______? — No, I'm _______ working on it.
5. Can we have the _______, please? And I'd like to leave a _______ for the _______.
6. She was _______ when nobody told her about the surprise party. But then she was _______ when she found out!
7. The _______ caused terrible _______ in the valley. Many houses were damaged.
8. I _______ go to the gym. Maybe five times a week.
9. Don't talk to _______. And always tell your _______ where you're going.
10. We _______ found a restaurant after walking for 30 minutes. The _______ was delicious.

## Trampas mas comunes — Recordatorio final

| Trampa | Correcto | Incorrecto |
|--------|----------|------------|
| library ≠ libreria | library = biblioteca | libreria = bookshop |
| embarrassed ≠ embarazada | embarrassed = avergonzado | embarazada = pregnant |
| eventually ≠ eventualmente | eventually = al final | eventualmente = possibly |
| still vs. yet | still = sigue pasando / yet = no ha pasado aun | No mezclarlos |
| bored vs. boring | I'm bored = estoy aburrido | I'm boring = soy aburrido (aburro a otros) |
| recipe | /ˈres.ɪ.pi/ (3 silabas) | No "re-saip" |
| dessert vs. desert | dessert /dɪˈzɜːrt/ = postre | desert /ˈdez.ərt/ = desierto |
