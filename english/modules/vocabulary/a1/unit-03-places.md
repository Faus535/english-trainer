# Unit 03 — Places

## Objetivo

Activar 10 lugares comunes que reconoces al leer pero que dudas al pronunciar o usar en contexto. Estos son sitios a los que vas regularmente o que necesitas mencionar cuando das o pides indicaciones.

## Palabras

| English | IPA | Spanish | Example |
|---------|-----|---------|---------|
| airport | /ˈer.pɔːrt/ | aeropuerto | We need to be at the **airport** two hours before the flight. |
| hospital | /ˈhɒs.pɪ.təl/ | hospital | She was taken to the **hospital** after the accident. |
| station | /ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ | estacion | The train **station** is a ten-minute walk from here. |
| office | /ˈɒf.ɪs/ | oficina | I'll be at the **office** until 6 pm today. |
| bank | /bæŋk/ | banco | I need to go to the **bank** to withdraw some money. |
| church | /tʃɜːrtʃ/ | iglesia | There's a beautiful old **church** in the town center. |
| library | /ˈlaɪ.brər.i/ | biblioteca | I borrowed this book from the **library**. |
| park | /pɑːrk/ | parque | Let's take the kids to the **park** this afternoon. |
| beach | /biːtʃ/ | playa | We spent the whole day at the **beach**. |
| bridge | /brɪdʒ/ | puente | You need to cross the **bridge** to get to the other side of the river. |

### Trampas y notas

- **library** vs. **bookshop/bookstore**: "Library" = biblioteca (prestas libros gratis). "Bookshop" = libreria (compras libros). FALSO AMIGO clasico: "libreria" en espanol NO es "library".
- **hospital**: En ingles se dice "go to THE hospital" (US) o "go to hospital" (UK, sin "the"). Ojo con el articulo.
- **station**: No solo de trenes. "Gas station" (gasolinera), "police station" (comisaria), "fire station" (estacion de bomberos).
- **office**: "Post office" = correos. "Box office" = taquilla. "Office" solo = tu lugar de trabajo.
- **bank**: Tambien significa "orilla" de un rio ("river bank"). No confundir en contexto.

## Ejercicios

### Fill in the blank

1. The flight leaves at 8 am, so we should arrive at the _______ by 6 am.
2. I study at the _______ because it's quiet and free.
3. Is there a _______ near here? I need to get some cash.
4. The kids love playing in the _______ after school.
5. We went to the _______ and built sandcastles all morning.
6. He was in the _______ for two weeks after his surgery.
7. My _______ is on the third floor of that building.
8. The _______ over the river was built 200 years ago.
9. Which _______ does the train to Madrid leave from?
10. The old _______ in the village is over 500 years old.

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. airport
2. library
3. bank
4. park
5. beach
6. hospital
7. office
8. bridge
9. station
10. church

</details>

### Translate to English

1. La biblioteca esta cerrada los domingos.
2. ¿Donde esta la estacion de tren mas cercana?
3. Trabajo en una oficina en el centro de la ciudad.
4. Fuimos a la playa el sabado pasado.
5. El puente esta cortado por obras.

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. The library is closed on Sundays.
2. Where is the nearest train station?
3. I work in an office in the city center.
4. We went to the beach last Saturday.
5. The bridge is closed for construction (roadworks).

</details>
