# Unit 05 — Emotions Advanced

## Objetivo

Ir mas alla de "happy", "sad", "angry". En B1, necesitas nombrar emociones complejas para contar como te sientes realmente, entender letras de canciones, seguir series y tener conversaciones profundas. Estas 10 palabras cubren el espectro emocional que los hispanohablantes mas necesitan.

## Palabras

| English | IPA | Spanish | Example | Trampa/Note |
|---------|-----|---------|---------|-------------|
| overwhelmed | /ˌoʊ.vɚˈwelmd/ | abrumado, desbordado | I feel **overwhelmed** with all this work. | Puede ser negativo (estres) o positivo (emocion): "overwhelmed with joy". |
| frustrated | /ˈfrʌs.treɪ.t̬ɪd/ | frustrado | I'm **frustrated** because nothing seems to work. | "Frustrated" es por obstaculos. "Angry" es por ofensas. No son lo mismo. |
| grateful | /ˈɡreɪt.fəl/ | agradecido | I'm **grateful** for your help. | "Grateful FOR something" o "grateful TO someone". NO "grateful of". |
| disappointed | /ˌdɪs.əˈpɔɪn.t̬ɪd/ | decepcionado | I was **disappointed** with the results. | "Disappointed WITH/BY" (algo). "Disappointed IN" (alguien). NO "I am disappoint". |
| anxious | /ˈæŋk.ʃəs/ | ansioso, inquieto | She feels **anxious** about the exam. | Pronunciacion: /ˈæŋk.ʃəs/ no /ˈæn.ʃi.ous/. Tambien puede significar "deseoso": "anxious to please". |
| nostalgic | /nɑːˈstæl.dʒɪk/ | nostalgico | Looking at old photos makes me feel **nostalgic**. | La "g" suena /dʒ/ no /g/. "Nostalgic FOR/ABOUT something". |
| bitter | /ˈbɪt̬.ɚ/ | amargado, resentido | He's still **bitter** about losing the promotion. | No solo es "amargo" (sabor). Como emocion = resentimiento prolongado. |
| thrilled | /θrɪld/ | emocionadisimo, encantado | I'm **thrilled** to be here! | Mas fuerte que "happy" o "excited". "Thrilled" = extasis de felicidad. Pronuncia la "th": /θrɪld/. |
| indifferent | /ɪnˈdɪf.ɚ.ənt/ | indiferente | He seemed completely **indifferent** to her problems. | "Indifferent TO" no "indifferent about" (aunque "about" se oye informalmente). |
| resentful | /rɪˈzent.fəl/ | resentido, rencoroso | She's **resentful** towards her parents for being so strict. | Mas especifico que "bitter". "Resentful" = sientes que algo fue injusto. "Resentful TOWARDS/OF". |

## Ejercicios

### Fill in the blank

1. I'm so _______ with work right now — I have five deadlines this week.
2. She felt _______ when she didn't get the job after three interviews.
3. I'm really _______ for everything you've done for me.
4. He gets very _______ before flying — he hates planes.
5. The song made me feel _______ about my childhood summers.
6. I'm _______ because I keep making the same mistake.
7. She was _______ when she heard she got into Harvard.
8. He's become _______ since the divorce — he doesn't trust anyone.
9. The CEO seemed completely _______ to the employees' complaints.
10. She's _______ towards her brother because he got more attention as a child.

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. overwhelmed
2. disappointed
3. grateful
4. anxious
5. nostalgic
6. frustrated
7. thrilled
8. bitter
9. indifferent
10. resentful

</details>

### Translate to English

1. Estoy agradecido por esta oportunidad.
2. Me siento abrumado con tanto trabajo.
3. Ella esta decepcionada con los resultados del examen.
4. No seas tan amargado — la vida sigue.
5. Estoy emocionadisimo con las noticias.

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. I'm grateful for this opportunity.
2. I feel overwhelmed with so much work.
3. She's disappointed with the exam results.
4. Don't be so bitter — life goes on.
5. I'm thrilled about the news.

</details>

### Preposition check — Fill in WITH, FOR, ABOUT, TO, or TOWARDS

1. grateful _______ your support
2. disappointed _______ the service
3. anxious _______ the future
4. indifferent _______ the outcome
5. resentful _______ his colleagues

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. grateful **for** your support
2. disappointed **with** the service
3. anxious **about** the future
4. indifferent **to** the outcome
5. resentful **towards** his colleagues

</details>
