## Mini-Test (20 ejercicios)

### Section A — Fill in the blank (10 points)

1. The _______ of the experiment was unexpected — nobody predicted that result.
2. She's very _______ — she donated her entire bonus to charity.
3. I just _______ _______ that the meeting was cancelled.
4. The _______ shows that regular sleep improves memory.
5. I feel _______ by all the responsibilities they've given me.
6. The company's _______ grew by 30%, but profit stayed the same.
7. That TikTok video _______ _______ and got 50 million views in a week.
8. We need to _______ awareness about cybersecurity in schools.
9. I _______ work from home, not at the office. (= actualmente)
10. She's not really sick — she's just _______ to avoid the meeting. (= fingiendo)

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. outcome
2. generous
3. found out
4. research / evidence
5. overwhelmed
6. revenue
7. blew up
8. raise
9. currently (NOT "actually")
10. pretending (NOT "intending")

</details>

### Section B — Choose the correct option (5 points)

11. He's too (stubborn / determined) to listen to anyone. He drives me crazy.
    - a) stubborn ← negativo
    - b) determined ← positivo

12. I'm really (grateful / thrilled) for your help with the project.
    - a) grateful
    - b) thrilled

13. We need to (meet / complete) the deadline by Friday.
    - a) meet
    - b) complete

14. After the breakup, it took her months to (get over / get through) it.
    - a) get over
    - b) get through

15. The (evidence / analysis) clearly proves he was there that night.
    - a) evidence
    - b) analysis

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

11. a) stubborn (connotacion negativa — "drives me crazy")
12. a) grateful (agradecido POR algo)
13. a) meet (meet a deadline)
14. a) get over (superar emocionalmente)
15. a) evidence (prueba concreta)

</details>

### Section C — Translate to English (5 points)

16. Necesitamos un enfoque diferente para este problema.
17. Ella esta decepcionada con los resultados.
18. Al final acabamos cenando en casa.
19. Asisti a una conferencia muy interesante ayer.
20. Este modelo de negocio es escalable y tiene buen retorno de inversion.

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

16. We need a different **approach** to this problem.
17. She's **disappointed** with the results.
18. We **ended up** having dinner at home.
19. I **attended** a very interesting conference yesterday. (NOT "assisted")
20. This business model is **scalable** and has a good **ROI** (return on investment).

</details>
