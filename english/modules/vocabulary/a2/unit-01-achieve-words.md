# Unit 01 — Achieve Words

## Objetivo

Activar 10 verbos de logro y accion que ves constantemente en articulos, correos de trabajo y noticias, pero que sustituyes por verbos mas simples ("do", "make", "have") cuando hablas. Estos verbos te hacen sonar profesional y preciso.

## Palabras

| English | IPA | Spanish | Example |
|---------|-----|---------|---------|
| achieve | /əˈtʃiːv/ | lograr, conseguir | She **achieved** her goal of running a marathon before turning 30. |
| improve | /ɪmˈpruːv/ | mejorar | I want to **improve** my English pronunciation this year. |
| develop | /dɪˈvel.əp/ | desarrollar | The company is **developing** a new product for the European market. |
| increase | /ɪnˈkriːs/ (v) | aumentar | They need to **increase** production to meet demand. |
| reduce | /rɪˈdjuːs/ | reducir | We should **reduce** our spending this month. |
| maintain | /meɪnˈteɪn/ | mantener | It's hard to **maintain** a healthy diet when you're busy. |
| require | /rɪˈkwaɪər/ | requerir, necesitar | This job **requires** at least three years of experience. |
| provide | /prəˈvaɪd/ | proporcionar, proveer | The hotel **provides** free Wi-Fi and breakfast. |
| support | /səˈpɔːrt/ | apoyar, dar soporte | My family always **supports** my decisions, even the crazy ones. |
| create | /kriˈeɪt/ | crear | She **created** a website to sell her paintings. |

### Trampas y notas

- **increase**: Como verbo /ɪnˈkriːs/ (acento en la segunda silaba). Como sustantivo /ˈɪn.kriːs/ (acento en la primera). "We need to inCREASE sales" vs. "There was an INcrease in sales."
- **achieve** vs. **get/reach**: "Achieve" implica esfuerzo y merito. No dices "I achieved a coffee" sino "I achieved my dream." Usalo para logros importantes.
- **require** vs. **need**: "Require" es mas formal. "This position requires a degree" (formal). "I need a coffee" (informal). No uses "require" para cosas cotidianas.
- **provide** vs. **give**: "Provide" es mas formal y sistematico. "The government provides healthcare" (sistema). "She gave me a book" (accion personal).
- **support**: No solo es "soporte tecnico". Tambien es apoyo emocional, economico, etc. "I support my family" = mantengo/apoyo a mi familia.

## Ejercicios

### Fill in the blank

1. The company wants to _______ sales by 20% this year.
2. You need to _______ at least 80% on the test to pass.
3. Regular exercise helps _______ your physical and mental health.
4. The job _______ fluent English and a university degree.
5. The government should _______ more resources for public education.
6. We need to _______ costs if we want to survive this quarter.
7. She _______ a successful business from scratch.
8. You need to _______ your car regularly, or it will break down.
9. The team is _______ a new app that will launch in September.
10. I'll always _______ you, no matter what you decide.

2. achieve

### Translate to English

1. Necesitamos reducir gastos y aumentar ingresos.
2. Este proyecto requiere al menos dos meses de trabajo.
3. Quiero mejorar mi nivel de ingles antes del verano.
4. La empresa proporciona seguro medico a todos los empleados.
5. Logro su objetivo despues de anos de esfuerzo.

5. She/He achieved her/his goal after years of effort.

## Repaso

| Front | Back |
|-------|------|
| lograr (con esfuerzo) | **achieve** — /əˈtʃiːv/ — She achieved her dream of studying abroad. |
| requerir | **require** — /rɪˈkwaɪər/ — The recipe requires fresh ingredients. |
| proporcionar | **provide** — /prəˈvaɪd/ — The school provides free meals. |
| mantener | **maintain** — /meɪnˈteɪn/ — It's important to maintain good habits. |
| aumentar | **increase** — /ɪnˈkriːs/ — Prices increased by 10% last year. |
