## Trampas mas comunes — Recordatorio final A2

| Trampa | Correcto | Incorrecto |
|--------|----------|------------|
| convenient ≠ conveniente (buena idea) | convenient = practico | Para "buena idea" usa "advisable" |
| argument ≠ argumento | argument = discusion | Para argumento logico usa "point" |
| prescription ≠ recipe (de cocina) | prescription = receta medica | recipe = receta de cocina |
| increase (verbo vs. nombre) | Verbo: inCREASE / Nombre: INcrease | Acento en sitio equivocado |
| suggest + gerund | suggest going | suggest to go |
| luggage = incontable | two pieces of luggage | two luggages |
| run out of (inseparable) | run out of milk | run milk out of |
| make a decision | make a decision | do/take a decision |
| pay attention TO | pay attention to the teacher | pay attention the teacher |
| comfortable = 3 silabas | CUMF-tuh-bul | com-FOR-ta-ble |
