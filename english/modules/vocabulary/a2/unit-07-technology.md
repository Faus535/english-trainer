# Unit 07 — Technology

## Objetivo

Activar 10 palabras de tecnologia que usas en espanol todos los dias (muchas son anglicismos que ya conoces) pero que no produces con confianza en una frase completa en ingles. Probablemente las lees en menus de apps y configuraciones, pero no las dices.

## Palabras

| English | IPA | Spanish | Example |
|---------|-----|---------|---------|
| device | /dɪˈvaɪs/ | dispositivo | This app works on any **device** — phone, tablet, or laptop. |
| software | /ˈsɒft.wer/ | software, programa | We need to update the **software** to fix the security issues. |
| update | /ʌpˈdeɪt/ (v) /ˈʌp.deɪt/ (n) | actualizar, actualizacion | Have you **updated** your phone? There's a new **update** available. |
| download | /ˌdaʊnˈloʊd/ | descargar | You can **download** the app for free from the App Store. |
| storage | /ˈstɔːr.ɪdʒ/ | almacenamiento | My phone is running out of **storage**. I need to delete some photos. |
| battery | /ˈbæt.ər.i/ | bateria | My **battery** is at 5%. I need to charge my phone. |
| screen | /skriːn/ | pantalla | The **screen** on my laptop is cracked. I need to get it repaired. |
| password | /ˈpæs.wɜːrd/ | contrasena | I forgot my **password** again. I need to reset it. |
| account | /əˈkaʊnt/ | cuenta (de usuario) | You need to create an **account** before you can use the service. |
| settings | /ˈset.ɪŋz/ | configuracion, ajustes | Go to **settings** and turn off notifications. |

### Trampas y notas

- **device**: No es solo un movil. Cualquier aparato electronico es un "device": telefono, tablet, portatil, reloj inteligente, etc.
- **software** vs. **hardware**: "Software" = programas, apps, sistemas operativos (intangible). "Hardware" = componentes fisicos (pantalla, teclado, procesador). En espanol usamos las mismas palabras.
- **update**: Como verbo, acento en la segunda silaba: "up-DATE the system." Como sustantivo, acento en la primera: "There's a new UP-date."
- **download** vs. **upload**: "Download" = descargar (del servidor a tu dispositivo). "Upload" = subir (de tu dispositivo al servidor). "Download a file" vs. "Upload a photo to Instagram."
- **storage**: Incontable. NO se dice "a storage." Se dice "storage space" o simplemente "storage." "I need more storage" (no "a more storage").
- **settings**: Siempre en PLURAL. "Go to settings" (no "go to setting"). Es como "ajustes" en espanol.

## Ejercicios

### Fill in the blank

1. My phone _______ only lasts about four hours. I need a new phone.
2. Can you _______ this file and send it to me?
3. I ran out of _______ on my phone. I can't take any more photos.
4. Go to _______ and change your language to English.
5. I need to _______ my laptop. The current _______ has some bugs.
6. She cracked the _______ of her phone when she dropped it.
7. What's the _______ for the Wi-Fi?
8. I can't log in to my _______. I think it's been hacked.
9. This _______ requires the latest version of the _______ to work properly.
10. You can _______ the app from Google Play or the App Store.
