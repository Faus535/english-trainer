# Unit 06 — Travel

## Objetivo

Activar 10 palabras de viajes que ves en apps de aerolineas, emails de Booking y carteles de aeropuerto, pero que no produces con confianza. Estas palabras son la diferencia entre viajar con estres y viajar con control.

## Palabras

| English | IPA | Spanish | Example |
|---------|-----|---------|---------|
| flight | /flaɪt/ | vuelo | My **flight** to London departs at 7:30 am. |
| boarding | /ˈbɔːr.dɪŋ/ | embarque | **Boarding** starts 30 minutes before departure. Gate 14. |
| luggage | /ˈlʌɡ.ɪdʒ/ | equipaje | How many pieces of **luggage** are you checking in? |
| customs | /ˈkʌs.təmz/ | aduana | We had to wait 40 minutes to get through **customs**. |
| accommodation | /əˌkɒm.əˈdeɪ.ʃən/ | alojamiento | We haven't booked our **accommodation** for Barcelona yet. |
| booking | /ˈbʊk.ɪŋ/ | reserva | I made a **booking** at the hotel for three nights. |
| cancellation | /ˌkæn.səˈleɪ.ʃən/ | cancelacion | Is there a **cancellation** fee if I change my mind? |
| delay | /dɪˈleɪ/ | retraso | There's a two-hour **delay** on all flights due to fog. |
| destination | /ˌdes.tɪˈneɪ.ʃən/ | destino | What's your final **destination**? — Madrid. |
| itinerary | /aɪˈtɪn.ər.er.i/ | itinerario | I've planned the whole **itinerary** for our trip. Day 1: Rome. Day 2: Florence. |

### Trampas y notas

- **luggage**: Es INCONTABLE en ingles. NO se dice "a luggage" ni "luggages." Se dice "a piece of luggage" o simplemente "luggage." "I have two pieces of luggage" (no "two luggages").
- **luggage** vs. **baggage**: Son sinonimos. "Luggage" es mas comun en UK, "baggage" en US (y en aeropuertos: "baggage claim").
- **customs** (aduana) vs. **customs** (costumbres): Misma palabra, diferente contexto. "Go through customs" = pasar la aduana. "Local customs" = costumbres locales.
- **accommodation**: En UK lleva doble C y doble M. Error ortografico muy frecuente. Ademas, es normalmente incontable: "I'm looking for accommodation" (no "an accommodation").
- **booking** vs. **reservation**: Sinonimos. "Booking" es mas comun en UK, "reservation" en US. "I have a booking/reservation under the name Garcia."
- **itinerary**: Pronunciacion dificil — /aɪˈtɪn.ər.er.i/ — cinco silabas. Muchos hispanohablantes dicen "iti-ne-ra-rio" (con r espanola). Practica: "ai-TIN-uh-reh-ree."

## Ejercicios

### Fill in the blank

1. My _______ has been delayed by three hours. I'm stuck at the airport.
2. _______ begins at Gate 22 in fifteen minutes.
3. How much _______ can I take on the plane for free?
4. After landing, we went through passport control and then _______.
5. We booked cheap _______ near the beach — a small Airbnb.
6. I need to confirm my _______ at the hotel. Can you give me the reference number?
7. There's no _______ fee if you cancel more than 48 hours in advance.
8. The _______ was 90 minutes, so we missed our connection.
9. Our final _______ is Tokyo, but we have a stopover in Dubai.
10. I sent you the full _______ by email. Check which cities we're visiting.

### Translate to English

1. Mi vuelo sale a las 8 de la manana.
2. ¿Cuantas piezas de equipaje puedo llevar?
3. Hemos reservado alojamiento para cuatro noches.
4. Hay un retraso de dos horas por niebla.
5. ¿Tiene una politica de cancelacion flexible?

5. Do you have a flexible cancellation policy?
