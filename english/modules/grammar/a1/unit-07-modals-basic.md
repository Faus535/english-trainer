# Basic Modals: Can / Must / Should — Activation

## Objetivo

Usar can, must y should sin confusiones en contextos reales: habilidad, obligacion, consejo. Producir frases con ellos automaticamente.

## Activacion rapida

| Modal | Uso | Ejemplo |
|-------|-----|---------|
| **can** | habilidad, permiso | I can swim. / Can I sit here? |
| **can't** | incapacidad, prohibicion | I can't drive. / You can't park here. |
| **must** | obligacion fuerte, deduccion | You must wear a seatbelt. / She must be tired. |
| **mustn't** | prohibicion | You mustn't use your phone here. |
| **should** | consejo, recomendacion | You should see a doctor. |
| **shouldn't** | consejo negativo | You shouldn't eat so much sugar. |

Todos van seguidos de **verbo base** (sin to, sin -s, sin -ing).
~~He can to swim.~~ → He can swim.
~~She must goes.~~ → She must go.

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Traduce al ingles

1. Puedo hablar tres idiomas.
2. No deberias quedarte despierto hasta tan tarde.
3. Los empleados deben llevar uniforme.
4. Se puede fumar aqui? — No, no se puede.
5. Deberiamos reservar mesa, el restaurante siempre esta lleno.

### B. Da consejos (escribe 2 frases para cada situacion)

6. Tu amigo tiene dolor de cabeza todos los dias. (should / shouldn't)
7. Tu companero tiene una entrevista de trabajo manana. (should / shouldn't)

### C. Corrige los errores

8. She can to play the piano very well.
9. You must to finish this before Friday.
10. He should goes to the dentist.

---

### Answer Key

**A.**
1. I can speak three languages.
2. You shouldn't stay up so late.
3. Employees must wear a uniform.
4. Can you smoke here? — No, you can't.
5. We should book/reserve a table — the restaurant is always full.

**C.**
8. She can play the piano very well. (sin "to")
9. You must finish this before Friday. (sin "to")
10. He should go to the dentist. (verbo base, sin -es)

## Repaso

1. Traduce: "No puedo ir a la reunion — estoy enfermo."
2. Da un consejo a alguien que quiere aprender ingles (usa should).
3. Escribe algo que debes hacer manana (must).
4. Corrige: "You don't must touch that!"
5. Traduce: "Deberias probar la comida local cuando viajes."

### Repaso — Answer Key

1. I can't go to the meeting — I'm ill/sick.
4. You **mustn't** touch that! (mustn't, no "don't must")
5. You should try the local food when you travel.
