# Comparatives & Superlatives — Activation

## Objetivo

Comparar cosas con naturalidad: mas grande, mas importante, el mejor. Sin dudar entre -er y more.

## Activacion rapida

| Tipo | Regla | Ejemplo |
|------|-------|---------|
| 1 silaba | adj + **-er / -est** | cheap → cheaper → the cheapest |
| 2 silabas en -y | -y → **-ier / -iest** | easy → easier → the easiest |
| 2+ silabas | **more / most** + adj | expensive → more expensive → the most expensive |
| Irregulares | memorizar | good → better → the best |
| | | bad → worse → the worst |
| | | far → further → the furthest |

Comparativo: A is **___er than** B / A is **more ___ than** B
Superlativo: A is **the ___est** / A is **the most ___**

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Traduce al ingles

1. Madrid es mas grande que Valencia.
2. Este restaurante es mas barato que aquel.
3. Ella es la persona mas inteligente que conozco.
4. El examen fue mas facil de lo que esperaba.
5. Cual es la ciudad mas bonita de Espana?

### B. Compara (escribe frases completas)

6. Compara tu ciudad con Londres (3 frases: tamano, clima, coste de vida).
7. Compara dos personas que conoces (aspecto, personalidad, habitos).

### C. Corrige los errores

8. She is more tall than her brother.
9. This is the most good restaurant in town.
10. My car is more cheap than yours.

---

### Answer Key

**A.**
1. Madrid is bigger than Valencia.
2. This restaurant is cheaper than that one.
3. She is the most intelligent / smartest person I know.
4. The exam was easier than I expected.
5. What is the most beautiful city in Spain?

**C.**
8. She is **taller** than her brother.
9. This is the **best** restaurant in town.
10. My car is **cheaper** than yours.

## Repaso

1. Traduce: "El cafe aqui es peor que en Italia."
2. Compara dos peliculas que hayas visto (usa 2 comparativos).
3. Corrige: "He is the more experienced player in the team."
4. Traduce: "Mi hermano mayor es mas alto que yo, pero yo soy mas rapido."
5. Completa: "What is the ___ (far) you have ever traveled?"

### Repaso — Answer Key

1. The coffee here is worse than in Italy.
3. He is the **most** experienced player in the team.
4. My older brother is taller than me, but I am faster.
5. What is the **furthest** you have ever traveled?
