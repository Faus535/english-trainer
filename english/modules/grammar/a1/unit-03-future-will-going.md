# Future: Will vs Going to — Activation

## Objetivo

Elegir automaticamente entre will y going to segun el contexto. Dejar de usar solo "will" para todo (error tipico).

## Activacion rapida

| Uso | Estructura | Ejemplo |
|-----|-----------|---------|
| Plan decidido | **be going to** + verb | I'm going to visit my parents this weekend. |
| Prediccion con evidencia | **be going to** + verb | Look at those clouds — it's going to rain. |
| Decision espontanea | **will** + verb | The phone's ringing — I'll answer it. |
| Prediccion/opinion | **will** + verb | I think she'll love the gift. |
| Ofrecimiento | **will** + verb | Don't worry, I'll help you. |
| Promesa | **will** + verb | I'll call you tomorrow, I promise. |

Negativas: **won't** / **not going to**.

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Elige will o going to y escribe la frase completa

1. (Plan) Este verano __________ (travel) a Irlanda.
2. (Ofrecimiento) No te preocupes, yo __________ (carry) esas bolsas.
3. (Prediccion con evidencia) Mira el trafico — __________ (be) tarde.
4. (Decision espontanea) Tengo hambre. Creo que __________ (order) una pizza.
5. (Plan) Mi hermano __________ (start) un curso de ingles en septiembre.

### B. Traduce al ingles

6. Voy a cambiar de trabajo el mes que viene.
7. Creo que manana llovera.
8. No te preocupes, te ayudare con eso.
9. Vamos a cenar con mis padres el viernes.
10. Ella no va a venir a la reunion — esta enferma.

### C. Responde las preguntas (frases completas)

11. What are you going to do this weekend? (escribe un plan real)
12. What do you think the weather will be like tomorrow?

---

### Answer Key

**A.**
1. This summer I'm going to travel to Ireland.
2. Don't worry, I'll carry those bags.
3. Look at the traffic — we're going to be late.
4. I'm hungry. I think I'll order a pizza.
5. My brother is going to start an English course in September.

**B.**
6. I'm going to change jobs next month.
7. I think it will rain tomorrow.
8. Don't worry, I'll help you with that.
9. We're going to have dinner with my parents on Friday.
10. She's not going to come to the meeting — she's ill/sick.

## Repaso

1. Traduce: "Alguien llama a la puerta — yo abro." (decision espontanea)
2. Escribe un plan que tengas para manana usando "going to".
3. Haz una prediccion sobre el proximo ano usando "will".
4. Corrige: "I'm going to help you — wait a second!" (es espontaneo, no un plan)
5. Traduce: "No voy a ir a la fiesta. Estoy cansado."

### Repaso — Answer Key

1. Someone's knocking at the door — I'll open it.
4. I**'ll** help you — wait a second!
5. I'm not going to go to the party. I'm tired.
