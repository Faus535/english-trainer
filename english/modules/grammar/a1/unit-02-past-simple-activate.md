# Past Simple — Activation

## Objetivo

Contar experiencias pasadas sin dudar. Automatizar el uso de -ed en regulares y memorizar los irregulares mas comunes que generan errores.

## Activacion rapida

| Tipo | Afirmativa | Negativa | Pregunta |
|------|-----------|----------|----------|
| Regular | I worked | I didn't work | Did you work? |
| Irregular | I went | I didn't go | Did you go? |

Clave: despues de **did/didn't**, el verbo va en **base form** (sin -ed, sin irregular). *I didn't ~~went~~.* *Did you ~~went~~?*

**Irregulares traidores para hispanohablantes:**

| Base | Past | Trampa comun |
|------|------|-------------|
| buy | bought | No es "buyed" |
| think | thought | No es "thinked" |
| bring | brought | No es "bringed" |
| teach | taught | No es "teached" |
| catch | caught | No es "catched" |
| feel | felt | No es "feeled" |
| leave | left | No es "leaved" |
| sleep | slept | No es "sleeped" |

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Traduce al ingles

1. Ayer fui al supermercado y compre fruta.
2. No dormi bien anoche.
3. Ella no vino a la fiesta porque estaba enferma.
4. Que hiciste el fin de semana pasado?
5. Pensamos que era buena idea, pero no funciono.

### B. Cuenta una historia (escribe 4-5 frases)

6. Describe lo que hiciste ayer desde que te levantaste hasta que te acostaste.
7. Cuenta algo divertido que te paso la semana pasada.

### C. Corrige los errores

8. I didn't went to the gym yesterday.
9. She buyed a new phone last week.
10. Did you slept well?

---

### Answer Key

**A.**
1. Yesterday I went to the supermarket and bought fruit.
2. I didn't sleep well last night.
3. She didn't come to the party because she was ill/sick.
4. What did you do last weekend?
5. We thought it was a good idea, but it didn't work.

**C.**
8. I didn't **go** to the gym yesterday.
9. She **bought** a new phone last week.
10. Did you **sleep** well?

## Repaso

1. Escribe 3 cosas que hiciste ayer usando verbos irregulares.
2. Traduce: "No traje mi ordenador porque lo olvide en casa."
3. Corrige: "He teached me how to drive."
4. Forma la pregunta: "You / catch / the bus / this morning?"
5. Transforma a negativa: "They left early."

### Repaso — Answer Key

2. I didn't bring my laptop/computer because I forgot it at home.
3. He **taught** me how to drive.
4. Did you catch the bus this morning?
5. They didn't leave early.
