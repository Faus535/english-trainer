# Questions & Negatives — Activation

## Objetivo

Formar preguntas y negativas sin errores de orden ni de auxiliar. El orden de las preguntas en ingles es distinto al espanol — automatizarlo.

## Activacion rapida

**Orden de pregunta:** (Wh-) + **Auxiliar** + Sujeto + Verbo base + ...?

| Tiempo | Pregunta | Negativa |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Present | Do/Does + S + V? | S + don't/doesn't + V |
| Past | Did + S + V? | S + didn't + V |
| Be | Am/Is/Are + S + ...? | S + am not/isn't/aren't |

Errores tipicos del espanol:
- ~~You like coffee?~~ → **Do** you like coffee?
- ~~Where you live?~~ → Where **do** you live?
- ~~How much it costs?~~ → How much **does** it cost?

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Forma la pregunta correcta

1. (tu hermano / vivir / en Barcelona) → ___________?
2. (cuanto / costar / ese movil) → ___________?
3. (por que / ella / irse / ayer) → ___________?
4. (a que hora / empezar / la pelicula) → ___________?
5. (tus padres / hablar / ingles) → ___________?

### B. Traduce las preguntas al ingles

6. Donde trabajas?
7. Que hiciste anoche?
8. Con que frecuencia vas al gimnasio?
9. Cuantos hermanos tienes?
10. A quien llamaste?

### C. Transforma a negativa

11. She likes spicy food. → ___________
12. They went to the concert. → ___________
13. I am ready. → ___________

### D. Corrige los errores

14. Where you bought that jacket?
15. How much it costs?
16. She not likes coffee.

---

### Answer Key

**A.**
1. Does your brother live in Barcelona?
2. How much does that phone cost?
3. Why did she leave yesterday?
4. What time does the film/movie start?
5. Do your parents speak English?

**B.**
6. Where do you work?
7. What did you do last night?
8. How often do you go to the gym?
9. How many brothers and sisters / siblings do you have?
10. Who did you call?

**C.**
11. She doesn't like spicy food.
12. They didn't go to the concert.
13. I'm not ready.

**D.**
14. Where **did you buy** that jacket?
15. How much **does it cost**?
16. She **doesn't like** coffee.

## Repaso

1. Traduce: "Tu jefe sabe que estas aqui?"
2. Forma la pregunta para esta respuesta: "I get up at 7." → "What time ___?"
3. Transforma a negativa: "We understood the instructions."
4. Corrige: "What means this word?"
5. Traduce: "Cuantas horas dormiste anoche?"

### Repaso — Answer Key

1. Does your boss know (that) you are here?
2. What time do you get up?
3. We didn't understand the instructions.
4. What **does** this word **mean**?
5. How many hours did you sleep last night?
