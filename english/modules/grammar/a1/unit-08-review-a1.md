## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Traduce al ingles (mezcla de tiempos y estructuras)

1. Normalmente me levanto a las 7, pero ayer me levante a las 9.
2. No hay ninguna farmacia cerca de aqui, deberias ir al centro.
3. Vas a viajar este verano? — Si, voy a ir a Portugal.
4. Mi jefe es mas estricto que el tuyo, pero es mejor organizador.
5. No pude dormir anoche porque habia demasiado ruido.

### B. Escribe un parrafo corto (5-6 frases)

6. **Mi dia tipico:** Usa present simple. Incluye al menos una negativa y una frase con adverbio de frecuencia (always, usually, sometimes, never).

7. **Lo que hice el fin de semana pasado:** Usa past simple. Incluye al menos 3 verbos irregulares y una pregunta que alguien te hizo.

8. **Planes para las proximas vacaciones:** Usa going to y will. Incluye un plan decidido y una prediccion.

### C. Corrige el texto (hay 6 errores)

9. Yesterday I go to the supermarket and buyed some food. There wasn't no milk, so I don't bought any. My sister can to cook very well, so she is going to makes dinner tonight. I think it will being delicious.

### D. Transforma

10. Transforma estas frases afirmativas en preguntas y negativas:
    - a) She works in a hospital.
    - b) They went to Paris last year.
    - c) There are some eggs in the fridge.
    - d) He can play chess.

---

### Answer Key

**A.**
1. I usually get up at 7, but yesterday I got up at 9.
2. There isn't any pharmacy near here / There is no pharmacy near here — you should go to the city centre.
3. Are you going to travel this summer? — Yes, I'm going to go to Portugal.
4. My boss is stricter than yours, but he/she is a better organiser.
5. I couldn't sleep last night because there was too much noise.

**C — 6 errores corregidos:**
Yesterday I **went** to the supermarket and **bought** some food. There wasn't **any** milk, so I **didn't buy** any. My sister **can** cook very well, so she is going to **make** dinner tonight. I think it **will be** delicious.

**D.**
a) Does she work in a hospital? / She doesn't work in a hospital.
b) Did they go to Paris last year? / They didn't go to Paris last year.
c) Are there any eggs in the fridge? / There aren't any eggs in the fridge.
d) Can he play chess? / He can't play chess.
