# B1 Grammar Review — Integrated Production

## Objetivo

Demostrar dominio de TODAS las estructuras B1 en tareas de produccion integrada. Si puedes hacer estos ejercicios con fluidez, estas listo para B2.

## Activacion rapida

| Estructura | Ejemplo clave |
|-----------|--------------|
| Second conditional | If I were you, I would accept. |
| Passive voice | The report was written by the team. |
| Reported speech | She said she had finished the project. |
| Relative clauses | The man whose car was stolen called the police. |
| Used to / would | I used to live there. We would play every day. |
| Third conditional | If I had known, I would have helped. |
| Wish / if only | I wish I had studied harder. |
| Indirect questions | Could you tell me where the station is? |
| Narrative tenses | While I was walking, I saw him. He had already left. |

## Ejercicios de produccion

### Task 1: Formal Email (pasiva + reported speech + indirect questions)

Escribe un email formal (8-10 frases) a tu jefe. Situacion: Hubo una reunion ayer a la que no pudiste asistir. Pregunta que se decidio.

Incluye:
- Al menos 2 pasivas (decisions were made, it was agreed...)
- Al menos 2 reported speech (a colleague told you that...)
- Al menos 2 indirect questions (I was wondering if..., Could you tell me whether...)

### Task 2: Childhood Story (narrative tenses + used to/would + third conditional)

Escribe un parrafo (8-10 frases) sobre tu infancia.

Incluye:
- "used to" para habitos/estados
- "would" para acciones repetidas
- Narrative tenses (past simple + past continuous + past perfect)
- Al menos 1 third conditional (algo que habria sido diferente)

### Task 3: Regrets and Wishes (wish + third conditional + second conditional)

Escribe 8 frases sobre tu vida:
- 2 deseos sobre el presente (wish + past simple)
- 2 arrepentimientos (wish + past perfect)
- 2 hipotesis presentes (second conditional)
- 2 hipotesis pasadas (third conditional)

### Task 4: News Report (passive + relative clauses + reported speech)

Escribe una noticia corta (6-8 frases) sobre un evento inventado.

Incluye:
- Al menos 3 pasivas (was arrested, has been found, will be announced)
- Al menos 2 relative clauses (the man who..., the building where...)
- Al menos 1 reported speech (police said that...)

### Task 5: Quick-Fire Translation

Traduce al ingles:

1. Si yo fuera tu, no aceptaria esa oferta.
2. El informe fue enviado ayer por el director.
3. Ella me dijo que habia estado esperando dos horas.
4. El restaurante donde cenamos, que esta cerca de tu casa, ha cerrado.
5. Solia ir a esa playa de nino. Ibamos todos los veranos.
6. Si hubiera ahorrado mas dinero, habria podido comprar esa casa.
7. Ojala hablara japones.
8. Podrias decirme si la tienda esta abierta?
9. Mientras yo cocinaba, alguien llamo a la puerta.
10. El hombre cuyo coche fue robado ha llamado a la policia.

## Respuestas

### Task 1 (respuesta modelo)

Dear Mr. Garcia,

I hope this email finds you well. I was unable to attend yesterday's meeting and I was wondering if you could tell me what was discussed. I have been told by a colleague that several important decisions were made. She mentioned that a new deadline had been agreed for the project. I was also informed that changes to the schedule were proposed. Could you tell me whether the budget was approved? I would also like to know if any tasks were assigned to me. I apologise for my absence and would appreciate any information you could share.

Best regards,
[Name]

### Task 2 (respuesta modelo)

When I was a child, I used to live in a small town near the coast. My family used to be very close, and we would spend every weekend together. Every Saturday morning, I would ride my bike to my grandmother's house, where she would make us her famous chocolate cake. The sun was always shining in my memory of those days. I used to have a dog called Max, and we would play in the fields for hours. One summer, we had been playing near the river when Max fell in. My father jumped in and saved him. If we hadn't been so close to the river that day, it wouldn't have happened. I still miss those carefree times.

### Task 3 (respuestas modelo)

Present wishes:
- I wish I had more free time.
- I wish I lived closer to the beach.

Past regrets:
- I wish I had learnt to play the guitar when I was young.
- I wish I hadn't sold my first car.

Present hypotheses:
- If I didn't have to work, I would travel around the world.
- If I spoke better English, I could get a promotion.

Past hypotheses:
- If I had studied abroad, I would have improved my English faster.
- If I hadn't met my partner, I might have moved to another country.

### Task 4 (respuesta modelo)

PRICELESS PAINTING STOLEN FROM NATIONAL MUSEUM

A painting worth over 2 million euros was stolen from the National Museum last night. The painting, which was created by a famous 19th-century artist, was taken from the main gallery where it had been displayed for over 30 years. The theft was discovered early this morning by a security guard who noticed that the alarm had been disabled. Police said that the museum's security cameras had been tampered with. Two suspects have been arrested in connection with the crime. An official statement will be released later today.
