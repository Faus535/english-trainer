# Third Conditional — Regrets About the Past

## Objetivo

Producir frases sobre situaciones pasadas hipoteticas con fluidez. Expresar arrepentimiento con should have / could have / might have.

## Activacion rapida

**Estructura:** If + past perfect, would/could/might + have + past participle

| Situacion | Ejemplo | Significado |
|-----------|---------|------------|
| Hipotesis pasada | If I **had studied**, I **would have passed**. | No estudie, no aprobe. |
| Posibilidad perdida | If you **had told** me, I **could have helped**. | No me lo dijiste. |
| Probabilidad pasada | If it **hadn't rained**, we **might have gone** out. | Llovio, no salimos. |
| Arrepentimiento | I **should have** listened to you. | No te escuche. Me arrepiento. |
| Reproche | You **could have** called me! | No me llamaste. Estoy molesto. |

**Error hispanohablante:** Traducir "hubiera" con "would have" en la clausula IF. NO: ~~If I would have studied~~. SI: If I **had** studied.

**Contracciones naturales:** I'd have = I would have. She'd've = She would have (oral).

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Traduce al ingles

1. Si hubiera sabido la verdad, no habria aceptado el trabajo.
2. Si hubieras llegado antes, habrias visto el espectaculo.
3. Si no hubiera llovido, habriamos ido a la playa.
4. Si ella hubiera estudiado mas, podria haber aprobado.
5. Deberia haber escuchado a mi madre.

### B. Completa con tu propia idea

6. If I had been born in another country, I would have...
7. If I hadn't chosen my current career, I might have...
8. If I had known ten years ago what I know now, I would have...

### C. Expresa arrepentimiento (should have / could have)

9. No estudie para el examen. → I should have...
10. No llame a mi amigo en su cumpleanos. → I should have...
11. No acepte esa oferta de trabajo. → I could have...
12. No le dije lo que sentia. → I should have...

### D. Corrige los errores

13. If I would have known, I would have told you.
14. If she had studied, she will have passed.
15. I should have went to the doctor earlier.

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. If I had known the truth, I wouldn't have accepted the job.
2. If you had arrived earlier, you would have seen the show.
3. If it hadn't rained, we would have gone to the beach.
4. If she had studied more, she could have passed.
5. I should have listened to my mother.

**B.** (respuestas modelo)
6. If I had been born in another country, I would have learned a different language.
7. If I hadn't chosen my current career, I might have become a teacher.
8. If I had known ten years ago what I know now, I would have saved more money.

**C.**
9. I should have studied for the exam.
10. I should have called my friend on his/her birthday.
11. I could have accepted that job offer.
12. I should have told him/her how I felt.

**D.**
13. If I **had known**, I would have told you.
14. If she had studied, she **would have** passed.
15. I should have **gone** to the doctor earlier.

## Repaso

1. Si hubiera cogido el tren, habria llegado a tiempo. → ___
2. Corrige: If I would have seen her, I would have said hello.
3. You look tired. You ___ (should/go) to bed earlier last night.
4. If we ___ (not/miss) the flight, we ___ (arrive) on time.
5. Escribe un arrepentimiento tuyo real usando "I should have..."

**Repaso — Respuestas:**
1. If I had taken the train, I would have arrived on time.
2. If I **had seen** her, I would have said hello.
3. You **should have gone** to bed earlier last night.
4. If we **hadn't missed** the flight, we **would have arrived** on time.
5. (respuesta libre)
