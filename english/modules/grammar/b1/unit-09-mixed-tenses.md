# Mixed Tenses — Narrative Writing

## Objetivo

Escribir parrafos que combinan 4+ tiempos verbales de forma natural. Dominar la narracion con past simple + past continuous + past perfect.

## Activacion rapida

| Tiempo | Funcion en narracion | Ejemplo |
|--------|---------------------|---------|
| **Past simple** | Acciones principales, secuencia | I **woke up**, **got dressed** and **left**. |
| **Past continuous** | Contexto, accion en progreso | The sun **was shining** and birds **were singing**. |
| **Past perfect** | Accion anterior a otra pasada | I **had forgotten** my keys, so I **went** back. |
| **Past perfect continuous** | Duracion antes de un punto pasado | I **had been waiting** for an hour when she **arrived**. |
| **Present perfect** | Conexion pasado-presente | I**'ve lived** here since 2020. |

**Combinaciones naturales:**
- Contexto (continuous) + evento (simple): While I **was walking** home, I **saw** an accident.
- Secuencia + flashback (perfect): When I **arrived**, everyone **had already left**.
- Causa pasada + resultado: She **was tired** because she **had been working** all day.

**Error hispanohablante:** Usar past simple para todo. En espanol "caminaba" y "camine" se usan sin problemas. En ingles, la distincion importa.

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Completa la historia con el tiempo correcto

When I (1)___ (arrive) at the airport, I (2)___ (realise) that I (3)___ (forget) my passport at home. I (4)___ (run) to find a taxi, but it (5)___ (rain) heavily and there (6)___ (be) no taxis available. While I (7)___ (wait) desperately, my phone (8)___ (ring). It was my sister — she (9)___ (find) my passport and (10)___ (be) already on her way.

### B. Escribe un parrafo (5-7 frases) sobre cada situacion. Usa al menos 3 tiempos verbales diferentes.

11. Tu peor dia de trabajo. Que paso? Que estabas haciendo cuando ocurrio? Que habia pasado antes?

12. Un viaje memorable. Describe la escena (continuous), lo que paso (simple), y algo que ya habia ocurrido antes (perfect).

### C. Traduce al ingles (mezcla de tiempos)

13. Cuando llegue a casa, mi mujer habia preparado la cena y los ninos estaban viendo la television.
14. Llevaba esperando dos horas cuando por fin llego el autobus.
15. Mientras ella hablaba por telefono, alguien llamo a la puerta.

### D. Corrige los errores de tiempo verbal

16. When I arrived, everyone already left.
17. I was walking to work when I was seeing an old friend.

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. arrived (past simple — accion principal)
2. realised (past simple — accion principal)
3. had forgotten (past perfect — anterior a "arrived")
4. ran (past simple — accion principal)
5. was raining (past continuous — contexto)
6. were (past simple — estado)
7. was waiting (past continuous — accion en progreso)
8. rang (past simple — evento)
9. had found (past perfect — anterior a la llamada)
10. was (past simple — estado en ese momento)

**B.** (respuestas modelo)
11. It was a Monday morning and I was feeling exhausted because I hadn't slept well. While I was preparing a presentation, my computer crashed. I had been working on it for three hours and hadn't saved it. My boss came in and asked if it was ready. I had to explain what had happened. It was the worst day of my career.

12. We arrived in Rome early in the morning. The sun was shining and the streets were already full of tourists. We had booked a small hotel near the Colosseum. After we checked in, we walked around the city. We had never been to Italy before, so everything felt magical.

**C.**
13. When I got/arrived home, my wife had made/prepared dinner and the children were watching television.
14. I had been waiting for two hours when the bus finally arrived.
15. While she was talking on the phone, someone knocked on the door.

**D.**
16. When I arrived, everyone **had already left**.
17. I was walking to work when I **saw** an old friend.

## Repaso

1. While I ___ (study), my brother ___ (play) loud music.
2. She was tired because she ___ (work) all day.
3. By the time we ___ (arrive), the film ___ (already/start).
4. Escribe 2 frases describiendo que estabas haciendo ayer a las 8 de la tarde.
5. Escribe 1 frase con past perfect: algo que ya habia pasado cuando llegaste a algun sitio.

**Repaso — Respuestas:**
1. While I **was studying**, my brother **was playing** loud music.
2. She was tired because she **had been working** all day.
3. By the time we **arrived**, the film **had already started**.
4. (respuesta libre, p.ej.: At 8 PM yesterday, I was cooking dinner and my children were doing their homework.)
5. (respuesta libre, p.ej.: When I arrived at the party, most people had already left.)
