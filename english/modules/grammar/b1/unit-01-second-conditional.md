# Second Conditional — Hypothetical Present

## Objetivo

Producir frases hipoteticas sobre el presente/futuro con fluidez. Dejar de decir "If I would have" y usar "If I were" sin dudar.

## Activacion rapida

**Estructura:** If + past simple, would/could/might + infinitive

| Situacion | Ejemplo | Error tipico |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Hipotesis presente | If I **were** rich, I **would** travel the world. | ~~If I would be rich~~ |
| Consejo | If I **were** you, I **would** accept the offer. | ~~If I would be you~~ |
| Posibilidad remota | If she **spoke** Chinese, she **could** work in Beijing. | ~~If she would speak~~ |
| Imaginacion | If I **had** more time, I **might** learn piano. | ~~If I would have~~ |

**Clave:** "If I were" es correcto en TODOS los sujetos (I/he/she/it). "If I was" se oye en conversacion informal, pero "If I were" es la forma segura.

**Nunca:** "would" en la clausula del IF. Solo en el resultado.

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Traduce al ingles

1. Si yo fuera presidente, cambiaria las leyes de educacion.
2. Si tuvieramos mas dinero, comprariamos una casa mas grande.
3. Si ella hablara frances, conseguiria ese trabajo.
4. Si no lloviera tanto, iriamos a la playa.
5. Que harias si ganaras la loteria?

### B. Completa con tu propia idea

6. If I could live anywhere in the world, I would...
7. If I had a superpower, I would...
8. If I were my boss, I would...
9. If I spoke perfect English, I could...
10. If I didn't have to work tomorrow, I might...

### C. Corrige los errores

11. If I would have more free time, I would read more.
12. If she would be taller, she could play basketball.
13. If I was you, I will accept the job.

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. If I were president, I would change the education laws.
2. If we had more money, we would buy a bigger house.
3. If she spoke French, she would get that job.
4. If it didn't rain so much, we would go to the beach.
5. What would you do if you won the lottery?

**C.**
11. If I **had** more free time, I would read more.
12. If she **were** taller, she **could** play basketball.
13. If I **were** you, I **would** accept the job.

## Repaso

1. Si pudiera volar, iria a trabajar volando. → ___
2. If I ___ (be) you, I ___ (not/accept) that deal.
3. What ___ you ___ (do) if you ___ (have) a million euros?
4. Corrige: If I would know the answer, I would tell you.
5. If I lived in London, I ___ (can) practise English every day.

**Repaso — Respuestas:**
1. If I could fly, I would fly to work.
2. If I **were** you, I **wouldn't accept** that deal.
3. What **would** you **do** if you **had** a million euros?
4. If I **knew** the answer, I would tell you.
5. If I lived in London, I **could** practise English every day.
