# Used to / Would / Past Simple — Past Habits

## Objetivo

Distinguir "used to", "would" y past simple para hablar de habitos pasados. Dejar de usar "used to" para todo.

## Activacion rapida

| Forma | Uso | Ejemplo | Limitacion |
|-------|-----|---------|-----------|
| **used to** + inf | Habito o estado pasado que ya no es verdad | I **used to** smoke. / I **used to** live in Madrid. | Negativa: I **didn't use to** (sin d) |
| **would** + inf | Habito pasado repetido (acciones, NO estados) | We **would** play football every Sunday. | NO estados: ~~I would live in Madrid~~ |
| **past simple** | Habito o hecho pasado | I **played** football every Sunday as a child. | Menos enfasis en "ya no" |

**Diferencia clave:**
- **used to** = habitos Y estados pasados
- **would** = SOLO habitos/acciones repetidas (NO: be, live, have, like, know, believe)
- **past simple** = siempre correcto, pero no enfatiza el cambio

**Error hispanohablante:** "I am used to" (estoy acostumbrado a) NO es lo mismo que "I used to" (solia).
- I **used to** wake up early. = Solia despertarme temprano (ya no).
- I **am used to** waking up early. = Estoy acostumbrado a despertarme temprano (ahora).

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Traduce al ingles (usa "used to")

1. De nino solia jugar en la calle.
2. Mi abuelo solia contarnos historias antes de dormir.
3. No solia gustarme el cafe, pero ahora me encanta.
4. Solias vivir en Barcelona?

### B. Reescribe con "would" donde sea posible. Si NO es posible, explica por que.

5. I used to walk to school every day.
6. I used to live in a small village.
7. We used to have dinner together every Friday.
8. She used to be very shy.

### C. Escribe sobre ti (frases completas)

9. 3 cosas que solias hacer de nino/a y ya no haces.
10. 2 cosas que no solias hacer y ahora haces.
11. Describe un dia tipico de cuando tenias 10 anos (usa "would" para las acciones repetidas).

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. As a child, I used to play in the street.
2. My grandfather used to tell us stories before bed.
3. I didn't use to like coffee, but now I love it.
4. Did you use to live in Barcelona?

**B.**
5. I **would** walk to school every day. (accion repetida — SI)
6. NO se puede usar "would" — "live" es un estado.
7. We **would** have dinner together every Friday. (accion repetida — SI, "have dinner" es una accion)
8. NO se puede usar "would" — "be" es un estado.

**C.** (respuestas modelo)
9. I used to collect stickers. / I used to ride my bike everywhere. / I used to watch cartoons every morning.
10. I didn't use to drink coffee. / I didn't use to exercise regularly.
11. I would wake up at 7, have breakfast with my family, and walk to school. After school, I would play with my friends in the park. In the evening, I would watch TV and go to bed at 9.

## Repaso

1. De adolescente solia escuchar mucho rock. → ___
2. Cambia a "would" si es posible: I used to know her very well. → ___
3. Corrige: I use to play tennis when I was young.
4. Traduce: No estoy acostumbrado a levantarme tan temprano. → ___
5. Every summer, we ___ (go) to my grandmother's house and she ___ (make) us her famous cake. (usa "would")

**Repaso — Respuestas:**
1. As a teenager, I used to listen to a lot of rock.
2. NO — "know" es un estado. Se queda como "used to".
3. I **used to** play tennis when I was young.
4. I'm not used to getting up so early.
5. Every summer, we **would go** to my grandmother's house and she **would make** us her famous cake.
