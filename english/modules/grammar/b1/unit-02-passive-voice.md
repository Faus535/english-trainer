# Passive Voice — Real Contexts

## Objetivo

Usar la voz pasiva en contextos reales: noticias, procesos, escritura formal. Dejar de construirla mentalmente pieza por pieza.

## Activacion rapida

**Estructura:** S + be (conjugado) + past participle (+ by agent, si es relevante)

| Tiempo | Activa | Pasiva |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Present simple | They **make** cars in Germany. | Cars **are made** in Germany. |
| Past simple | Someone **stole** my bike. | My bike **was stolen**. |
| Present perfect | They **have arrested** the suspect. | The suspect **has been arrested**. |
| Future (will) | They **will announce** the results. | The results **will be announced**. |
| Modal | You **must complete** the form. | The form **must be completed**. |

**Cuando usar pasiva:**
- No sabemos quien hizo la accion: My wallet **was stolen**.
- No importa quien: The bridge **was built** in 1890.
- Formal/noticias: Three people **were injured** in the accident.
- Procesos: The coffee **is roasted**, then **ground**, then **brewed**.

**Error hispanohablante:** Usar "se" como si fuera pasiva. "Se habla ingles" = English **is spoken** here (NO "English speaks here").

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Transforma activa → pasiva

1. Someone broke the window last night.
2. They will publish the report next week.
3. The police have arrested two suspects.
4. People speak Spanish in 20 countries.
5. They are building a new hospital.

### B. Traduce al ingles (usa pasiva)

6. El email fue enviado ayer.
7. Se habla ingles aqui.
8. El proyecto sera terminado en marzo.
9. Han cancelado el vuelo.
10. El edificio fue disenado por un arquitecto famoso.

### C. Escribe titulares de noticias (usa pasiva)

11. Escribe un titular sobre un robo en un museo.
12. Escribe un titular sobre el descubrimiento de un nuevo planeta.

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. The window was broken last night.
2. The report will be published next week.
3. Two suspects have been arrested (by the police).
4. Spanish is spoken in 20 countries.
5. A new hospital is being built.

**B.**
6. The email was sent yesterday.
7. English is spoken here.
8. The project will be finished/completed in March.
9. The flight has been cancelled.
10. The building was designed by a famous architect.

**C.** (respuestas modelo)
11. Priceless painting stolen from national museum.
12. New planet discovered beyond our solar system.

## Repaso

1. Someone has eaten my sandwich. → (pasiva) ___
2. They must finish the report by Friday. → (pasiva) ___
3. Traduce: Se venden coches aqui. → ___
4. Corrige: The house was build in 1990.
5. Traduce: El partido fue cancelado por la lluvia. → ___

**Repaso — Respuestas:**
1. My sandwich has been eaten.
2. The report must be finished by Friday.
3. Cars are sold here.
4. The house was **built** in 1990.
5. The match was cancelled because of the rain.
