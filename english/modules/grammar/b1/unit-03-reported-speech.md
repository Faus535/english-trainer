# Reported Speech — Retelling Conversations

## Objetivo

Contar lo que otros dijeron sin pensar en reglas. Automatizar el backshift de tiempos y usar reporting verbs variados.

## Activacion rapida

| Directo | Reportado | Cambio |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| "I **am** tired." | He said he **was** tired. | am → was |
| "I **like** coffee." | She said she **liked** coffee. | like → liked |
| "I **went** home." | He said he **had gone** home. | went → had gone |
| "I **will** call you." | She said she **would** call me. | will → would |
| "I **can** help." | He said he **could** help. | can → could |
| "**Do** you like it?" | She **asked if** I liked it. | do → asked if/whether |
| "**Where** do you live?" | He **asked where** I lived. | question → statement order |

**Reporting verbs utiles:** say, tell, ask, claim, admit, deny, insist, suggest, explain, warn, promise, refuse

**Error hispanohablante:** Mantener el orden de pregunta en reported speech. NO: "He asked where did I live." SI: "He asked where I lived."

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Transforma a reported speech

1. "I'm working on a new project." — She said...
2. "We have finished the report." — They told me...
3. "I will be there at 8." — He promised...
4. "I don't understand the question." — She admitted...
5. "Can you help me with this?" — He asked me...

### B. Reporta estas preguntas

6. "Where do you work?" — She asked me...
7. "Have you ever been to Japan?" — He asked me...
8. "Are you coming to the party?" — She asked me...

### C. Traduce al ingles

9. El dijo que no podia venir a la reunion.
10. Ella me pregunto si habia terminado el informe.
11. Mi jefe insistio en que el proyecto estaria listo el viernes.
12. El nego haber robado el dinero.

### D. Retell — Cuenta esta conversacion en reported speech

> Ana: "I need to leave early today."
> Boss: "Why? Is everything OK?"
> Ana: "My son is sick. I have to pick him up from school."
> Boss: "OK, you can go. But finish the email first."

13. Escribe 4 frases reportando esta conversacion.

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. She said (that) she was working on a new project.
2. They told me (that) they had finished the report.
3. He promised (that) he would be there at 8.
4. She admitted (that) she didn't understand the question.
5. He asked me if/whether I could help him with that.

**B.**
6. She asked me where I worked.
7. He asked me if/whether I had ever been to Japan.
8. She asked me if/whether I was coming to the party.

**C.**
9. He said (that) he couldn't come to the meeting.
10. She asked me if/whether I had finished the report.
11. My boss insisted (that) the project would be ready on Friday.
12. He denied having stolen the money. / He denied that he had stolen the money.

**D.** (respuesta modelo)
13. Ana told her boss (that) she needed to leave early that day. Her boss asked her why and if everything was OK. Ana explained that her son was sick and she had to pick him up from school. Her boss said she could go but told her to finish the email first.

## Repaso

1. "I love this city." → She said ___.
2. "Where did you buy it?" → He asked me ___.
3. "I won't tell anyone." → She promised ___.
4. Corrige: He asked me where did I live.
5. "I didn't do it!" → He denied ___.

**Repaso — Respuestas:**
1. She said (that) she loved that city.
2. He asked me where I had bought it.
3. She promised (that) she wouldn't tell anyone.
4. He asked me where I **lived**.
5. He denied doing/having done it.
