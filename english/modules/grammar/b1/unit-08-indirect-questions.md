# Indirect Questions — Polite and Embedded Questions

## Objetivo

Formular preguntas indirectas automaticamente: cambiar el orden de palabras sin pensarlo. Sonar educado y natural.

## Activacion rapida

| Directa | Indirecta | Cambio clave |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Where **is** the station? | Could you tell me where the station **is**? | Orden de afirmacion (no inversion) |
| What time **does** it start? | Do you know what time it **starts**? | Eliminar does/do/did |
| **Did** she call? | Do you know **if/whether** she called? | Yes/No → if/whether |
| How much **does** it cost? | Could you tell me how much it **costs**? | Eliminar does |
| **Is** he coming? | I was wondering **if** he **is** coming. | No inversion |

**Introductores comunes:**
- Could you tell me...? / Can you tell me...?
- Do you know...? / Do you happen to know...?
- I was wondering... / I'd like to know...
- Would you mind telling me...?

**Error hispanohablante numero 1:** Mantener el orden de pregunta. NO: "Could you tell me where **is** the station?" SI: "...where the station **is**."

**Error numero 2:** Usar "if" con preguntas WH. NO: "Do you know **if** where he lives?" SI: "Do you know **where** he lives?"

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Transforma en pregunta indirecta (usa "Could you tell me...")

1. Where is the nearest pharmacy?
2. What time does the museum close?
3. How much does this cost?
4. When did the meeting start?

### B. Transforma (usa "Do you know...")

5. Is there a supermarket near here?
6. Has the train left yet?
7. Can I park here?
8. Will it rain tomorrow?

### C. Traduce al ingles (usa preguntas indirectas)

9. Podrias decirme donde esta el bano?
10. Sabes si hay un restaurante italiano por aqui?
11. Me preguntaba si podrias ayudarme.
12. Te importaria decirme como llego a la estacion?

### D. Corrige los errores

13. Could you tell me where is the post office?
14. Do you know what time does the shop open?
15. I'd like to know if is he coming to the party.

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. Could you tell me where the nearest pharmacy is?
2. Could you tell me what time the museum closes?
3. Could you tell me how much this costs?
4. Could you tell me when the meeting started?

**B.**
5. Do you know if/whether there is a supermarket near here?
6. Do you know if/whether the train has left yet?
7. Do you know if/whether I can park here?
8. Do you know if/whether it will rain tomorrow?

**C.**
9. Could you tell me where the bathroom/toilet is?
10. Do you know if there's an Italian restaurant around here?
11. I was wondering if you could help me.
12. Would you mind telling me how to get to the station?

**D.**
13. Could you tell me where the post office **is**? (mover "is" al final)
14. Do you know what time the shop **opens**? (quitar "does", conjugar "open")
15. I'd like to know if **he is** coming to the party. (orden afirmativo)

## Repaso

1. What time is it? → Could you tell me ___?
2. Does she speak English? → Do you know ___?
3. Traduce: Me preguntaba si podias prestarme tu coche. → ___
4. Corrige: Can you tell me how much does it cost?
5. Where can I buy tickets? → Would you mind telling me ___?

**Repaso — Respuestas:**
1. Could you tell me what time it is?
2. Do you know if/whether she speaks English?
3. I was wondering if you could lend me your car.
4. Can you tell me how much it **costs**?
5. Would you mind telling me where I can buy tickets?
