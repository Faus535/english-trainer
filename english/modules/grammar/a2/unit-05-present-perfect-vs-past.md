# Present Perfect vs Past Simple — Activation

## Objetivo

Dominar LA distincion mas problematica del ingles para hispanohablantes. Elegir entre present perfect y past simple sin dudar.

## Activacion rapida

| Present Perfect | Past Simple |
|----------------|-------------|
| **Sin** tiempo especifico | **Con** tiempo especifico |
| Conexion con el presente | Terminado, en el pasado |
| I**'ve been** to London. | I **went** to London **last year**. |
| She**'s lost** her keys. (aun no las tiene) | She **lost** her keys **yesterday**. |
| I**'ve lived** here **since** 2020. (aun vivo aqui) | I **lived** there **for** 3 years. (ya no) |

**Palabras clave:**

| Present Perfect | Past Simple |
|----------------|-------------|
| ever, never, just, already, yet | yesterday, last week, in 2019, ago |
| since, for (hasta ahora) | when I was young, that day |
| this week/month/year (periodo no terminado) | |

**Trampa:** "This morning" puede ser ambos:
- It's 10am: I **have had** two coffees this morning. (la manana continua)
- It's 3pm: I **had** two coffees this morning. (la manana ya paso)

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Elige Present Perfect o Past Simple y escribe la frase

1. I ___ (visit) Rome. It's a beautiful city.
2. I ___ (visit) Rome in 2019.
3. She ___ (work) here since January.
4. She ___ (work) there for two years, then she left.
5. ___ you ever ___ (try) Thai food?
6. ___ you ___ (try) the new restaurant last week?
7. We ___ (just / finish) the project.
8. We ___ (finish) the project two hours ago.

### B. Traduce al ingles (elige el tiempo correcto)

9. He perdido mis llaves — no las encuentro.
10. Perdi mis llaves ayer, pero las encontre esta manana.
11. Cuantas veces has estado en Londres?
12. Cuando fuiste a Londres por primera vez?
13. Hemos vivido aqui cinco anos. (seguimos aqui)
14. Vivimos alli cinco anos. (ya no vivimos alli)

### C. Corrige los errores

15. I have seen that film last night.
16. Did you ever been to Japan?
17. She has left the company two months ago.
18. I didn't finish yet.

---

### Answer Key

**A.**
1. I **have visited** Rome.
2. I **visited** Rome in 2019.
3. She **has worked** here since January.
4. She **worked** there for two years, then she left.
5. **Have** you ever **tried** Thai food?
6. **Did** you **try** the new restaurant last week?
7. We **have just finished** the project.
8. We **finished** the project two hours ago.

**B.**
9. I've lost my keys — I can't find them.
10. I lost my keys yesterday, but I found them this morning.
11. How many times have you been to London?
12. When did you go to London for the first time?
13. We have lived here for five years.
14. We lived there for five years.

**C.**
15. I **saw** that film last night. (last night = tiempo especifico)
16. **Have** you **ever been** to Japan?
17. She **left** the company two months ago. (ago = past simple)
18. I **haven't** finished yet. (yet = present perfect)

## Repaso

1. Traduce: "Ya has comido? — Si, comi hace una hora."
2. Elige: "I ___ (know) Maria for 10 years." / "I ___ (meet) Maria in 2014."
3. Corrige: "She has gone to Paris last summer."
4. Traduce: "Nunca he estado en Asia, pero fui a Africa en 2020."
5. Escribe 2 frases sobre ti: una con present perfect y una con past simple sobre el mismo tema.

### Repaso — Answer Key

1. Have you eaten yet? — Yes, I ate an hour ago.
2. I **have known** Maria for 10 years. / I **met** Maria in 2014.
3. She **went** to Paris last summer.
4. I have never been to Asia, but I went to Africa in 2020.
