# Prepositions: in / on / at — Activation

## Objetivo

Elegir in, on, at sin pensar para tiempo y lugar. Estos tres causan errores constantes en hispanohablantes porque el espanol usa "en" para casi todo.

## Activacion rapida

### Tiempo

| Preposicion | Uso | Ejemplo |
|------------|-----|---------|
| **at** | hora, momentos puntuales | at 3pm, at night, at the weekend, at Christmas |
| **on** | dias, fechas | on Monday, on 5th March, on my birthday |
| **in** | meses, anos, estaciones, partes del dia | in January, in 2024, in summer, in the morning |

Excepciones: **at night** (no "in the night"), **at the weekend** (BrE) / **on the weekend** (AmE)

### Lugar

| Preposicion | Uso | Ejemplo |
|------------|-----|---------|
| **at** | punto especifico, edificio (como lugar) | at the station, at home, at work, at the door |
| **on** | superficie, calle | on the table, on the wall, on Oxford Street |
| **in** | espacio cerrado, ciudad, pais | in the room, in Madrid, in Spain, in the car |

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Completa con in, on o at

1. I was born ___ 1995.
2. The meeting is ___ Monday ___ 10am.
3. She lives ___ Paris, ___ a small flat ___ the 3rd floor.
4. We usually go on holiday ___ August.
5. He's ___ home right now.
6. There's a painting ___ the wall.
7. I'll see you ___ Friday ___ the morning.
8. She was sitting ___ the bus ___ the back.
9. My birthday is ___ 15th June.
10. We arrived ___ the airport ___ midnight.
11. He works ___ a bank ___ the city centre.
12. The keys are ___ the table.

---

### Answer Key

**A.**
1. **in** 1995
2. **on** Monday **at** 10am
3. **in** Paris, **in** a small flat **on** the 3rd floor
4. **in** August
5. **at** home
6. **on** the wall
7. **on** Friday **in** the morning
8. **on** the bus **at** the back
9. **on** 15th June
10. **at** the airport **at** midnight
11. **in/at** a bank **in** the city centre
12. **on** the table

## Repaso

1. Completa: "I'll meet you ___ the restaurant ___ 8 o'clock ___ Saturday evening."
2. Completa: "She was born ___ March ___ a small town ___ the south of France."
3. Corrige: "I'm going to the dentist in Monday at the afternoon."
4. Completa: "We live ___ a house ___ the corner of the street."
5. Traduce: "Hay alguien en la puerta." / "El libro esta en la mesa." / "Estoy en el trabajo."

### Repaso — Answer Key

1. **at** the restaurant **at** 8 o'clock **on** Saturday evening
2. **in** March **in** a small town **in** the south of France
3. I'm going to the dentist **on** Monday **in** the afternoon.
4. We live **in** a house **on/at** the corner of the street.
5. There's someone **at** the door. / The book is **on** the table. / I'm **at** work.
