# Advanced Modals: Might / Could / Would — Activation

## Objetivo

Usar might, could y would con naturalidad para expresar posibilidad, habilidad pasada, peticiones educadas e hipotesis suaves.

## Activacion rapida

| Modal | Uso | Ejemplo |
|-------|-----|---------|
| **might** | posibilidad (menos seguro que "may") | I might go to the party. (quizas) |
| **might not** | posibilidad negativa | She might not come. (puede que no) |
| **could** | habilidad pasada | I could swim when I was 5. |
| **could** | posibilidad | It could rain later. |
| **could** | sugerencia | You could try the other restaurant. |
| **could / could you** | peticion educada | Could you open the window, please? |
| **would** | situacion hipotetica | I would love to travel more. |
| **would you** | peticion muy educada | Would you mind helping me? |
| **would like** | querer (educado) | I would like a coffee, please. |

**Diferencia clave:**
- **Can** you help? = informal, directo
- **Could** you help? = mas educado
- **Would** you mind helping? = muy educado

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Traduce al ingles

1. Podria llover esta tarde — lleva un paraguas.
2. Cuando era nino, podia correr muy rapido.
3. Podrias repetir eso, por favor?
4. Me gustaria reservar una mesa para dos.
5. Puede que no venga a la reunion — esta muy ocupado.

### B. Responde usando might, could o would

6. What would you do if you had more free time? (escribe 2 ideas)
7. Someone asks you for restaurant recommendations. Give 2 suggestions using "could".
8. A friend asks about your weekend plans. You're not sure yet — respond using "might".

### C. Transforma: de informal a educado

9. Give me the salt. → ___________
10. Help me with this. → ___________
11. I want a glass of water. → ___________

### D. Corrige los errores

12. I might to go to the cinema tonight.
13. Could you to pass me the bread?
14. When I was young, I can run very fast.

---

### Answer Key

**A.**
1. It might/could rain this afternoon — take an umbrella.
2. When I was a child, I could run very fast.
3. Could you repeat that, please?
4. I would like to book/reserve a table for two.
5. He might not come to the meeting — he's very busy.

**C.**
9. Could you pass me the salt, please? / Would you mind passing me the salt?
10. Could you help me with this, please? / Would you mind helping me with this?
11. I would like a glass of water, please.

**D.**
12. I might go to the cinema tonight. (sin "to")
13. Could you pass me the bread? (sin "to")
14. When I was young, I **could** run very fast.

## Repaso

1. Traduce: "Quizas cambie de trabajo el ano que viene." (might)
2. Escribe una peticion educada para pedir a alguien que baje la musica.
3. Traduce: "De nino podia tocar el piano, pero ya no puedo."
4. Corrige: "Would you like come to the party?"
5. Escribe lo que harias si tuvieras un mes libre (2 frases con would).

### Repaso — Answer Key

1. I might change jobs next year.
2. Could you turn the music down, please? / Would you mind turning the music down?
3. When I was a child, I could play the piano, but I can't anymore.
4. Would you like **to** come to the party?
