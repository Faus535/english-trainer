# Connectors — Activation

## Objetivo

Conectar ideas con variedad y precision. Dejar de usar solo "but" y "because" y empezar a usar however, although, despite, therefore, etc.

## Activacion rapida

| Conector | Tipo | Estructura | Ejemplo |
|----------|------|-----------|---------|
| **however** | contraste | Frase. **However,** frase. | It was expensive. However, it was worth it. |
| **although / even though** | contraste | **Although** + clausula, clausula. | Although it was raining, we went out. |
| **despite / in spite of** | contraste | **Despite** + sustantivo/-ing | Despite the rain, we went out. / Despite being tired, she kept working. |
| **therefore** | consecuencia | Frase. **Therefore,** frase. | He was late. Therefore, he missed the meeting. |
| **as a result** | consecuencia | Frase. **As a result,** frase. | She didn't study. As a result, she failed. |
| **whereas / while** | comparacion | Clausula **whereas** clausula. | I like coffee, whereas my sister prefers tea. |
| **moreover / furthermore** | adicion | Frase. **Moreover,** frase. | The hotel was cheap. Moreover, it was clean. |

**Error comun:** ~~Although it was raining, but we went out.~~ No mezcles although con but. Elige uno.

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Une las dos frases usando el conector indicado

1. The film was long. It was interesting. (however)
2. It was cold. We went for a walk. (despite)
3. She speaks three languages. She can't find a job. (even though)
4. He didn't set an alarm. He was late. (as a result)
5. I prefer working from home. My colleague prefers the office. (whereas)

### B. Traduce al ingles

6. Aunque estaba cansado, termine el informe.
7. A pesar del trafico, llegamos a tiempo.
8. La comida era buena. Sin embargo, el servicio era terrible.

### C. Corrige los errores

9. Although it was expensive, but I bought it.
10. Despite of the noise, I managed to sleep.
11. He was sick. Moreover, he went to work. (wrong connector)

---

### Answer Key

**A.**
1. The film was long. However, it was interesting.
2. Despite the cold, we went for a walk. / Despite it being cold, we went for a walk.
3. Even though she speaks three languages, she can't find a job.
4. He didn't set an alarm. As a result, he was late.
5. I prefer working from home, whereas my colleague prefers the office.

**B.**
6. Although I was tired, I finished the report.
7. Despite the traffic, we arrived on time.
8. The food was good. However, the service was terrible.

**C.**
9. **Although** it was expensive, I bought it. (eliminar "but")
10. **Despite** the noise, I managed to sleep. (sin "of" despues de despite)
11. He was sick. **However,** he went to work. (moreover = ademas, no tiene sentido de contraste aqui)

## Repaso

1. Une: "The hotel was noisy. I slept well." (in spite of)
2. Traduce: "No me gustan los lunes. Sin embargo, este lunes fue bastante bueno."
3. Escribe una frase con "therefore" sobre una decision que tomaste.
4. Corrige: "Despite of being late, the teacher didn't say anything."
5. Une: "He has a lot of experience. He is also very creative." (moreover)

### Repaso — Answer Key

1. In spite of the noise, I slept well.
2. I don't like Mondays. However, this Monday was quite good.
4. **Despite** being late, the teacher didn't say anything.
5. He has a lot of experience. Moreover, he is very creative.
