# A2 Review — Full Production

## Objetivo

Integrar todas las estructuras A2 en tareas de produccion realistas: un email, una experiencia pasada, dar consejos. Demostrar que puedes usar present perfect, past continuous, first conditional, preposiciones, conectores y modales avanzados de forma combinada.

## Activacion rapida

No hay teoria nueva. Todo lo necesario esta en las unidades 1-7.

## Ejercicios de produccion

### Task 1: Write a short email (8-10 frases)

Situacion: Escribes a un amigo/a britanico/a. Cuentale:
- Que has hecho ultimamente (present perfect)
- Algo que te paso la semana pasada (past simple + past continuous)
- Tus planes para las proximas semanas (going to / might)
- Proponle quedar (could / would you like)

Usa al menos: 1 present perfect, 1 past continuous, 1 conector (however/although), 1 modal (could/would/might).

**Ejemplo de inicio:**
> Hi Sarah,
>
> How have you been? I haven't written for a while because I've been really busy...

---

### Task 2: Describe a past experience (6-8 frases)

Cuenta una experiencia memorable (un viaje, un evento, un dia especial). Incluye:
- Contexto: donde estabas, que estaba pasando (past continuous)
- Que ocurrio (past simple)
- Como te sentiste / que aprendiste (past simple + present perfect si sigue siendo relevante)
- Un conector (although, despite, however)

**Ejemplo de inicio:**
> Last summer, I went to Lisbon for the first time. The sun was shining and the streets were full of tourists...

---

### Task 3: Give advice (6-8 frases)

Situacion: Un amigo te dice que quiere mudarse a otro pais pero tiene miedo. Dale consejos usando:
- should / shouldn't (al menos 2)
- might / could para posibilidades
- first conditional (if + present, will)
- Un conector (however, moreover)

**Ejemplo de inicio:**
> I think you should go for it! If you don't try, you'll always wonder "what if?"...

---

### Self-check: Cuando termines cada task, revisa que:

- [ ] No has puesto "will" en la clausula del "if"
- [ ] Has usado "since" con punto en el tiempo y "for" con duracion
- [ ] Las preposiciones in/on/at son correctas
- [ ] No has mezclado "although" con "but"
- [ ] Los modales van seguidos de verbo base (sin to, sin -s)
- [ ] Present perfect para experiencias sin tiempo especifico, past simple con tiempo especifico

---

### Bonus: Quick-fire corrections (mezcla de todos los errores A2)

Corrige las siguientes frases:

1. I have visited Paris last year.
2. Although it was raining, but we went out.
3. If I will have time, I'll call you.
4. She lives here since 2018.
5. Could you to help me with this?
6. I was study when the phone rang.
7. Despite of the cold weather, we had a great time.
8. He might to change his mind.
9. We met at Monday at the morning.
10. I didn't finished yet.

---

### Answer Key — Bonus corrections

1. I **visited** Paris last year. (last year = past simple)
2. **Although** it was raining, we went out. (quitar "but")
3. If I **have** time, I'll call you. (presente en la clausula if)
4. She **has lived** here **since** 2018. (present perfect con since)
5. Could you help me with this? (sin "to")
6. I was **studying** when the phone rang. (was + -ing)
7. **Despite** the cold weather, we had a great time. (sin "of")
8. He might change his mind. (sin "to")
9. We met **on** Monday **in** the morning.
10. I **haven't** finished yet. (present perfect con yet)

## Repaso

1. Traduce: "Aunque no he estado en Japon, me gustaria ir algun dia."
2. Traduce: "Estaba esperando el autobus cuando empezo a llover. Sin embargo, no me importo porque habia llevado un paraguas."
3. Traduce: "Si encuentras un buen hotel, podrias reservarlo? Me gustaria ir en agosto."
4. Escribe 3 frases sobre lo que has aprendido en ingles ultimamente (present perfect).
5. Escribe un consejo para alguien que quiere mejorar su ingles (should, could, if).

### Repaso — Answer Key

1. Although I haven't been to Japan, I would like to go one day.
2. I was waiting for the bus when it started to rain. However, I didn't mind because I had brought an umbrella.
3. If you find a good hotel, could you book it? I would like to go in August.
