# Past Continuous — Activation

## Objetivo

Describir acciones en progreso en el pasado y combinarlas con el past simple para contar interrupciones. Usar "was/were + -ing" sin pensar.

## Activacion rapida

**Estructura:** S + **was/were** + verb**-ing**

| Uso | Ejemplo |
|-----|---------|
| Accion en progreso en un momento pasado | At 8pm, I **was watching** TV. |
| Accion interrumpida (past continuous + past simple) | I **was having** a shower **when** the phone **rang**. |
| Dos acciones simultaneas | **While** she **was cooking**, he **was reading**. |
| Contexto/escenario | The sun **was shining** and the birds **were singing**. |

**when** + past simple (la interrupcion)
**while** + past continuous (la accion larga)

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Traduce al ingles

1. Que estabas haciendo a las 10 de la noche?
2. Estaba durmiendo cuando sono la alarma.
3. Mientras yo cocinaba, mi hermano estaba viendo la tele.
4. Estaba lloviendo cuando salimos del cine.
5. No estaba prestando atencion cuando el profesor hizo la pregunta.

### B. Completa la historia (usa past continuous + past simple)

6. I ___ (walk) to work when I ___ (see) an old friend.
7. She ___ (talk) on the phone when she ___ (trip) and ___ (fall).
8. We ___ (have) dinner when the lights ___ (go) out.

### C. Escribe 3 frases sobre lo que estabas haciendo ayer a estas horas

9. A las 9 de la manana...
10. A las 3 de la tarde...
11. A las 10 de la noche...

### D. Corrige los errores

12. I was watch TV when you called.
13. While I was cooking, the phone was rang.
14. She were sleeping at midnight.

---

### Answer Key

**A.**
1. What were you doing at 10pm?
2. I was sleeping when the alarm went off.
3. While I was cooking, my brother was watching TV.
4. It was raining when we left/came out of the cinema.
5. I wasn't paying attention when the teacher asked the question.

**B.**
6. I **was walking** to work when I **saw** an old friend.
7. She **was talking** on the phone when she **tripped** and **fell**.
8. We **were having** dinner when the lights **went** out.

**D.**
12. I was **watching** TV when you called.
13. While I was cooking, the phone **rang**. (la interrupcion va en past simple)
14. She **was** sleeping at midnight.

## Repaso

1. Traduce: "Llovia y hacia frio. La gente corria para coger el autobus."
2. Escribe una frase con "while" y dos acciones simultaneas.
3. Corrige: "When I was arriving home, I realized I forgot my keys."
4. Completa: "The kids ___ (play) in the garden when it ___ (start) to rain."
5. Traduce: "Que estabas haciendo cuando te llame?"

### Repaso — Answer Key

1. It was raining and it was cold. People were running to catch the bus.
3. When I **arrived** home, I realized I **had forgotten** my keys. (llegar = accion puntual, no continua)
4. The kids **were playing** in the garden when it **started** to rain.
5. What were you doing when I called you?
