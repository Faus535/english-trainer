# Register and Nuance — Same Idea, Three Ways

## Objetivo

Expresar la misma idea en registro informal, neutro y formal. Adaptar tu ingles al contexto sin sonar forzado ni demasiado casual.

## Activacion rapida

| Informal | Neutro | Formal |
|----------|--------|--------|
| I think... | It seems to me that... | It could be argued that... |
| You need to... | It would be advisable to... | It is imperative that... |
| It's getting worse | The situation is deteriorating | There has been a marked decline |
| Lots of people | Many people | A significant number of individuals |
| I want to say that... | I'd like to point out that... | I wish to draw attention to the fact that... |
| We can't do this | This is not feasible | This approach is deemed unfeasible |
| I don't agree | I'm not entirely convinced | I would respectfully take issue with that view |
| The thing is... | The point is that... | The fundamental issue at hand is... |
| Basically... | In essence... | Fundamentally speaking... |
| So... | Therefore... | Consequently / It follows that... |

**Cuando usar cada registro:**
- **Informal:** amigos, mensajes, conversacion casual
- **Neutro:** emails de trabajo, reuniones, presentaciones generales
- **Formal:** ensayos academicos, cartas oficiales, documentos legales, discursos

**Error hispanohablante:** Usar siempre registro formal por inseguridad ("I would like to inform you..." en un WhatsApp). O usar siempre informal por comodidad ("gonna" en una presentacion).

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Transforma la misma idea a los 3 registros

1. "This plan is bad."
   - Informal: ___
   - Neutro: ___
   - Formal: ___

2. "We should help them."
   - Informal: ___
   - Neutro: ___
   - Formal: ___

3. "I don't know why they did that."
   - Informal: ___
   - Neutro: ___
   - Formal: ___

### B. Identifica el registro y reescribe en el opuesto

4. "I wish to express my sincere gratitude for your assistance." → Informal: ___
5. "Gonna grab some food, wanna come?" → Formal: ___
6. "The data suggests a correlation between the two variables." → Informal: ___

### C. Escribe la misma frase en neutro y formal

7. "Many people have lost their jobs." → Neutro: ___ / Formal: ___
8. "We need to act fast." → Neutro: ___ / Formal: ___
9. "I think this is the best option." → Neutro: ___ / Formal: ___

### D. Elige el registro apropiado para el contexto

10. Email a tu jefe pidiendo vacaciones: escribe 3-4 frases en registro neutro.
11. Discurso en una conferencia sobre cambio climatico: escribe 3-4 frases en registro formal.
12. Mensaje a un companero de trabajo con el que tienes confianza: escribe 3-4 frases en registro informal.

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. Informal: This plan sucks. / This plan's rubbish.
   Neutro: This plan has several problems. / I don't think this plan will work.
   Formal: This proposal presents a number of significant shortcomings.

2. Informal: We should totally help them out.
   Neutro: I think we should offer them our support.
   Formal: It would be advisable to extend our assistance to them. / I would recommend that we provide them with the necessary support.

3. Informal: No idea why they did that. / Beats me.
   Neutro: I'm not sure why they did that.
   Formal: The rationale behind their decision remains unclear. / I am unable to ascertain the motivation for their actions.

**B.**
4. Thanks so much for your help! / Cheers for helping out!
5. I would like to propose that we adjourn briefly for refreshments. Would you care to join me?
6. Looks like those two things are connected. / Seems like one thing affects the other.

**C.**
7. Neutro: A large number of people have lost their jobs.
   Formal: A substantial proportion of the workforce has been made redundant. / There has been a significant increase in unemployment.

8. Neutro: We need to take action quickly.
   Formal: It is imperative that immediate measures be taken. / Swift action is required.

9. Neutro: I believe this is the best option available.
   Formal: It is my considered view that this represents the most viable course of action.

**D.** (respuestas modelo)
10. Hi [Boss], I hope you're well. I was wondering if I could take a few days off next month. I'd like to take the 15th to 19th if possible. Please let me know if that works for you.

11. Ladies and gentlemen, the evidence is unequivocal: our planet is facing an unprecedented environmental crisis. It is imperative that we take decisive action to reduce carbon emissions. Failure to do so would have catastrophic consequences for future generations. I urge all stakeholders to commit to meaningful change.

12. Hey! Quick heads up — I'm gonna be late to the meeting. Got stuck in traffic. Can you let everyone know? Thanks!

## Repaso

1. "I messed up." → Neutro: ___ / Formal: ___
2. "It is incumbent upon us to address this matter forthwith." → Informal: ___
3. Email a un cliente: "Hey, the stuff you ordered is on its way." → Reescribe en neutro: ___
4. Escribe "I disagree" en 3 registros.
5. Identifica el registro: "The findings are broadly consistent with previous research."

**Repaso — Respuestas:**
1. Neutro: I made a mistake. / Formal: An error was made. / I acknowledge the oversight.
2. We really need to deal with this right now. / We gotta sort this out ASAP.
3. Dear [Client], I am writing to let you know that your order has been dispatched and is on its way.
4. Informal: No way, I totally disagree. / Neutro: I don't really agree with that. / Formal: I would respectfully disagree with that assessment.
5. Formal/academic register.
