# Hedging and Softening — Making Claims Less Absolute

## Objetivo

Suavizar afirmaciones para sonar mas academico, diplomatico y matizado. Dejar de hacer claims absolutas que suenan dogmaticas en ingles.

## Activacion rapida

| Estrategia | Absoluto (suena agresivo) | Hedged (suena profesional) |
|-----------|--------------------------|---------------------------|
| Modal verbs | This **is** the cause. | This **might/could be** the cause. |
| Adverbs | He **always** arrives late. | He **tends to** arrive late. |
| Qualifiers | This is **wrong**. | This is **somewhat/arguably** problematic. |
| Impersonal | **I think** this is bad. | **It would appear that** this is suboptimal. |
| Extent phrases | This **is** true. | This is true **to some extent / to a certain degree**. |
| Verbs | This **proves** X. | This **suggests/indicates** X. |
| Frequency | People **always** do this. | People **tend to / are inclined to** do this. |

**Herramientas de hedging:**

| Tipo | Expresiones |
|------|-----------|
| Modals | might, could, may, would |
| Adverbs | perhaps, possibly, arguably, somewhat, relatively, to some extent |
| Verbs | seem, appear, suggest, indicate, tend to |
| Phrases | it would appear that, it could be argued that, there is reason to believe |
| Qualifiers | in most cases, under certain conditions, to a certain degree |

**Error hispanohablante:** En espanol se puede ser directo sin sonar maleducado. En ingles academico/profesional, afirmaciones sin hedge suenan arrogantes o poco rigurosas.

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Suaviza estas afirmaciones absolutas

1. Social media causes depression in teenagers.
2. Remote work is always better than office work.
3. This policy has failed completely.
4. Everyone agrees that the economy is getting worse.
5. This research proves that exercise prevents cancer.

### B. Traduce al ingles con hedging apropiado

6. Parece que el problema esta relacionado con la falta de financiacion.
7. Se podria argumentar que esta politica no es la mejor opcion.
8. Los datos sugieren que existe una correlacion entre las dos variables.
9. En cierta medida, la teoria es correcta.

### C. Elige la version mas apropiada para un ensayo academico

10. a) "This is wrong." / b) "This appears to be somewhat problematic."
11. a) "Everyone knows that climate change is real." / b) "There is broad consensus that climate change is occurring."
12. a) "The study shows X." / b) "The findings suggest X."

### D. Escribe un parrafo (4-5 frases) sobre este tema, usando al menos 5 hedging strategies diferentes:

"Does technology improve education?"

13. (parrafo completo)

### E. Soften these workplace statements

14. "You're wrong about this." → ___
15. "This report is terrible." → ___
16. "You need to change your approach." → ___

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. Social media **may/could** contribute to depression in teenagers. / There **appears to be** a link between social media use and depression in teenagers.
2. Remote work **tends to be** more productive for **some** workers **in certain contexts**.
3. This policy **appears to have** had **limited** success. / This policy has been **largely** unsuccessful.
4. **There is a growing perception** that the economy is declining. / **Many commentators suggest** that the economy is getting worse.
5. This research **suggests** that exercise **may play a role in** reducing cancer risk.

**B.**
6. It would appear that the problem is related to a lack of funding. / The problem seems to be linked to insufficient funding.
7. It could be argued that this policy is not the most effective option.
8. The data suggests that there may be a correlation between the two variables.
9. To a certain extent / To some degree, the theory is correct.

**C.**
10. b) — Mas apropiado para escritura academica.
11. b) — Evita la generalizacion absoluta.
12. b) — "Suggest" es mas cauteloso y riguroso que "show".

**D.** (respuesta modelo)
13. Technology **could potentially** improve education, **although** the extent of its impact **remains a matter of debate**. **It would appear that** digital tools **tend to** enhance engagement for **some** students, **particularly** those who learn visually. **However**, there is **reason to believe** that excessive screen time **may** have negative effects on concentration. **To some extent**, the effectiveness of technology in education **seems to depend** on how it is implemented by teachers. **Overall**, while the evidence **suggests** benefits, further research is **arguably** needed before drawing definitive conclusions.

**E.**
14. I'm not entirely sure I agree with that. / I see your point, but I might look at it differently.
15. There are perhaps a few areas in this report that could be improved. / This report might benefit from some revisions.
16. It might be worth considering a slightly different approach. / You might want to think about adjusting your approach.
