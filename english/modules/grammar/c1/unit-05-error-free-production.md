# Error-Free Production — Fossilized Spanish-Speaker Errors

## Objetivo

Eliminar los errores que persisten incluso en nivel C1. Estos son errores "fosilizados" que los hispanohablantes siguen cometiendo porque su lengua materna interfiere.

## Activacion rapida

| Categoria | Error tipico | Correcto | Regla |
|-----------|-------------|----------|-------|
| Articulos | ~~The life is beautiful.~~ | **Life** is beautiful. | No article con conceptos generales |
| Articulos | ~~I like the music.~~ (en general) | I like **music**. | No article con sustantivos incontables generales |
| Articulos | ~~She is doctor.~~ | She is **a** doctor. | Article obligatorio con profesiones |
| Make/Do | ~~make an exam~~ | **do/take** an exam | Make = crear; Do = realizar |
| Make/Do | ~~do a mistake~~ | **make** a mistake | Make = producir algo (incluso errores) |
| Say/Tell | ~~He said me that...~~ | He **told** me that... | Tell + persona; Say + palabras |
| Preposiciones | ~~depend of~~ | depend **on** | |
| Preposiciones | ~~consist in~~ | consist **of** | |
| Preposiciones | ~~married with~~ | married **to** | |
| Falsos amigos | ~~actually~~ (= actualmente) | **currently** / actually = en realidad | |
| Falsos amigos | ~~sensible~~ (= sensible) | **sensitive** / sensible = sensato | |
| Falsos amigos | ~~eventually~~ (= eventualmente) | **possibly** / eventually = finalmente | |
| Genero | ~~He is very nice (hablando de "she")~~ | **She** is very nice | Sin genero gramatical en objetos |
| Word order | ~~I like very much the coffee~~ | I like coffee **very much** | Adverbio al final (no entre verbo y objeto) |
| Redundancia | ~~return back~~ | **return** | "Re-" ya significa "back" |

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Articulos: anade, quita o cambia el articulo

1. The happiness is important in the life.
2. She wants to be doctor.
3. I went to the school yesterday. (como alumno)
4. The Spanish people are very friendly.
5. I had the dinner at 8.

### B. Make vs Do: elige la correcta

6. She ___ (make/do) a lot of exercise.
7. Can you ___ (make/do) me a favour?
8. He ___ (make/do) a terrible mistake.
9. I need to ___ (make/do) a decision.
10. They ___ (make/do) business together.

### C. Say vs Tell: completa correctamente

11. She ___ me that she was leaving.
12. He ___ goodbye and left.
13. Can you ___ me the time?
14. They ___ that the weather would improve.
15. I ___ her a lie. / I ___ a lie to her.

### D. Preposiciones: corrige

16. It depends of the weather.
17. She is married with a lawyer.
18. This consists in three parts.
19. I arrived to the airport late.
20. She listened the music.

### E. Falsos amigos: elige la palabra correcta

21. He is ___ (actually/currently) working in London. (ahora mismo)
22. She is very ___ (sensible/sensitive) — she cries easily.
23. ___ (Eventually/Possibly), they will finish the project by December. (al final)
24. I am very ___ (excited/nervous) about the concert. (con ilusion)
25. The lecture was very ___ (boring/annoying). (aburrida)

### F. Orden de palabras y otros errores: corrige

26. I like very much this restaurant.
27. She explained me the problem.
28. He suggested me to go home.
29. I am agree with you.
30. She returned back the book to the library.

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. **Happiness** is important in **life**. (sin articulo — conceptos generales)
2. She wants to be **a** doctor. (articulo con profesiones)
3. I went to **school** yesterday. (sin "the" — como alumno. Con "the" = al edificio fisico)
4. **Spanish** people are very friendly. (sin "the" — generalizacion)
5. I had **dinner** at 8. (sin articulo con comidas generales)

**B.**
6. does (do exercise)
7. do (do a favour)
8. made (make a mistake)
9. make (make a decision)
10. do (do business)

**C.**
11. told
12. said
13. tell
14. said
15. told / told

**D.**
16. It depends **on** the weather.
17. She is married **to** a lawyer.
18. This consists **of** three parts.
19. I arrived **at** the airport late.
20. She listened **to** the music.

**E.**
21. currently (actually = en realidad)
22. sensitive (sensible = sensato)
23. Eventually (possibly = posiblemente)
24. excited (nervous = nervioso, no entusiasmado)
25. boring (annoying = molesta, irritante)

**F.**
26. I like this restaurant very much. / I really like this restaurant.
27. She explained the problem **to** me. (explain no lleva objeto indirecto directo)
28. He suggested **(that) I (should) go** home. (suggest no lleva "me to")
29. I agree with you. (sin "am")
30. She returned the book to the library. (sin "back")

## Repaso

1. Corrige: The water is essential for the life.
2. Corrige: She said me that she was tired.
3. Corrige: Actually, I'm working in a new project. (quiere decir "ahora mismo")
4. Corrige: He is very sensible and cries easily.
5. Make o do? He ___ his homework every day.

**Repaso — Respuestas:**
1. Water is essential for life.
2. She told me that she was tired.
3. Currently, I'm working on a new project.
4. He is very **sensitive** and cries easily.
5. does
