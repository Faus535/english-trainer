# Unit 07 — Emphasis Structures

## Objetivo

Dominar las estructuras de enfasis en ingles. En espanol enfatizamos con entonacion y poco mas. En ingles hay estructuras gramaticales especificas para dar enfasis, y usarlas correctamente marca la diferencia entre un B2 y un C1. Aqui aprenderas do/does enfatico, oraciones hendidas (cleft sentences), pseudo-clefts, fronting y estructuras existenciales enfaticas.

## Activacion rapida

| Estructura | Ejemplo | Efecto |
|---|---|---|
| Do/does + verb | I **do** understand your concern. | Refuerza la verdad de la afirmacion |
| It-cleft | **It was John** who broke the window. | Focaliza un elemento especifico |
| Wh-cleft (pseudo-cleft) | **What I need** is a break. | Pone el foco al final |
| Reverse wh-cleft | A break **is what I need**. | Pone el foco al principio |
| Fronting con inversion | **Never before** had I seen such a thing. | Dramatiza, registro formal/literario |
| Existencial enfatico | **There is no way** that this will work. | Enfasis absoluto en la negacion |

## 1. Do / Does / Did para enfasis

En espanol decimos "Yo SI entiendo". En ingles usamos do/does/did + verbo base para el mismo efecto.

| Situacion | Sin enfasis | Con enfasis |
|---|---|---|
| Presente (I/you/we/they) | I understand. | I **do** understand. |
| Presente (he/she/it) | She works hard. | She **does** work hard. |
| Pasado | He called you. | He **did** call you. |
| Imperativo | Come in. | **Do** come in. (muy britanico, muy educado) |

**Contextos donde es natural:**
- Contradecir: "You don't care!" — "I **do** care!"
- Confirmar algo inesperado: "She **does** speak Japanese, actually."
- Corregir un malentendido: "He **did** send the email — check your spam folder."
- Invitaciones corteses: "**Do** sit down. **Do** help yourself."

**Error hispanohablante:** Decir "I really understand" o "I truly care" cuando la estructura natural seria "I do understand" / "I do care". El do enfatico es mucho mas idiomatico.

## 2. Oraciones hendidas (Cleft sentences)

### It-clefts: It + be + foco + who/that/where/when

Sirven para destacar UN elemento de la frase. Compara:

| Normal | It-cleft | Que se enfatiza |
|---|---|---|
| John broke the window. | **It was John** who broke the window. | La persona (John) |
| John broke the window. | **It was the window** that John broke. | El objeto (the window) |
| I met her in Paris. | **It was in Paris** that I met her. | El lugar |
| She resigned on Monday. | **It was on Monday** that she resigned. | El momento |
| His attitude annoys me. | **It's his attitude** that annoys me. | La causa |

### What-clefts (pseudo-clefts): What + sujeto + verbo + be + foco

El foco va al final, dandole maximo peso:

| Normal | What-cleft |
|---|---|
| I need a break. | **What I need** is a break. |
| She said something surprising. | **What she said** was surprising. |
| We want peace. | **What we want** is peace. |
| The economy worries me. | **What worries me** is the economy. |
| They did something terrible. | **What they did** was terrible. |

### Variaciones avanzadas

| Estructura | Ejemplo | Uso |
|---|---|---|
| What happened was... | **What happened was** that the server crashed. | Narrar un evento enfatizando la accion |
| The reason why... is that... | **The reason why** I left **is that** I felt unwelcome. | Enfatizar la causa |
| The thing is... | **The thing is**, nobody actually read the report. | Enfasis conversacional |
| All + sujeto + verbo | **All I want** is peace. / **All you need** is love. | Enfasis de exclusividad (= lo unico que) |
| The person who... | **The person who** should apologize is him. | Focalizar identidad |

## 3. Fronting con inversion

En registro formal o literario, podemos mover un elemento negativo o restrictivo al principio de la frase, lo que obliga a invertir el sujeto y el auxiliar:

| Expresion fronteada | Ejemplo completo | Significado |
|---|---|---|
| Never before | **Never before** had I seen such chaos. | Jamas habia visto semejante caos. |
| Only then | **Only then** did I realize the truth. | Solo entonces me di cuenta. |
| Not only... but also | **Not only** did he lie, **but** he also stole. | No solo mintio, sino que tambien robo. |
| Rarely | **Rarely** have I encountered such talent. | Rara vez he encontrado tal talento. |
| Seldom | **Seldom** does he admit his mistakes. | Pocas veces admite sus errores. |
| Under no circumstances | **Under no circumstances** should you open that door. | Bajo ninguna circunstancia... |
| At no point | **At no point** did she mention the cost. | En ningun momento menciono el coste. |
| Little did I know | **Little did I know** that it was the last time. | Poco sabia yo que... |
| No sooner... than | **No sooner** had I arrived **than** it started raining. | Apenas habia llegado cuando... |
| Hardly... when | **Hardly** had we sat down **when** the alarm went off. | Apenas nos habiamos sentado cuando... |

**Regla clave:** Despues de la expresion negativa/restrictiva, la estructura es: auxiliar + sujeto + verbo.
- Never → **had** I seen (no: ~~I had seen~~)
- Only then → **did** I realize (no: ~~I realized~~)

## 4. Existencial enfatico con "there"

| Estructura | Ejemplo | Efecto |
|---|---|---|
| There's no way... | **There's no way** that's going to work. | Imposibilidad total |
| There's no doubt... | **There's no doubt** she's the best candidate. | Certeza absoluta |
| There's no denying... | **There's no denying** the evidence. | Imposible negar |
| There's nothing... | **There's nothing** we can do about it. | Resignacion enfatica |
| There's no question... | **There's no question** that he's guilty. | Sin ninguna duda |

## Dialogos

### Dialogo 1 — Reunion de equipo

> **Manager:** I've heard the team didn't support the new initiative.
> **You:** Actually, we **did** support it — what we disagreed with was the timeline. What we need is more time to prepare.
> **Manager:** But the deadline is fixed.
> **You:** I understand. The thing is, not only is the timeline unrealistic, but it also puts quality at risk. What I'm suggesting is a phased approach. It was the marketing team who originally proposed a two-stage rollout.
> **Manager:** Rarely have I seen such a well-argued case. Let me think about it.

### Dialogo 2 — Narracion dramatica

> **Friend:** So what happened at the airport?
> **You:** Well, no sooner had I arrived than I realized I'd left my passport at home. Little did I know that it was going to be the worst day of my life. What happened next was that the airline refused to rebook. Under no circumstances would they make an exception. Never before had I felt so helpless.

### B. Transforma a it-cleft

1. "Maria solved the problem." → It was ___
2. "I discovered the error on Friday." → It was ___
3. "His arrogance caused the conflict." → It was ___
4. "They met in Barcelona." → It was ___
5. "The CEO made the final decision." → It was ___

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. It was Maria who solved the problem.
2. It was on Friday that I discovered the error.
3. It was his arrogance that caused the conflict.
4. It was in Barcelona that they met.
5. It was the CEO who made the final decision.

</details>

### C. Transforma a what-cleft o pseudo-cleft

1. "I need more information." → What ___
2. "She said something shocking." → What ___
3. "The lack of communication caused the problem." → What ___
4. "I want an honest answer." → All ___
5. "He refused to cooperate. That surprised me." → What ___

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. What I need is more information.
2. What she said was shocking.
3. What caused the problem was the lack of communication.
4. All I want is an honest answer.
5. What surprised me was that he refused to cooperate.

</details>

### D. Completa con fronting e inversion

1. Never before ___ (I / see) such a beautiful sunset.
2. Not only ___ (he / lie), but he also cheated.
3. Only then ___ (she / understand) the gravity of the situation.
4. Hardly ___ (we / start) the meeting when the fire alarm went off.
5. Under no circumstances ___ (you / should / reveal) this information.

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. Never before **had I seen** such a beautiful sunset.
2. Not only **did he lie**, but he also cheated.
3. Only then **did she understand** the gravity of the situation.
4. Hardly **had we started** the meeting when the fire alarm went off.
5. Under no circumstances **should you reveal** this information.

</details>

### E. Traduce al ingles usando estructuras de enfasis

1. Lo que necesitamos es mas tiempo.
2. Fue ella quien empezo la discusion.
3. Bajo ninguna circunstancia deberias aceptar esa oferta.
4. Yo SI lei el informe — fue el quien no lo leyo.
5. Apenas habia terminado de hablar cuando sono el telefono.
6. La razon por la que renuncio es que no se sentia valorada.

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. What we need is more time.
2. It was her/she who started the argument.
3. Under no circumstances should you accept that offer.
4. I did read the report — it was him who didn't read it.
5. Hardly had I finished speaking when the phone rang. / No sooner had I finished speaking than the phone rang.
6. The reason why she resigned is that she didn't feel valued.

</details>

### F. Produccion libre

Escribe un parrafo de 5-6 frases sobre un evento memorable usando al menos:
- 1 do/does/did enfatico
- 1 it-cleft
- 1 what-cleft
- 1 fronting con inversion

Ejemplo:
> "I **do** remember that day vividly. It was in 2019 that I visited Tokyo for the first time. What struck me was the incredible mix of tradition and modernity. Never before had I seen a city that felt so alive. All I wanted was to stay longer. Not only did the experience change my perspective, but it also inspired me to learn Japanese."

## Repaso

| Concepto | Ejemplo clave | Cuando usarlo |
|---|---|---|
| Do enfatico | I **do** understand. | Contradecir, confirmar, ser cortesmente enfatico |
| It-cleft | **It was Maria** who solved it. | Focalizar un elemento especifico |
| What-cleft | **What I need** is time. | Poner el foco al final de la frase |
| Fronting + inversion | **Never before** had I seen... | Registro formal, narrativa dramatica |
| All + subject + verb | **All I want** is peace. | Enfasis de exclusividad |
| Existencial enfatico | **There's no way** this works. | Negacion o certeza total |
