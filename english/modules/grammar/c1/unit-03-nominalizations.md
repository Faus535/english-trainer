# Nominalizations — Academic Density

## Objetivo

Convertir verbos y adjetivos en sustantivos para escritura densa y formal. Saber cuando nominalizar aporta claridad y cuando oscurece el mensaje.

## Activacion rapida

| Verbo/Adjetivo | Nominalizacion | Ejemplo academico |
|---------------|----------------|------------------|
| implement | **implementation** | The **implementation** of the policy was delayed. |
| develop | **development** | Economic **development** requires investment. |
| improve | **improvement** | There has been a significant **improvement**. |
| fail | **failure** | The **failure** to act had consequences. |
| decide | **decision** | The **decision** was made unanimously. |
| important | **importance** | The **importance** of education cannot be overstated. |
| able | **ability** | Her **ability** to communicate is remarkable. |
| reduce | **reduction** | A **reduction** in costs was achieved. |
| apply | **application** | The **application** of this theory is limited. |
| aware | **awareness** | Public **awareness** has increased. |

**Cuando nominalizar:**
- Escritura academica: "The **implementation** of the new policy..." (vs "They implemented...")
- Titulares: "**Reduction** in crime rates"
- Para empaquetar informacion densa

**Cuando NO nominalizar:**
- Conversacion: "We need to **implement** the plan." (NO: "The implementation of the plan is necessary.")
- Cuando oscurece al agente: "**Mistakes were made**" esconde quien los hizo

**Error hispanohablante:** El espanol nominaliza mucho naturalmente ("la implementacion", "la realizacion"), asi que este recurso es relativamente familiar. El error es ABUSAR en ingles, creando prosa densa e ilegible.

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Nominaliza — Reescribe en estilo academico

1. They decided to close the factory. → The ___ to close the factory...
2. The government failed to respond quickly. → The government's ___ to respond quickly...
3. Air quality has improved significantly. → There has been a significant ___ in air quality.
4. We need to analyse the data carefully. → Careful ___ of the data is required.
5. They are developing a new vaccine. → The ___ of a new vaccine is underway.

### B. De-nominaliza — Reescribe en estilo mas claro y directo

6. The establishment of a new committee was announced.
7. The implementation of the regulation led to the reduction of emissions.
8. The investigation into the disappearance of the funds is ongoing.

### C. Traduce al ingles usando nominalizaciones

9. La destruccion del medio ambiente es un problema global.
10. El descubrimiento de esta tecnica ha revolucionado la medicina.
11. La falta de comunicacion entre los departamentos ha causado retrasos.
12. El reconocimiento de sus logros fue bien recibido.

### D. Elige: nominalizado o verbal? Justifica.

13. Contexto: email de trabajo a un colega.
    a) "The completion of the report is expected by Friday."
    b) "We expect to complete the report by Friday."

14. Contexto: ensayo academico.
    a) "They failed to consider the long-term effects."
    b) "Their failure to consider the long-term effects was significant."

15. Contexto: presentacion ejecutiva.
    a) "We reduced costs by 15%."
    b) "A 15% reduction in costs was achieved."

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. The **decision** to close the factory...
2. The government's **failure** to respond quickly...
3. There has been a significant **improvement** in air quality.
4. Careful **analysis** of the data is required.
5. The **development** of a new vaccine is underway.

**B.**
6. They announced that a new committee would be set up / established.
7. When the regulation was implemented, emissions were reduced. / Implementing the regulation reduced emissions.
8. They are still investigating how the funds disappeared. / Investigators are still looking into where the funds went.

**C.**
9. The destruction of the environment is a global problem.
10. The discovery of this technique has revolutionised medicine.
11. The lack of communication between departments has caused delays.
12. The recognition of his/her achievements was well received.

**D.**
13. b) — Email de trabajo: directo es mejor. La nominalizacion suena excesivamente formal para un colega.
14. b) — Ensayo academico: la nominalizacion empaqueta la idea y permite analizarla como sujeto.
15. Ambas funcionan. a) para un tono directo y de impacto; b) para un informe escrito mas formal.

## Repaso

1. They are refusing to negotiate. → (nominaliza) ___
2. The acquisition of the company was completed last month. → (de-nominaliza) ___
3. Traduce (formal): El aumento del desempleo preocupa a los expertos. → ___
4. Cuando es MEJOR no nominalizar?
5. Nominaliza: People are becoming more aware of climate change. → ___

**Repaso — Respuestas:**
1. Their refusal to negotiate...
2. They acquired/bought the company last month.
3. The rise/increase in unemployment is a source of concern for/among experts.
4. En comunicacion directa (emails, conversacion) y cuando nominalizar esconde al agente de la accion.
5. There is a growing awareness of climate change. / Public awareness of climate change is increasing.
