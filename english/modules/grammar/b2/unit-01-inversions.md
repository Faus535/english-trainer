# Inversions — Emphasis Through Word Order

## Objetivo

Producir inversiones formales/literarias sin titubear. Transformar frases normales a estilo enfatico con inversion sujeto-verbo.

## Activacion rapida

| Expresion negativa/restrictiva | Ejemplo con inversion |
|-------------------------------|----------------------|
| No sooner...than | **No sooner had** I arrived **than** the phone rang. |
| Hardly/Scarcely...when | **Hardly had** I sat down **when** the meeting started. |
| Not only...but also | **Not only did** she win, **but** she also broke the record. |
| Little | **Little did** I know it would change my life. |
| Never | **Never have** I seen such a beautiful sunset. |
| Rarely/Seldom | **Rarely do** I eat out during the week. |
| Only after/when/if | **Only after** the exam **did** I realise my mistake. |
| Under no circumstances | **Under no circumstances should** you open that door. |
| Not until | **Not until** I got home **did** I notice the problem. |
| At no point | **At no point did** he apologise. |

**Mecanismo:** Expresion negativa al inicio → auxiliar antes del sujeto (como pregunta).
- Normal: I have **never** seen... → **Never have** I seen...
- Normal: She **not only** won... → **Not only did** she win...

**Error hispanohablante:** Olvidar invertir. "Never I have seen" es incorrecto. Se necesita la inversion: "Never **have I** seen."

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Transforma a oracion invertida

1. I had no sooner left the house than it started raining.
2. She not only passed the exam, but she also got the highest mark.
3. I have never experienced anything like this.
4. I little knew that she was planning a surprise.
5. He rarely complains about anything.

### B. Traduce al ingles con inversion

6. Apenas habia terminado de comer cuando sono el telefono.
7. En ningun momento me dijo la verdad.
8. Rara vez tenemos la oportunidad de conocer a alguien asi.
9. Solo despues de perder el trabajo me di cuenta de lo importante que era.

### C. Completa las frases

10. Never before ___ I ___ (see) such a disorganised event.
11. Not only ___ he ___ (lie) to us, but he also stole money.
12. Hardly ___ we ___ (arrive) when the storm hit.
13. Little ___ she ___ (know) that her life was about to change.

### D. Escribe frases originales usando estas estructuras

14. Escribe una frase con "No sooner...than" sobre tu dia.
15. Escribe una frase con "Not only...but also" sobre alguien que admiras.

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. No sooner had I left the house than it started raining.
2. Not only did she pass the exam, but she also got the highest mark.
3. Never have I experienced anything like this.
4. Little did I know that she was planning a surprise.
5. Rarely does he complain about anything.

**B.**
6. Hardly/Scarcely had I finished eating when the phone rang.
7. At no point did he tell me the truth.
8. Rarely/Seldom do we have the opportunity to meet someone like that.
9. Only after losing my job did I realise how important it was.

**C.**
10. Never before **had** I **seen** such a disorganised event.
11. Not only **did** he **lie** to us, but he also stole money.
12. Hardly **had** we **arrived** when the storm hit.
13. Little **did** she **know** that her life was about to change.

**D.** (respuestas modelo)
14. No sooner had I sat down at my desk than my boss called me to a meeting.
15. Not only is she an excellent scientist, but she is also a talented musician.

## Repaso

1. I have seldom met such a generous person. → (inversion) ___
2. Completa: Not until I read the email ___ I ___ (understand) the problem.
3. Traduce con inversion: Nunca habia visto una pelicula tan aburrida. → ___
4. Corrige: Never I have been so embarrassed.
5. Under no circumstances ___ you ___ (must/touch) that button.

**Repaso — Respuestas:**
1. Seldom have I met such a generous person.
2. Not until I read the email **did** I **understand** the problem.
3. Never had I seen / Never have I seen such a boring film.
4. Never **have I been** so embarrassed.
5. Under no circumstances **must** you **touch** that button.
