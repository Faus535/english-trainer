# Future Perfect & Future Perfect Continuous — Timelines

## Objetivo

Proyectar logros y duraciones hacia el futuro con precision. Distinguir future perfect (resultado) de future perfect continuous (duracion en progreso).

## Activacion rapida

| Tiempo | Estructura | Uso | Ejemplo |
|--------|-----------|-----|---------|
| Future perfect | will + **have** + pp | Accion completada ANTES de un punto futuro | By June, I **will have finished** the course. |
| Future perfect continuous | will + **have been** + -ing | Duracion de una accion EN PROGRESO hasta un punto futuro | By June, I **will have been studying** for 6 months. |

| Contraste | Ejemplo |
|-----------|---------|
| Resultado completado | By 2030, they **will have built** 500 houses. (= 500 terminadas) |
| Duracion en progreso | By 2030, they **will have been building** houses for 10 years. (= llevan/llevaran 10 anos construyendo) |

**Marcadores temporales:** by + fecha, by the time + clausula, by this time next year, in 5 years' time

**Error hispanohablante:** Usar present perfect donde se necesita future perfect. NO: "By next year I have finished." SI: "By next year I **will have** finished."

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Completa con future perfect o future perfect continuous

1. By the end of this year, I ___ (work) at this company for 10 years.
2. By Friday, they ___ (finish) the project.
3. By the time you arrive, I ___ (cook) dinner. (estara listo)
4. By the time you arrive, I ___ (cook) for two hours. (estare en proceso)
5. In 2030, we ___ (live) in this house for 20 years.

### B. Traduce al ingles

6. Para cuando llegues, habra terminado la pelicula.
7. En junio llevare 3 anos estudiando ingles.
8. Para manana a esta hora, ya habre entregado el informe.
9. Para el ano que viene, habremos ahorrado suficiente dinero para el viaje.

### C. Escribe sobre tu vida futura

10. By this time next year, I will have... (3 cosas que habras logrado)
11. By the time I retire, I will have been... (2 duraciones)
12. By the end of this month, I will have... (2 logros)

### D. Elige la forma correcta

13. By 6 PM, I ___ (write) the report. — a) will have written b) will have been writing
    (Contexto: tu jefe necesita el informe terminado a las 6.)
14. By 6 PM, I ___ (write) for 4 hours straight. — a) will have written b) will have been writing

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. will have been working (duracion continua)
2. will have finished (resultado completado)
3. will have cooked (resultado: la cena estara lista)
4. will have been cooking (duracion: el proceso)
5. will have been living (duracion continua)

**B.**
6. By the time you arrive, the film will have finished.
7. In June, I will have been studying English for 3 years.
8. By this time tomorrow, I will have already handed in / submitted the report.
9. By next year, we will have saved enough money for the trip.

**C.** (respuestas modelo)
10. By this time next year, I will have passed my English exam, I will have visited 3 new countries, and I will have finished my online course.
11. By the time I retire, I will have been working for over 40 years, and I will have been living in this city for 30 years.
12. By the end of this month, I will have completed this grammar unit and I will have read two books.

**D.**
13. a) **will have written** (el informe tiene que estar terminado — resultado)
14. b) **will have been writing** (enfasis en la duracion del proceso)

## Repaso

1. By December, I ___ (learn) English for 2 years.
2. By the time she's 30, she ___ (visit) 20 countries.
3. Traduce: Para las 10, habre terminado todo. → ___
4. Corrige: By next month I have been working here for a year.
5. Para manana a esta hora, llevare 24 horas sin dormir. → ___

**Repaso — Respuestas:**
1. will have been learning
2. will have visited
3. By 10 o'clock, I will have finished everything.
4. By next month, I **will have been** working here for a year.
5. By this time tomorrow, I will have been awake for 24 hours. / I will have gone 24 hours without sleep.
