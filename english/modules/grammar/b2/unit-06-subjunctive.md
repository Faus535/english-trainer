# Subjunctive — Formal Recommendations and Demands

## Objetivo

Producir el subjuntivo ingles en contextos formales y reconocerlo en textos. Saber cuando suena natural y cuando es excesivo.

## Activacion rapida

| Contexto | Estructura | Ejemplo |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Sugerencia formal | suggest/recommend/propose + that + S + **base form** | I suggest that he **be** promoted. |
| Demanda formal | demand/insist/require + that + S + **base form** | They insisted that she **attend** the meeting. |
| Es esencial | It is essential/vital/important + that + S + **base form** | It is essential that everyone **be** present. |
| If I were | Subjuntivo en condicional | If I **were** you, I would leave. |

**Verbos que activan subjuntivo:** suggest, recommend, propose, demand, insist, require, request, ask (formal), urge

**Adjetivos que activan subjuntivo:** essential, vital, important, necessary, crucial, imperative

**Clave:** El subjuntivo usa la forma BASE del verbo para TODOS los sujetos. Sin "s", sin "to".
- It is essential that he **be** there. (NO: ~~is~~, NO: ~~to be~~)
- I suggest that she **take** a break. (NO: ~~takes~~, NO: ~~to take~~)
- Negativo: I recommend that he **not go**. (NO: ~~doesn't go~~)

**Alternativa informal (mas comun en British English):** should + infinitive
- I suggest that he **should be** promoted.
- It's important that everyone **should be** present.

**Error hispanohablante:** El subjuntivo espanol es mucho mas comun. En ingles, solo se usa en contextos formales. En conversacion informal, usa "should" o reestructura la frase.

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Completa con la forma correcta (subjuntivo)

1. The doctor recommended that she ___ (take) a week off work.
2. It is essential that every employee ___ (attend) the training session.
3. The committee proposed that the deadline ___ (be) extended.
4. I insist that he ___ (apologise) for his behaviour.
5. It is vital that this information ___ (not/be) shared with anyone.

### B. Traduce al ingles (usa subjuntivo formal)

6. Sugiero que el sea incluido en el equipo.
7. Es crucial que ella este presente en la reunion.
8. El director exigio que todos los empleados llegaran a tiempo.
9. Recomiendo que no aceptes esa oferta.

### C. Reescribe usando subjuntivo

10. She should be given a promotion. → I recommend that...
11. Everyone must be present. → It is essential that...
12. He shouldn't resign. → I suggest that...

### D. Formal o informal? Elige la forma apropiada para el contexto

13. En un email a un amigo: "I think he should ___" / "I suggest that he ___" (go to the doctor)
14. En un informe oficial: "The board recommends that the CEO ___" / "The board thinks the CEO should ___" (resign)
15. En una reunion de trabajo: "It is imperative that we ___" / "We really need to ___" (act quickly)

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. take
2. attend
3. be extended
4. apologise
5. not be shared

**B.**
6. I suggest that he be included in the team.
7. It is crucial that she be present at the meeting.
8. The director demanded that all employees arrive on time.
9. I recommend that you not accept that offer. / I recommend that you do not accept that offer.

**C.**
10. I recommend that she be given a promotion.
11. It is essential that everyone be present.
12. I suggest that he not resign.

**D.**
13. Informal: "I think he should go to the doctor."
14. Formal: "The board recommends that the CEO resign."
15. Ambas son posibles, pero "It is imperative that we act quickly" es mas formal; "We really need to act quickly" es para contexto menos formal.

## Repaso

1. The law requires that every citizen ___ (carry) identification.
2. Traduce (formal): Es necesario que el informe este terminado manana. → ___
3. Reescribe con subjuntivo: They should not be allowed to enter. → I insist that...
4. Elige: En un email casual, dirias "I suggest that he be promoted" o "I think he should get promoted"?
5. Completa: It is vital that this mistake ___ (not/happen) again.

**Repaso — Respuestas:**
1. carry
2. It is necessary that the report be finished/completed by tomorrow.
3. I insist that they not be allowed to enter.
4. "I think he should get promoted." (el subjuntivo es demasiado formal para un email casual)
5. not happen
