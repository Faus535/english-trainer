# Mixed Conditionals — Crossing Time Frames

## Objetivo

Mezclar tiempos en condicionales: condicion pasada con resultado presente, y condicion presente con resultado pasado. Producirlas sin pensar en "reglas".

## Activacion rapida

| Tipo | Estructura | Ejemplo | Logica |
|------|-----------|---------|--------|
| Pasado → Presente | If + past perfect, would + infinitive | If I **had studied** medicine, I **would be** a doctor now. | Algo que no hice ENTONCES afecta mi situacion AHORA |
| Presente → Pasado | If + past simple, would + have + pp | If I **weren't** so shy, I **would have spoken** to her. | Mi personalidad AHORA explica lo que no hice ENTONCES |

**Comparacion con condicionales puras:**
| Condicional | If-clause | Result |
|------------|-----------|--------|
| 2nd (pura) | past simple → | would + inf (ambas presente/futuro) |
| 3rd (pura) | past perfect → | would have + pp (ambas pasado) |
| Mixed 1 | past perfect → | would + inf (pasado → presente) |
| Mixed 2 | past simple → | would have + pp (presente → pasado) |

**Error hispanohablante:** Usar "would" en ambas clausulas. NO: "If I would have studied, I would be..." SI: "If I **had** studied, I **would be**..."

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Completa con la forma correcta (mixed conditional)

1. If I ___ (not/miss) the train yesterday, I ___ (be) at work now.
2. If she ___ (be) more confident, she ___ (apply) for that job last month.
3. If we ___ (book) the hotel earlier, we ___ (not/be) in this terrible room now.
4. If he ___ (not/be) so stubborn, he ___ (accept) our help last week.

### B. Traduce al ingles

5. Si hubiera aceptado ese trabajo, ahora estaria viviendo en Londres.
6. Si no fuera tan timido, le habria pedido su numero de telefono.
7. Si hubieramos comprado esa casa en 2010, ahora seriamos ricos.
8. Si ella no fuera tan desorganizada, no habria perdido el vuelo.

### C. Escribe mixed conditionals sobre tu vida

9. Algo que hiciste/no hiciste en el pasado y afecta tu presente: If I had/hadn't..., I would/wouldn't...
10. Una caracteristica tuya que afecto algo en el pasado: If I were/weren't..., I would/wouldn't have...
11. Una decision pasada que cambiaria tu situacion actual: If I had..., I would...
12. Un rasgo de personalidad que cambio un evento pasado: If I were..., I would have...

### D. Identifica: 2nd, 3rd, o mixed?

13. If I had more money, I would travel more.
14. If I had saved more money, I would be travelling now.
15. If I had saved more money, I would have bought a house.
16. If I were braver, I would have jumped.

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. If I **hadn't missed** the train yesterday, I **would be** at work now. (mixed: past → present)
2. If she **were** more confident, she **would have applied** for that job last month. (mixed: present → past)
3. If we **had booked** the hotel earlier, we **wouldn't be** in this terrible room now. (mixed: past → present)
4. If he **weren't** so stubborn, he **would have accepted** our help last week. (mixed: present → past)

**B.**
5. If I had accepted that job, I would be living in London now.
6. If I weren't so shy, I would have asked for her phone number.
7. If we had bought that house in 2010, we would be rich now.
8. If she weren't so disorganised, she wouldn't have missed the flight.

**C.** (respuestas modelo)
9. If I had studied English abroad, I would speak it much better now.
10. If I weren't so cautious, I would have started my own business years ago.
11. If I had moved to another city after university, I would have a completely different life now.
12. If I were more spontaneous, I would have said yes to that opportunity.

**D.**
13. 2nd conditional (present → present)
14. Mixed (past → present)
15. 3rd conditional (past → past)
16. Mixed (present → past)
