# Discourse Markers — Academic and Formal Connectors

## Objetivo

Usar conectores avanzados para escritura formal, ensayos y presentaciones. Dejar de depender de "also", "but", "so" y "because".

## Activacion rapida

| Funcion | Basico (B1) | Avanzado (B2) | Ejemplo |
|---------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Adicion | also, and | **furthermore, moreover, in addition, what is more** | The plan is expensive. **Furthermore**, it is unrealistic. |
| Contraste | but, however | **nevertheless, nonetheless, on the other hand, whereas, yet** | The results were poor. **Nevertheless**, we continued. |
| Causa | because, so | **therefore, consequently, hence, thus, as a result** | Costs rose sharply; **consequently**, profits fell. |
| Condicion | if | **provided that, on condition that, as long as** | You can go, **provided that** you finish your work. |
| Concesion | although | **despite, in spite of, notwithstanding, albeit** | **Despite** the rain, we went out. / He accepted, **albeit** reluctantly. |
| Proposito | to, for | **in order to, so as to, with a view to, with the aim of** | **With a view to** improving sales, we hired a new team. |
| Referencia | about | **with regard to, regarding, in terms of, in light of** | **With regard to** your request, I have good news. |
| Resumen | in the end | **to sum up, all in all, on the whole, in conclusion** | **All in all**, the event was a success. |
| Resultado | so | **thereby, thus, accordingly** | He studied hard, **thereby** passing all his exams. |

**Puntuacion clave:**
- Inicio de frase: **Furthermore,** + S + V
- Mitad de frase (entre punto y coma): ...**; nevertheless,** S + V
- Parentetico: The plan, **moreover,** is unrealistic.

**Error hispanohablante:** Usar "on the other hand" para contrastar con algo negativo. "On the other hand" presenta una alternativa, no una oposicion directa. Para oposicion: "however", "nevertheless".

## Ejercicios de produccion

### A. Sustituye el conector basico por uno avanzado

1. The company lost money last year. **But** it still hired new employees.
2. She was tired. **So** she went home early.
3. **Although** he was sick, he came to work.
4. The project failed. **Also**, we lost two clients.
5. I'm writing **about** the marketing budget.

### B. Completa con el discourse marker mas apropiado

6. The experiment was a success. ___, further research is needed. (concesion)
7. We need to cut costs; ___, we will reduce the travel budget. (resultado)
8. ___ your email dated March 10, I would like to confirm the meeting. (referencia)
9. ___ the evidence suggests otherwise, the theory remains popular. (concesion)
10. She trained every day, ___ winning the competition. (resultado)

### C. Traduce al ingles usando conectores formales

11. Ademas, el proyecto tiene problemas de presupuesto.
12. No obstante, seguimos adelante con el plan.
13. Con respecto a su solicitud, le informo que ha sido aceptada.
14. A pesar de las dificultades, el equipo logro sus objetivos.
15. Por consiguiente, recomendamos cancelar el contrato.

### D. Escribe un parrafo formal (5-6 frases) sobre este tema: "Should companies allow remote work?"

Incluye al menos 4 discourse markers diferentes de la tabla.

---

## Respuestas

**A.**
1. The company lost money last year. **Nevertheless/Nonetheless**, it still hired new employees.
2. She was tired. **Consequently/Therefore**, she went home early.
3. **Despite being** sick / **Notwithstanding** his illness / **In spite of being** sick, he came to work.
4. The project failed. **Furthermore/Moreover**, we lost two clients.
5. I'm writing **with regard to / regarding** the marketing budget.

**B.**
6. **Nevertheless / Nonetheless** (concesion — a pesar de eso)
7. **accordingly / therefore** (resultado)
8. **With regard to / Regarding / In reference to** (referencia)
9. **Although / Even though / Notwithstanding the fact that** (concesion)
10. **thereby** (resultado directo)

**C.**
11. Furthermore / Moreover, the project has budget problems/issues.
12. Nevertheless / Nonetheless, we went/are going ahead with the plan.
13. With regard to your application, I am pleased to inform you that it has been accepted.
14. Despite / In spite of the difficulties, the team achieved its objectives.
15. Consequently / Therefore, we recommend cancelling the contract.

**D.** (respuesta modelo)
Remote work has become increasingly common in recent years. **On the one hand**, it offers flexibility and can improve work-life balance. **Furthermore**, companies can reduce office costs and attract talent from different regions. **On the other hand**, some employees may feel isolated, and collaboration can become more challenging. **Nevertheless**, with the right tools and management practices, these issues can be addressed. **All in all**, allowing remote work, at least partially, seems to be a sensible approach for most companies.

## Repaso

1. El resultado fue malo. Sin embargo, aprendimos mucho. → (usa "nonetheless") ___
2. Completa: ___ the current economic climate, we have decided to freeze salaries. (referencia)
3. The plan is risky; ___, it could be very profitable. (concesion/contraste)
4. He didn't study; ___, he failed the exam. (resultado logico)
5. Escribe 1 frase usando "thereby": ___

**Repaso — Respuestas:**
1. The result was poor. Nonetheless, we learnt a great deal.
2. In light of / Given
3. nevertheless / however / on the other hand
4. consequently / therefore / as a result
5. (respuesta libre, p.ej.: She automated the process, thereby saving the company thousands of euros.)
