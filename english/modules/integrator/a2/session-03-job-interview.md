# Session 03 — Entrevista de Trabajo Basica

## Objetivo

Integrar tres habilidades en el contexto de una entrevista de trabajo:
- **Listening**: Comprender preguntas tipicas del entrevistador
- **Pronunciacion**: Acento en "experience", "responsible"; entonacion formal
- **Phrases**: Frases clave para responder preguntas de entrevista

Al final podras entender y responder a preguntas basicas de entrevista con confianza.

## Calentamiento

Piensa antes de empezar:
- ¿Has tenido alguna entrevista en ingles? ¿Que fue lo mas dificil?
- ¿Sabes decir tus tres fortalezas principales en ingles?
- ¿Cual crees que es la pregunta de entrevista mas comun en ingles?

## Listening

### Transcripcion del audio

Escucha con TTS sin leer. Luego lee y escucha de nuevo.

> **Interviewer:** Good morning. Please, take a seat. So, tell me a little about yourself.
> **Candidate:** Good morning. Thank you for having me. My name is Laura Gomez. I'm from Valencia, and I've been living in London for two years. I studied marketing at university, and I have three years of experience in digital marketing. Currently, I'm working as a marketing assistant at a small agency.
> **Interviewer:** That sounds great. Why are you interested in this position?
> **Candidate:** Well, I'm looking for a new challenge. Your company is a leader in the industry, and I'd love to work on bigger projects. I'm also really interested in the international side of your business.
> **Interviewer:** What would you say are your main strengths?
> **Candidate:** I'd say I'm very organised and detail-oriented. I'm also good at working in a team. My colleagues often say I'm reliable and easy to work with.
> **Interviewer:** And what about weaknesses?
> **Candidate:** Sometimes I spend too much time on details. I'm learning to prioritise better and focus on the big picture.
> **Interviewer:** Good. Where do you see yourself in five years?
> **Candidate:** I'd like to be managing my own team and leading international campaigns.
> **Interviewer:** Very well. Do you have any questions for us?
> **Candidate:** Yes, could you tell me more about the team I'd be working with?

### Preguntas de comprension

1. Where is Laura from and where does she live now?
2. What is her educational background?
3. How much experience does she have?
4. What is her main reason for applying?
5. What weakness does she mention?
6. Where does she see herself in five years?

## Pronunciacion

### Word stress en vocabulario profesional

En entrevistas, pronunciar mal una palabra clave da muy mala impresion:

| Palabra | Acento | IPA | Error comun |
|---------|--------|-----|-------------|
| ex**PE**rience | 2a silaba | /ɪkˈspɪə.ri.əns/ | "EXperience" |
| res**PON**sible | 2a silaba | /rɪˈspɒn.sə.bəl/ | "reSPONSAble" |
| **OR**ganised | 1a silaba | /ˈɔːr.ɡə.naɪzd/ | "orgaNISED" |
| **IN**terview | 1a silaba | /ˈɪn.tər.vjuː/ | "interVIEW" |
| qua**LI**fi**CA**tion | 4a silaba | /ˌkwɒl.ɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən/ | Acento plano |
| op**POR**tunity | 3a silaba | /ˌɒp.ərˈtjuː.nə.ti/ | "OPPortunity" |
| en**THU**siastic | 3a silaba | /ɪnˌθjuː.ziˈæs.tɪk/ | Silabas planas |

**Practica**: "I have three years of ex**PE**rience. I'm very res**PON**sible and **OR**ganised."

### Entonacion formal en entrevistas

En una entrevista, tu entonacion transmite profesionalidad:

**Entonacion confiada (tono baja al final de la frase)**:
- "I have three years of experience↘."
- "I'm looking for a new challenge↘."
- "I'm very organised↘."

**Entonacion de pregunta educada (tono sube)**:
- "Could you tell me about the team↗?"
- "What would the typical day look like↗?"

**Error**: Terminar frases afirmativas con tono ascendente (suena inseguro o como pregunta):
- MAL: "I have experience↗?" (suena a que no estas seguro)
- BIEN: "I have experience↘." (suena confiado)

**Practica**: Di "I'd say I'm very organised and detail-oriented↘" con tono descendente y firme.

## Phrases — Entrevista de Trabajo

### Responder a "Tell me about yourself"

Estructura: Presente → Pasado → Futuro

1. "Currently, I'm working as a _______ at _______." (presente)
2. "I studied _______ and I have _____ years of experience in _______." (pasado)
3. "I'm looking for _______ because _______." (futuro/motivacion)

## Practica integradora

### Ejercicio 1: Dictado con vocabulario de entrevista

Escucha con TTS y escribe:

1. "I have five years of ex**pe**rience in software development."
2. "I'm very res**pon**sible and I work well under pressure."
3. "I'm looking for a new op**por**tunity in an international company."
4. "Could you tell me more about the qua**li**fi**ca**tions required?"

### Ejercicio 2: Tu "Tell me about yourself"

Prepara y graba tu respuesta de 30-45 segundos siguiendo la estructura:
1. **Ahora**: "Currently, I'm..." (trabajo o estudios actuales)
2. **Antes**: "I studied... and I have... experience in..."
3. **Motivacion**: "I'm looking for... because..."

Escuchate y verifica: ¿Suenas confiado? ¿La entonacion baja al final de las frases?

### Ejercicio 3: Responde estas preguntas

Practica en voz alta:

1. What are your main strengths?
2. What's your biggest weakness?
3. Why do you want to work here?
4. Where do you see yourself in five years?
5. Do you have any questions for us?

### Ejercicio 4: Traduce al ingles

1. Tengo tres años de experiencia en enseñanza.
2. Soy muy organizado y fiable.
3. Busco un nuevo reto profesional.
4. Me gustaria saber mas sobre el equipo.
