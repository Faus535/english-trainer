# Session 04 — En el Medico

## Objetivo

Integrar tres habilidades en el contexto de una visita al medico:
- **Listening**: Comprender como alguien describe sintomas y las instrucciones del medico
- **Pronunciacion**: /θ/ en "therapy", vocales en partes del cuerpo, -ed en "prescribed"
- **Vocabulary**: Sintomas, partes del cuerpo, terminos medicos basicos

Al final podras explicar que te pasa y entender las instrucciones del medico en ingles.

## Calentamiento

Piensa antes de empezar:
- ¿Sabes decir "me duele la cabeza" en ingles? (pista: NO es "I have pain in my head")
- ¿Conoces la diferencia entre "ache" y "pain"?
- ¿Has ido alguna vez al medico en un pais anglosajono?

## Listening

### Transcripcion del audio

Escucha con TTS sin leer. Luego lee y escucha de nuevo.

> **Doctor:** Good morning. What seems to be the problem?
> **Patient:** Hi, doctor. I've been feeling really unwell for the past three days. I have a terrible headache, and my throat is very sore.
> **Doctor:** I see. Do you have a temperature?
> **Patient:** Yes, I checked this morning. It was thirty-eight point five.
> **Doctor:** Any other symptoms? Cough, runny nose?
> **Patient:** I've had a cough since yesterday, and my body aches all over. I'm also really tired.
> **Doctor:** Let me take a look at your throat... Yes, it's quite red and swollen. I think you have the flu. It's going around at the moment.
> **Patient:** Oh no. What should I do?
> **Doctor:** I'm going to prescribe some medication for the fever and the sore throat. Take two tablets three times a day, after meals. You should also rest as much as possible and drink plenty of fluids.
> **Patient:** How long will it take to get better?
> **Doctor:** Usually about a week. If you're not feeling better after five days, come back and see me.
> **Patient:** Should I stay home from work?
> **Doctor:** Definitely. Stay home for at least three days. You're contagious.
> **Patient:** Okay, thank you, doctor.
> **Doctor:** Take care. Here's your prescription.

### Preguntas de comprension

1. How long has the patient been feeling unwell?
2. What are all the symptoms mentioned?
3. What does the doctor think is wrong?
4. How should the patient take the medication?
5. How many days should the patient stay home?

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. Three days
2. Headache, sore throat, temperature (38.5), cough, body aches, tiredness
3. The flu
4. Two tablets, three times a day, after meals
5. At least three days

</details>

## Pronunciacion

### /θ/ en contexto medico — "therapy", "throat", "thermometer"

El sonido /θ/ aparece mucho en vocabulario medico:

| Palabra | IPA | Como producirlo |
|---------|-----|----------------|
| therapy | /ˈθer.ə.pi/ | Lengua entre dientes + "erapy" |
| throat | /θroʊt/ | Lengua entre dientes + "roht" |
| thermometer | /θərˈmɒm.ɪ.tər/ | La th inicial es suave |
| three (times) | /θriː/ | No "free" ni "tree" |
| breathe | /briːð/ | La th final es sonora /ð/ |

**Diferencia clave**: "th" sorda /θ/ (como en "therapy") vs "th" sonora /ð/ (como en "breathe"). En la sonora, las cuerdas vocales vibran.

**Practica**: "My **throat** hurts. The **thermometer** says **thirty**-eight. I need **therapy** **three** times a week."

## Practica integradora

### Ejercicio 1: Dictado medico

Escucha con TTS y escribe:

1. "I've had a terrible **headache** and a **sore throat** for three days."
2. "The doctor **prescribed** some tablets for the **fever**."
3. "Take the medication **three** times a day, after meals."
4. "My **knee** hurts and my **stomach** aches."

### Ejercicio 2: Describe tus sintomas

Imagina que tienes gripe. Graba 30 segundos explicando al medico:
- Cuanto tiempo llevas mal
- Que sintomas tienes (al menos 3)
- Si has tomado algo

Modelo: "I've been feeling unwell for two days. I have a headache and a sore throat. I also have a cough and I feel very tired. I haven't taken any medication yet."

### Ejercicio 3: Traduce al ingles

1. Me duele la garganta desde ayer.
2. Tengo fiebre — treinta y ocho grados.
3. El medico me receto antibioticos.
4. Tome dos pastillas despues de cada comida.
5. ¿Deberia quedarme en casa?

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. My throat has been sore since yesterday. / I've had a sore throat since yesterday.
2. I have a fever — thirty-eight degrees.
3. The doctor prescribed me antibiotics.
4. Take two tablets after each meal.
5. Should I stay home?

</details>
