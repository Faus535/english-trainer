# Unit 04 — Formal Debate & Academic Discussion

## Objetivo

Dominar el registro formal para debates, presentaciones y textos académicos. Estas expresiones te permiten argumentar con autoridad, citar evidencia y estructurar ideas como un profesional. Son las frases que oirás en conferencias, TED Talks, artículos de opinión y debates serios. Te elevan del "I think" al nivel de un hablante educado y persuasivo.

## Frases

| English | Pronunciation hint | Spanish | When to use it |
|---------|--------------------|---------|----------------|
| It could be argued that... | /ɪt kʊd bi ˈɑːr.ɡjuːd ðæt/ | Se podría argumentar que... | Para presentar un argumento sin hacerlo tuyo. Distancia académica. Elegante. |
| One might contend... | /wʌn maɪt kənˈtend/ | Uno podría sostener que... | Más formal aún. Perfecto para ensayos y presentaciones. |
| While there is merit in... | /waɪl ðeər ɪz ˈmer.ɪt ɪn/ | Aunque hay mérito en... | Reconoces el argumento contrario antes de contradecirlo. Muy persuasivo. |
| The evidence suggests... | /ðə ˈev.ɪ.dəns səˈdʒests/ | La evidencia sugiere... | Para respaldar tu argumento con datos. Más fuerte que "I think". |
| It is worth noting that... | /ɪt ɪz wɜːrθ ˈnoʊ.tɪŋ ðæt/ | Cabe señalar que... / Merece la pena destacar que... | Para añadir un punto importante que otros podrían pasar por alto. |
| This raises the question of... | /ðɪs ˈreɪ.zɪz ðə ˈkwes.tʃən əv/ | Esto plantea la pregunta de... | Para señalar implicaciones. Muestra pensamiento crítico. |
| There is a growing body of evidence... | /ðeər ɪz ə ˈɡroʊ.ɪŋ ˈbɒd.i əv ˈev.ɪ.dəns/ | Hay cada vez más evidencia... | Para hablar de tendencias respaldadas por datos. Muy académico. |

## Diálogos

### Diálogo 1 — Panel de discusión

> **Moderator:** Should AI replace teachers in schools?
> **You:** It could be argued that AI can personalize learning better than a single teacher with 30 students. The evidence suggests that adaptive learning tools improve outcomes.
> **Opponent:** But children need human connection, not screens.
> **You:** While there is merit in that view, it is worth noting that we're not talking about replacing teachers entirely — we're talking about augmenting their capabilities.
> **Opponent:** But where does it end?
> **You:** This raises the question of regulation, which is a separate but important issue.

### Diálogo 2 — Presentación en el trabajo

> **You:** There is a growing body of evidence that remote work increases productivity. However, it would be premature to conclude that offices are obsolete.
> **Audience member:** So what's your recommendation?
> **You:** One might contend that a hybrid model offers the best of both worlds. The evidence suggests that two to three days in the office maximizes both collaboration and focus time.
> **Audience member:** But what about company culture?
> **You:** It is worth noting that culture is built through meaningful interaction, not just physical proximity. This raises the question of how we define "presence" in the modern workplace.

## Práctica

### Reformula estas opiniones simples en registro formal

1. "I think climate change is a big problem."
   → _______

2. "Some people say social media is bad, but I disagree."
   → _______

3. "Studies show that exercise helps mental health."
   → _______

4. "We shouldn't make a decision yet."
   → _______

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. There is a growing body of evidence that climate change poses a significant threat.
2. While there is merit in the argument that social media has negative effects, one might contend that its benefits outweigh the drawbacks.
3. The evidence suggests that regular exercise has a positive impact on mental health.
4. It would be premature to reach a conclusion at this stage.

</details>

### Traduce a inglés (registro formal)

1. Se podría argumentar que la educación online es igual de efectiva.
2. Cabe señalar que no todos los estudios llegan a la misma conclusión.
3. Esto plantea la pregunta de quién es responsable.
4. Aunque hay mérito en esa posición, la evidencia sugiere lo contrario.
5. Sería prematuro tomar una decisión sin más datos.

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. It could be argued that online education is equally effective.
2. It is worth noting that not all studies reach the same conclusion.
3. This raises the question of who is responsible.
4. While there is merit in that position, the evidence suggests otherwise.
5. It would be premature to make a decision without further data.

</details>
