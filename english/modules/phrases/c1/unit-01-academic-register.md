# Unit 01 — Academic Register

## Objetivo

Dominar el registro académico en inglés: el lenguaje de papers, tesis, conferencias, informes y presentaciones de alto nivel. No se trata de sonar "complicado" sino de sonar preciso, objetivo y autoritativo. Estas expresiones transforman opiniones en argumentos y comentarios en análisis. Si quieres publicar, presentar en conferencias o escribir informes ejecutivos, necesitas este registro.

## Diálogos

### Diálogo 1 — Presentación en conferencia

> **You:** Good morning. It is widely acknowledged that climate change poses an existential threat. However, the findings from our research indicate that current mitigation strategies are insufficient.
> **Audience:** What do you propose?
> **You:** In light of the evidence, we believe a paradigm shift is needed — from reactive to proactive policy. To that end, we have developed a framework that integrates economic and environmental data.
> **Audience:** But isn't that too ambitious?
> **You:** Notwithstanding the limitations of our model, a comprehensive analysis reveals that early intervention is significantly more cost-effective. The implications of this are far-reaching — it is imperative that policymakers act now rather than later.

### Diálogo 2 — Defensa de tesis

> **Examiner:** Your methodology is interesting. But your sample size is quite small.
> **You:** That's a fair observation. Notwithstanding the limitations of the sample size, the findings indicate a clear pattern that is consistent with previous research. This warrants further investigation with a larger cohort.
> **Examiner:** And what are the practical applications?
> **You:** It is widely acknowledged that this field needs more data-driven approaches. In light of the evidence presented here, we propose a new diagnostic tool. To that end, we've already begun preliminary trials.
> **Examiner:** The implications could be significant.
> **You:** Indeed. A comprehensive analysis of the initial results reveals promising outcomes, though it is imperative that we validate these findings through broader testing.

## Práctica

### Reformula al registro académico

Transforma estas frases simples:

1. "Everyone knows that exercise is good for you."
   → _______

2. "Our research shows that the drug works."
   → _______

3. "Even though our study wasn't perfect, the results are useful."
   → _______

4. "This is really important and we need to do more research."
   → _______

5. "This changes everything about how we understand the brain."
   → _______

<details>
<summary>Respuestas</summary>

1. It is widely acknowledged that physical exercise has significant health benefits.
2. The findings indicate that the drug demonstrates efficacy.
3. Notwithstanding the limitations of the study, the results provide valuable insights.
4. This warrants further investigation. It is imperative that additional research is conducted.
5. This represents a paradigm shift in our understanding of the brain. The implications are far-reaching.

</details>

### Escribe un párrafo académico

Elige un tema (inteligencia artificial, cambio climático, educación online) y escribe un párrafo de 5-6 frases usando al menos 5 expresiones de esta unidad.

Ejemplo:

> "It is widely acknowledged that artificial intelligence is transforming the workplace. The findings from recent studies indicate that up to 40% of current jobs may be automated within two decades. In light of the evidence, this represents a paradigm shift in employment. The implications of this are far-reaching — it is imperative that governments invest in retraining programs. To that end, several nations have already launched digital skills initiatives. Notwithstanding the limitations of early programs, a comprehensive analysis reveals that participants show significantly improved employability."

### Traduce a inglés (registro académico)

1. Los hallazgos indican que la terapia es efectiva.
2. Es ampliamente reconocido que la pobreza afecta a la educación.
3. A la luz de la evidencia, recomendamos cambiar el protocolo.
4. Esto merece una investigación más profunda con una muestra más grande.
5. A pesar de las limitaciones, las implicaciones son de gran alcance.

1. The findings indicate that the therapy is effective.
2. It is widely acknowledged that poverty affects education.
3. In light of the evidence, we recommend changing the protocol.
4. This warrants further investigation with a larger sample.
5. Notwithstanding the limitations, the implications are far-reaching.
