## Bloque 2: Past Continuous (10 min)

### Estructura

> **Subject + was/were + verb-ing**

| Sujeto | Auxiliar | Ejemplo |
|--------|----------|---------|
| I / He / She / It | was | I **was working** at 8 pm. |
| You / We / They | were | They **were sleeping** when I called. |

### Negativo e interrogativo

- Negativo: I **wasn't** working. / They **weren't** sleeping.
- Pregunta: **Was** she working? / **Were** they sleeping?

### Cuando usarlo

**1. Accion en progreso interrumpida por otra:**

> I **was reading** when she **called**. (leia = en progreso, llamo = interrupcion)

La accion larga va en Past Continuous, la corta en Past Simple.

**2. Dos acciones simultaneas:**

> While she **was reading**, he **was cleaning**. (las dos al mismo tiempo)

**3. Describir una escena pasada:**

> The sun **was shining**. Birds **were singing**. People **were walking** in the park.

### Palabras clave

| Palabra | Uso |
|---------|-----|
| when | I was cooking **when** the phone rang. |
| while | **While** I was studying, my brother arrived. |
| at that time | **At that time**, we were living in Barcelona. |
| all day/night | It **was raining** all day. |

### Ejercicio: Completa con Past Continuous o Past Simple

1. I ___ (walk) home when it ___ (start) to rain. → **was walking**, **started**
2. While she ___ (cook), the kids ___ (play) outside. → **was cooking**, **were playing**
3. He ___ (sleep) when the alarm ___ (go off). → **was sleeping**, **went off**
4. They ___ (watch) TV when I ___ (arrive). → **were watching**, **arrived**
5. At 10 pm, I ___ (study) for my exam. → **was studying**

## Bloque 3: Idioms — 10 mas (5 min)

| Idiom | Significado | Ejemplo |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| a piece of cake | muy facil | That test was a piece of cake! |
| once in a blue moon | muy raramente | I eat fast food once in a blue moon. |
