## Bloque 2: Phrasal Verbs ‚ÄĒ Vida diaria y hogar (10 min)

| Phrasal verb | Significado | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| wake up | despertarse | I **wake up** at 7 every day. |
| get up | levantarse | I **got up** late this morning. |
| tidy up | ordenar | Can you **tidy up** your room? |
| clean up | limpiar | We need to **clean up** after dinner. |
| throw away | tirar (a la basura) | Don't **throw away** those papers! |
| put away | guardar | Please **put away** your clothes. |
| turn on | encender | **Turn on** the lights, please. |
| turn off | apagar | **Turn off** the TV before bed. |
| run out of | quedarse sin | We've **run out of** milk. |
| break down | averiarse | My car **broke down** on the highway. |

**Ejercicio:** Completa con el phrasal verb correcto:
1. I always ___ at 6:30 AM. (despertarse)
2. Don't forget to ___ the oven. (apagar)
3. We ___ sugar ‚ÄĒ can you buy some? (quedarse sin)
4. Please ___ your toys. (guardar)
5. My laptop ___ yesterday. (averiarse)
