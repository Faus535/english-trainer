# Dia 65 — Dictation + Reported Speech Practice (25 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Hacer dictado con audio real y practicar transformaciones de estilo directo a reported speech.

## Bloque 1: Dictation (10 min)

**Instrucciones:**
1. Elige un video en YouTube (noticias, podcast, documental)
2. Escucha 60 segundos completos sin pausar
3. Escribe todo lo que puedas
4. Rebobina y escucha de nuevo, pausando cada 10 segundos
5. Verifica con subtitulos si estan disponibles

**Enfoque de hoy:** Presta atencion a como los hablantes enlazan palabras (linking) y eliminan sonidos (elision). Marca las partes donde la pronunciacion real difiere mucho de lo escrito.

**Meta:** Captar al menos el 65% de las palabras.

## Bloque 2: Reported Speech — Practica intensiva (10 min)

**Transforma estas citas directas:**

| # | Directo | Reported Speech |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | "I will come tomorrow." | He said he **would come the next day**. |
| 2 | "We are leaving now." | They said they **were leaving then**. |
| 3 | "I have never been to Japan." | She said she **had never been to Japan**. |
| 4 | "I can't find my keys." | He said he **couldn't find his keys**. |
| 5 | "We must finish today." | They said they **had to finish that day**. |

**Nota:** "must" cambia a "had to" en reported speech.

**Ejercicio — Forma 5 oraciones originales de reported speech:**

Imagina que tu amigo te dijo estas cosas ayer. Reportalas:
1. "I'm feeling sick." → My friend said ___
2. "I'll call you later." → He said ___
3. "I don't like this movie." → She said ___
4. "We've already eaten." → They said ___
5. "I can help you tomorrow." → He said ___

**Respuestas:**
1. My friend said he/she was feeling sick.
2. He said he would call me later.
3. She said she didn't like that movie.
4. They said they had already eaten.
5. He said he could help me the next day.

## Bloque 3: Flashcards — 20 palabras (5 min)

Repasa 20 palabras de vocabulario. Metodo rapido: mira la palabra, di el significado y una frase ejemplo.
