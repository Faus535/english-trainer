# Dia 85 — Fillers y Discourse Markers (30 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Aprender los sonidos y palabras que los nativos usan para pensar, conectar ideas y ganar tiempo en conversacion. Introducir el Present Perfect Continuous.

## Bloque 1: Fillers y Discourse Markers (10 min)

Los **fillers** NO son mal ingles — son herramientas naturales de conversacion. Sin ellos, suenas como un robot.

| Filler | IPA | Uso | Ejemplo |
|--------|-----|-----|---------|
| Well... | /wel/ | Introducir respuesta pensativa | "Well... I think it depends." |
| You know... | /jə.noʊ/ | Buscar acuerdo del oyente | "It was, you know, really hard." |
| I mean... | /aɪ.miːn/ | Aclarar o corregir | "I mean, it's not that bad." |
| Like... | /laɪk/ | Pausa casual (informal) | "It was like... amazing." |
| So... | /soʊ/ | Introducir conclusion o tema | "So, what do you think?" |
| Actually... | /ˈæk.tʃu.ə.li/ | Corregir expectativa | "Actually, I changed my mind." |
| Basically... | /ˈbeɪ.sɪk.li/ | Resumir | "Basically, we need more time." |
| Honestly... | /ˈɒn.ɪst.li/ | Dar opinion sincera | "Honestly, I didn't like it." |
| Right... | /raɪt/ | Confirmar o ganar tiempo | "Right, so the thing is..." |
| Let me think... | /let.mi.θɪŋk/ | Pedir tiempo para pensar | "Let me think... maybe Tuesday?" |

**Conversacion ejemplo:**
- A: "What do you think about the new office?"
- B: "Well... honestly, I think it's too small. I mean, it's nice, but, you know, we need more space."
- A: "Right. So, basically, you don't like it?"
- B: "Actually, I like the design. It's just... like... the size is the problem."

**Ejercicio:** Elige 3-4 fillers y responde estas preguntas en voz alta usandolos:
1. What's your favorite food?
2. What do you do on weekends?
3. Do you like your job?

## Bloque 2: Present Perfect Continuous (10 min)

**Estructura:** have/has + been + verb-**ing**

| Sujeto | Forma | Ejemplo |
|--------|-------|---------|
| I/You/We/They | have been + -ing | "I **have been working** all day." |
| He/She/It | has been + -ing | "She **has been studying** for 3 hours." |

**Uso principal:** Accion que empezo en el pasado y **continua ahora**, o accion reciente con **resultado visible**.
- "She's tired because she **has been running**." (resultado visible: esta cansada)
- "It **has been raining** all morning." (sigue lloviendo o acaba de parar)

**Diferencia con Present Perfect Simple:**
- "I **have written** 3 emails." → Enfasis en **resultado** (3 emails completados)
- "I **have been writing** emails." → Enfasis en **duracion/actividad** (la actividad en si)

**Palabras clave:** for, since, all day, all morning, how long, lately, recently

**Ejercicio:** Completa con Present Perfect Continuous:
1. How long _______ you _______ (wait)?
2. She _______ (study) English since January.
3. It _______ (rain) all day.
4. They _______ (not/sleep) well lately.
5. I _______ (learn) English for 3 months.

> Respuestas: 1) have...been waiting 2) has been studying 3) has been raining 4) haven't been sleeping 5) have been learning

## Bloque 3: Serie sin subtitulos (10 min)

Pon un episodio de tu serie favorita **sin subtitulos**. No pares ni rebobines. Deja que el ingles fluya. Intenta captar la idea general, no cada palabra. Usa el contexto visual para ayudarte.

**Despues de ver:** Escribe 3 frases en ingles resumiendo lo que entendiste.
