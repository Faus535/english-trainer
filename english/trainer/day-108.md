# Dia 108 — Conversacion + Gramatica (30 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Conversacion real de 15 minutos usando TODAS las estructuras aprendidas. Luego, revision de tus 3 puntos gramaticales mas debiles.

## Bloque 1: Conversacion Real — Usa Todo (15 min)

Esta es tu conversacion mas ambiciosa. Usa Tandem, HelloTalk, Discord, amigo, espejo o IA.

**Mision:** En 15 minutos, intenta usar TODAS estas herramientas:

| Herramienta | Ejemplo |
|-------------|---------|
| Fillers | "Well...", "I mean...", "You know..." |
| Indirect questions | "Could you tell me...?", "I was wondering if..." |
| Used to / Would | "I used to play...", "We would go to..." |
| Conditionals | "If I had more time, I would...", "If it rains, I'll..." |
| Passive voice | "English is spoken...", "It was built in..." |
| Relative clauses | "The person who...", "The place where..." |
| Present Perfect Continuous | "I've been learning...", "I've been working..." |

**Temas sugeridos para cubrir todo:**
1. **Tu progreso en ingles:** "I've been learning English for about 4 months. I used to be afraid of speaking, but now I feel more confident."
2. **Planes futuros:** "If I keep practicing, I'll be fluent soon. I was wondering if you could recommend some resources."
3. **Tu ciudad:** "The city where I live is... It was founded in... English is spoken in many places here."

**No te preocupes si no usas todas.** El objetivo es intentarlo. Anota cuales usaste.

**Despues:** Marca las que usaste:
- [ ] Fillers
- [ ] Indirect questions
- [ ] Used to / Would
- [ ] Conditionals
- [ ] Passive voice
- [ ] Relative clauses
- [ ] Present Perfect Continuous

## Bloque 2: Revision de Gramatica — Tus 3 Debilidades (15 min)

**Paso 1:** Identifica tus 3 puntos gramaticales mas debiles. ¿Cuales te cuestan mas?

Opciones comunes:
- Tiempos verbales (Present Perfect vs Past Simple)
- Passive voice
- Reported speech
- Conditionals (first vs second)
- Relative clauses
- Indirect questions
- Used to vs Would

**Paso 2:** Para cada punto debil, escribe 5 oraciones correctas.

**Ejemplo — Si tu debilidad es "conditionals":**
1. "If it rains tomorrow, I will stay home." (first)
2. "If I were rich, I would travel the world." (second)
3. "If she studies hard, she will pass the exam." (first)
4. "If I had more time, I would learn Japanese." (second)
5. "If you heat water to 100°C, it boils." (zero)

**Paso 3:** Lee las 15 oraciones (5 x 3 debilidades) en voz alta. Si alguna no suena natural, reescribela.

**Paso 4:** Elige la estructura que peor se te da y escribe un mini parrafo de 3-4 frases usandola en contexto.
