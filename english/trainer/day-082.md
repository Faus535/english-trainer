# Dia 82 — FULL TEST Block C (30 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Evaluar todo lo aprendido en el Bloque C (Semanas 9-12): pronunciacion, gramatica y comprension auditiva.

## Bloque 1: Test completo (15 min)

### Parte 1: Comprension auditiva (5 min)
Mira 5 minutos de una serie **sin subtitulos**.
- Que porcentaje entiendes? ____%
- Puedes seguir la trama general? Si / No
- Captaste alguna reduced form? Cuales? ___

**Escala:**
- 30-40%: Sigue practicando, vas progresando
- 40-60%: Buen nivel, sigue con el shadowing
- 60-80%: Muy bien, estas captando connected speech
- 80%+: Excelente, puedes consumir contenido nativo

### Parte 2: Gramatica — 10 oraciones (7 min)

Forma las siguientes oraciones **sin mirar apuntes:**

**Passive Voice:**
1. (presente) English / speak / in many countries → ___
2. (pasado) The Mona Lisa / paint / by Da Vinci → ___
3. (present perfect) The work / already / finish → ___

**Reported Speech:**
4. "I am leaving tomorrow." → She said ___
5. "We can't come today." → They told me ___
6. "I have never been to Japan." → He said ___

**Relative Clauses:**
7. (defining, who) The student / won / is my neighbor → ___
8. (non-defining, which) Paris / is beautiful / is in France → ___
9. (where) That's the restaurant / we had dinner → ___

**Indirect Question:**
10. Where is the station? → Could you tell me ___?

**Respuestas:**
1. English is spoken in many countries.
2. The Mona Lisa was painted by Da Vinci.
3. The work has already been finished.
4. She said she was leaving the next day.
5. They told me they couldn't come that day.
6. He said he had never been to Japan.
7. The student who won is my neighbor.
8. Paris, which is beautiful, is in France.
9. That's the restaurant where we had dinner.
10. Could you tell me where the station is?

### Parte 3: Pronunciacion — 5 minimal pairs (3 min)

Di cada par en voz alta y nota la diferencia:

| Par | Sonido 1 | Sonido 2 |
|---|---|---|
| ship /ʃɪp/ vs sheep /ʃiːp/ | /ɪ/ corta | /iː/ larga |
| measure /ˈmeʒ.ər/ vs mesher /ˈmeʃ.ər/ | /ʒ/ sonora | /ʃ/ sorda |
| about /əˈbaʊt/ vs a boot /ə buːt/ | /ə/ schwa | /uː/ larga |
| think /θɪŋk/ vs sink /sɪŋk/ | /θ/ dental | /s/ alveolar |
| butter /ˈbʌt.ər/ vs better /ˈbet.ər/ | /ʌ/ | /e/ |

**Autoevaluacion:**

- 8-10: Excelente dominio del Bloque C
- 6-7: Buen nivel, repasa los puntos debiles
- Menos de 6: Necesitas repasar — vuelve a los dias de errores

## Bloque 2: Pelicula sin subtitulos (15 min)

Mira 15 minutos de una pelicula en ingles **sin subtitulos**.

**Mientras miras, nota:**
- Reduced forms (gonna, wanna, shoulda...)
- Sentence stress (que palabras suenan mas fuertes)
- Linking y elision entre palabras
- Diferencia de acento si hay personajes americanos y britanicos

**Despues:** Puedes activar subtitulos y rever la misma escena para verificar cuanto captaste.
