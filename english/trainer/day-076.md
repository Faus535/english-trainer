# Dia 76 — Repaso semanal (30 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Consolidar todo lo aprendido en la Semana 11: sentence stress, ritmo del ingles y relative clauses.

## Bloque 1: Repaso activo (15 min)

### Test 1: Relative Clauses — Forma 5 oraciones

Escribe 5 oraciones con relative clauses (sin mirar apuntes):
1. Defining con "who": ___
2. Defining con "which/that": ___
3. Non-defining con "who": ___
4. Con "where": ___
5. Con "whose": ___

**Ejemplo de respuestas:**
1. The doctor who treated me was very professional.
2. The phone that I bought last week is already broken.
3. My sister, who lives in Barcelona, is visiting next month.
4. That's the restaurant where we had our anniversary dinner.
5. The student whose project won the competition is only 16.

### Test 2: Sentence Stress — Marca el stress en 5 oraciones

Marca las palabras acentuadas con MAYUSCULAS:
1. We should have left earlier. → We **SHOULD** have **LEFT** **EAR**lier.
2. The meeting was cancelled. → The **MEET**ing was **CAN**celled.
3. I think she's right about that. → I **THINK** she's **RIGHT** aBOUT **THAT**.
4. They've been working all day. → They've been **WORK**ing **ALL** **DAY**.
5. Can you pass me the salt? → Can you **PASS** me the **SALT**?

### Test 3: Content vs Function Words

Clasifica estas palabras como Content (C) o Function (F):
1. beautiful → C
2. the → F
3. quickly → C
4. of → F
5. believe → C
6. is → F
7. yesterday → C
8. and → F

**Si acertaste 7-8:** Excelente comprension.
**Si acertaste 5-6:** Repasa el Dia 74.

## Bloque 2: Episodio con subtitulos en ingles (15 min)

Mira 15 minutos de un episodio con subtitulos en ingles.

**Mientras miras:**
- Nota que palabras el actor dice mas fuerte (content words)
- Identifica relative clauses en los dialogos
- Presta atencion al ritmo: las frases largas suenan igual de rapidas que las cortas
