# Dia 66 — Shadowing + Reported Speech Errors (25 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Practicar shadowing con pronunciacion del sonido L y corregir los errores mas comunes de reported speech.

## Bloque 1: Shadowing — BBC Learning English (10 min)

**Video:** "Pronunciation Tips: L Sound" — BBC Learning English (YouTube: dX60zUj_jt8)

**Instrucciones:**
1. Mira el video completo una vez (2-3 min)
2. Rebobina al inicio
3. Repite cada frase **al mismo tiempo** que el hablante
4. Enfocate en la posicion de la lengua para el sonido /l/:
   - **Light L** (inicio de silaba): lengua toca el alveolar ridge — "light" /laɪt/, "love" /lʌv/
   - **Dark L** (final de silaba): lengua se retrae — "full" /fʊɫ/, "call" /kɔːɫ/
5. Repite cada seccion 3 veces

**Ejercicio:** Practica estos pares:
- light /laɪt/ vs right /raɪt/
- led /led/ vs red /red/
- feel /fiːl/ vs fill /fɪl/
- pull /pʊl/ vs pool /puːl/

## Bloque 2: Reported Speech — Errores de hispanohablantes (10 min)

### Error 1: No hacer backshift
- MAL: "She said that she **is** tired."
- BIEN: "She said that she **was** tired."
- **Regla:** Si el verbo introductor esta en pasado (said, told), el tiempo verbal retrocede.

### Error 2: Confundir SAY y TELL
- **Say** = NO lleva persona directamente despues
  - "He **said** that he was happy." (correcto)
  - "He **said me** that he was happy." (INCORRECTO)
- **Tell** = SIEMPRE lleva persona
  - "He **told me** that he was happy." (correcto)
  - "He **told** that he was happy." (INCORRECTO)

### Error 3: No cambiar pronombres
- Directo: "**I** like **my** car."
- MAL: "He said **I** liked **my** car."
- BIEN: "He said **he** liked **his** car."

### Error 4: No cambiar expresiones de tiempo
- Directo: "I will do it **tomorrow**."
- MAL: "She said she would do it **tomorrow**."
- BIEN: "She said she would do it **the next day**."

**Ejercicio — Corrige estos errores:**
1. She told that she was busy. → ___
2. He said me he would come. → ___
3. They said they are leaving now. → ___
4. She said I am happy. (reportando lo que ella dijo de si misma) → ___
5. He told that he can swim. → ___

**Respuestas:**
1. She said (that) she was busy. / She told me (that) she was busy.
2. He said he would come. / He told me he would come.
3. They said they were leaving then.
4. She said she was happy.
5. He told me (that) he could swim. / He said (that) he could swim.
