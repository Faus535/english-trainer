## Bloque 2: Present Simple vs Past Simple (10 min)

| Present Simple (habitos) | Past Simple (acciones terminadas) |
|-------------------------|----------------------------------|
| I **work** every day. | I **worked** yesterday. |
| She **plays** tennis. | She **played** tennis last week. |
| We **don't eat** meat. | We **didn't eat** meat at the party. |
| **Do** you **speak** English? | **Did** you **speak** English there? |
| He **goes** to the gym. | He **went** to the gym yesterday. |

**Practica — forma 10 pares de frases:**

1. I **get up** at 7. / Yesterday I **got up** at 9.
2. She **drives** to work. / Last Monday she **drove** to work.
3. We **have** lunch at 1. / We **had** lunch at 2 yesterday.
4. They **don't watch** TV. / They **didn't watch** TV last night.
5. **Do** you **cook** dinner? / **Did** you **cook** dinner yesterday?
6. He **reads** a book every night. / He **read** a book last night.
7. I **take** the bus. / I **took** the bus yesterday.
8. She **makes** coffee. / She **made** coffee this morning.
9. We **see** our friends on Fridays. / We **saw** our friends last Friday.
10. **Does** he **run**? / **Did** he **run** yesterday?
