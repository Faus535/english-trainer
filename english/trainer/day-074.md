# Dia 74 — Pronunciacion + Sentence Stress (25 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Dominar el sentence stress (que palabras acentuar en una oracion) y repasar verbos irregulares.

## Bloque 1: Sentence Stress — Content vs Function Words (10 min)

En ingles, NO todas las palabras se pronuncian con la misma fuerza. Las **content words** se acentuan, las **function words** se reducen.

### Content words (ACENTUADAS) — llevan significado
- Sustantivos: book, dog, city
- Verbos principales: run, eat, think
- Adjetivos: big, happy, fast
- Adverbios: quickly, always, never

### Function words (reducidas) — solo conectan
- Articulos: the, a, an
- Preposiciones: to, of, in, at, for
- Pronombres: I, he, she, it, we, they
- Auxiliares: is, are, was, have, do
- Conjunciones: and, but, or

### Practica — Las MAYUSCULAS son las palabras acentuadas:

| # | Oracion | Con stress marcado |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | I want to go to the shop. | I **WANT** to **GO** to the **SHOP**. |
| 2 | She said she would come tomorrow. | She **SAID** she would **COME** to**MOR**row. |
| 3 | The children are playing in the park. | The **CHIL**dren are **PLAY**ing in the **PARK**. |
| 4 | He bought a new car last week. | He **BOUGHT** a **NEW** **CAR** **LAST** **WEEK**. |
| 5 | I don't know what to do. | I don't **KNOW** what to **DO**. |
| 6 | She's working at the hospital now. | She's **WORK**ing at the **HOS**pital **NOW**. |
| 7 | They live in a big house near the river. | They **LIVE** in a **BIG** **HOUSE** near the **RIV**er. |
| 8 | We need to finish before Friday. | We **NEED** to **FIN**ish be**FORE** **FRI**day. |
| 9 | Can you help me with this? | Can you **HELP** me with **THIS**? |
| 10 | I've been waiting for two hours. | I've been **WAIT**ing for **TWO** **HOURS**. |

**Ejercicio:** Lee las 10 oraciones en voz alta. Exagera el stress al principio — di las content words MAS FUERTE y las function words casi susurradas. Luego suaviza hasta que suene natural.

## Bloque 2: Verbos irregulares 125-150 — Repaso (10 min)

Repasa los verbos irregulares mas dificiles. Enfocate en los participios que mas se confunden:

| Base | Past | Past Participle | Significado |
|---|---|---|---|
| bite | bit | bitten | morder |
| hide | hid | hidden | esconder |
| rise | rose | risen | subir/levantarse |
| shake | shook | shaken | agitar/temblar |
| steal | stole | stolen | robar |
| tear | tore | torn | rasgar |
| swear | swore | sworn | jurar |
| freeze | froze | frozen | congelar |
| weave | wove | woven | tejer |
| slide | slid | slid | deslizar |

**Ejercicio — Completa el past participle:**
1. bite - bit - ___ (bitten)
2. hide - hid - ___ (hidden)
3. steal - stole - ___ (stolen)
4. tear - tore - ___ (torn)
5. freeze - froze - ___ (frozen)

## Bloque 3: Series sin subtitulos (5 min)

Mira 5 minutos de serie. Enfocate en el sentence stress — nota como las palabras importantes suenan mas fuertes y claras.
