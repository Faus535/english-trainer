# Dia 80 ‚ÄĒ Indirect Questions (25 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Aprender a formar preguntas indirectas (mas educadas) y practicar mas frases dificiles con IPA.

## Bloque 1: Indirect Questions (10 min)

Las **preguntas indirectas** son mas educadas y formales. La regla clave: en la pregunta indirecta **NO hay inversion** de sujeto y verbo.

### Estructura
- Directa: auxiliar + sujeto + verbo (inversion)
- Indirecta: sujeto + verbo (orden normal, como en una afirmacion)

### Transformaciones

| # | Pregunta directa | Pregunta indirecta |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Where is the bank? | Could you tell me **where the bank is**? |
| 2 | What time does it start? | Do you know **what time it starts**? |
| 3 | How much does it cost? | Could you tell me **how much it costs**? |
| 4 | Where did she go? | Do you know **where she went**? |
| 5 | Why is he late? | Do you have any idea **why he is late**? |

**Nota importante:** En preguntas de si/no, usamos **if** o **whether**:
- "Is she coming?" ‚Üí "Do you know **if/whether she is coming**?"
- "Did he pass?" ‚Üí "Could you tell me **if/whether he passed**?"

### Frases introductorias comunes
- Could you tell me...?
- Do you know...?
- Do you have any idea...?
- I was wondering...
- Would you mind telling me...?
- I'd like to know...

**Error tipico de hispanohablantes:**
- MAL: "Could you tell me **where is** the bank?" (inversion!)
- BIEN: "Could you tell me **where the bank is**?" (orden normal)

**Ejercicio ‚ÄĒ Transforma a preguntas indirectas:**
1. Where is the nearest station? ‚Üí Could you tell me ___?
2. What time does the shop close? ‚Üí Do you know ___?
3. How old is she? ‚Üí Do you have any idea ___?
4. Is there a pharmacy nearby? ‚Üí Could you tell me ___?
5. When did the meeting finish? ‚Üí Do you know ___?

**Respuestas:**
1. Could you tell me where the nearest station is?
2. Do you know what time the shop closes?
3. Do you have any idea how old she is?
4. Could you tell me if there is a pharmacy nearby?
5. Do you know when the meeting finished?
