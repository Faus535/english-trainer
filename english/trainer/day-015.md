# Dia 15 — Vocales largas (25 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Aprender las 5 vocales largas del ingles y los 20 verbos irregulares mas importantes.

## Bloque 1: Las 5 vocales largas (10 min)

Las vocales largas se marcan con /ː/ en IPA. Son mas largas que las españolas — estira el sonido el doble.

| Simbolo | Ejemplo | Sonido | Truco |
|---------|---------|--------|-------|
| /iː/ | s**ee**, m**ea**t | "i" española pero MAS larga | Sonrie y estira: "iiii" |
| /ɑː/ | c**a**r, f**a**ther | "a" abierta y larga | Abre mucho la boca: "aaaa" |
| /ɔː/ | d**oo**r, m**o**re | "o" larga y redondeada | Redondea los labios: "oooo" |
| /uː/ | f**oo**d, bl**ue** | "u" española pero MAS larga | Frunce los labios: "uuuu" |
| /ɜː/ | b**ir**d, w**or**k | NO existe en español | Boca relajada, lengua en medio, di "eee" pero sin mover los labios |

**El sonido /ɜː/ es el mas dificil.** Aparece en palabras muy comunes: bird, word, work, first, girl, learn, earth, heard. Practica: di "e" pero con los labios relajados (no sonrias).

**Diferencia corta vs larga — cambia el significado:**

| Corta | Larga | Significado |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| ship /ʃɪp/ | sheep /ʃiːp/ | barco / oveja |
| pull /pʊl/ | pool /puːl/ | tirar / piscina |
| cot /kɒt/ | cart /kɑːt/ | cuna / carro |
| pot /pɒt/ | port /pɔːt/ | olla / puerto |

## Bloque 2: Top 20 verbos irregulares (10 min)

Los verbos irregulares no siguen la regla de -ed. Hay que memorizarlos.

| Base | Pasado | Participio | Español |
|------|--------|-----------|---------|
| be | was/were | been | ser/estar |
| have | had | had | tener |
| do | did | done | hacer |
| say | said | said | decir |
| go | went | gone | ir |
| get | got | got | obtener |
| make | made | made | hacer/fabricar |
| know | knew | known | saber |
| think | thought | thought | pensar |
| take | took | taken | tomar |
| see | saw | seen | ver |
| come | came | come | venir |
| find | found | found | encontrar |
| give | gave | given | dar |
| tell | told | told | decir/contar |
| feel | felt | felt | sentir |
| become | became | become | convertirse |
| leave | left | left | irse |
| put | put | put | poner |
| mean | meant | meant | significar |

**Patrones utiles:** Algunos no cambian nunca: put/put/put, cut/cut/cut, let/let/let.
