## Bloque 2: Relative Clauses — Practica (10 min)

### Combinar oraciones con relative clauses

| # | Oraciones separadas | Combinada |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | I have a friend. He speaks Chinese. | I have a friend **who speaks Chinese**. |
| 2 | She bought a dress. The dress was expensive. | She bought a dress **which/that was expensive**. |
| 3 | That's the hotel. We stayed there last year. | That's the hotel **where we stayed last year**. |
| 4 | I met a woman. Her son is a doctor. | I met a woman **whose son is a doctor**. |
| 5 | Do you remember the day? We first met that day. | Do you remember the day **when we first met**? |

**Ejercicio — Combina estas oraciones:**
1. I know a girl. She speaks 5 languages. → ___
2. This is the park. I play football here. → ___
3. He has a brother. His brother lives in Canada. → ___
4. That was the moment. I realized I was wrong. → ___
5. She works at a company. The company makes software. → ___

### Cuando omitir el pronombre relativo

Puedes omitir **that/who/which** cuando es el **objeto** de la clausula (no el sujeto):

- "The book **(that)** I read was great." → Puedes omitir "that" (I es el sujeto)
- "The man **who** called me..." → NO puedes omitir "who" (es el sujeto)

**Regla rapida:** Si despues del pronombre relativo hay un **sujeto** (I, he, she, we, they), puedes omitirlo.

**Ejercicio — Puedes omitir el pronombre? Si/No:**
1. The movie that we watched → Si (we es sujeto)
2. The teacher who helped me → No (who es sujeto)
3. The food that she cooked → Si (she es sujeto)
4. The dog that bit me → No (that es sujeto)
5. The car that I bought → Si (I es sujeto)
