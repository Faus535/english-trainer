# Dia 45 — Connected speech nivel 2 (25 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Practicar connected speech con frases mas largas y aprender phrasal verbs de relaciones.

## Bloque 1: Connected speech — frases largas (10 min)

Ahora vamos con frases de 5-8 palabras, como las dicen los nativos de verdad.

### Frases completas y su pronunciacion real

| Lo que esta escrito | Lo que se dice | IPA aproximada |
|--------------------|--------------------|----------------|
| What are you going to do? | Whatcha gonna do? | /wɒtʃ.ə ˈɡʌn.ə duː/ |
| I should have told him. | I shoulda told him. | /aɪ ˈʃʊd.əv ˈtoʊld.ɪm/ |
| Do you want to come? | D'you wanna come? | /djə ˈwɒn.ə kʌm/ |
| I am going to eat. | I'm gonna eat. | /aɪm ˈɡʌn.ə iːt/ |
| What do you think? | Whaddya think? | /ˈwɒd.jə θɪŋk/ |
| I don't know. | I dunno. | /aɪ dəˈnoʊ/ |
| Let me see. | Lemme see. | /ˈlem.i siː/ |
| Give me a minute. | Gimme a minute. | /ˈɡɪm.i ə ˈmɪn.ɪt/ |
| Could you help me? | Couldja help me? | /ˈkʊdʒ.ə help miː/ |
| Would you like some? | Wouldja like some? | /ˈwʊdʒ.ə laɪk sʌm/ |

### Ejercicio: Di cada frase 3 veces

Primero lento (forma completa), luego normal, luego rapido (forma reducida):

1. **What are you going to do?** → What are you gonna do? → Whatcha gonna do?
2. **I should have told him.** → I should've told him. → I shoulda told him.
3. **Do you want to come?** → Do you wanna come? → D'you wanna come?
4. **What do you think?** → What d'you think? → Whaddya think?
5. **Give me a minute.** → Give me a minute. → Gimme a minute.

### Practica de comprension

Imagina que escuchas esto. ¿Que dice realmente?

1. "Whatcha gonna do about it?" → ___
2. "I shoulda called earlier." → ___
3. "Djya wanna come with us?" → ___
4. "Lemme think about it." → ___
5. "I dunno what happened." → ___

**Respuestas:**
1. What are you going to do about it?
2. I should have called earlier.
3. Do you want to come with us?
4. Let me think about it.
5. I don't know what happened.

## Bloque 2: Phrasal verbs — Relaciones (10 min)

| Phrasal verb | Significado | Ejemplo |
|-------------|-------------|---------|
| break up | romper (relacion) | They broke up last month. |
| make up | reconciliarse | They argued but made up quickly. |
| fall out | pelearse | I fell out with my best friend. |
| cheer up | animar | Cheer up! Things will get better. |
| let down | decepcionar | Don't let me down. |
| get along | llevarse bien | Do you get along with your sister? |
| ask out | invitar a salir | He asked her out on a date. |
| go out | salir (pareja) | They've been going out for 2 years. |
| settle down | sentar cabeza | He wants to settle down and start a family. |
| grow apart | distanciarse | We grew apart over the years. |

### Ejercicio: Completa la historia

Maria and Carlos started (1)___ (salir juntos) in 2019. They (2)___ (se llevaban bien) with each other's families. But slowly, they (3)___ (se distanciaron). Carlos felt (4)___ (decepcionado). They (5)___ (rompieron) in 2021. A year later, they met again and decided to (6)___ (reconciliarse).

**Respuestas:** (1) going out, (2) got along, (3) grew apart, (4) let down, (5) broke up, (6) make up
