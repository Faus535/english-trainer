# Dia 73 — Shadowing + Relative Clauses Errors (25 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Practicar shadowing con cualquier TED Talk y corregir los errores tipicos de hispanohablantes con relative clauses.

## Bloque 1: Shadowing (10 min)

**Elige cualquier TED Talk o video de YouTube** que te interese (5-10 min de duracion).

**Instrucciones:**
1. Escucha 15-20 segundos sin repetir
2. Rebobina y repite al mismo tiempo que el hablante
3. Imita todo: ritmo, entonacion, pausas, stress
4. Repite cada fragmento 2-3 veces antes de avanzar

**Sugerencias de videos:**
- Cualquier TED Talk que te interese
- BBC Learning English (videos cortos)
- Vox (documentales breves)

**Enfoque:** Nota como el hablante acentua las palabras de contenido y reduce las de funcion.

## Bloque 2: Relative Clauses — Errores comunes (10 min)

### Error 1: Usar "which" para personas
- MAL: "The man **which** called me."
- BIEN: "The man **who/that** called me."
- **Regla:** who/that para personas, which/that para cosas.

### Error 2: Usar "what" en lugar de "that/which"
- MAL: "The book **what** I read was great."
- BIEN: "The book **that** I read was great."
- **"What"** NO es un pronombre relativo en ingles estandar.

### Error 3: Comas en defining clauses
- MAL: "The man**,** who called me**,** is my father." (esto cambia el significado)
- BIEN: "The man who called me is my father." (defining = sin comas)
- **Con comas** cambia a non-defining: implica que ya sabes quien es "the man"

### Error 4: Pronombre redundante
- MAL: "The house where I live **in it**."
- BIEN: "The house where I live."
- MAL: "The man who I saw **him**."
- BIEN: "The man who I saw."

### Error 5: Usar "that" en non-defining
- MAL: "London, **that** is the capital, is expensive."
- BIEN: "London, **which** is the capital, is expensive."
- **Regla:** En non-defining (con comas), NUNCA uses "that".

**Ejercicio — Corrige estos errores:**
1. The woman which helped me was nice. → ___
2. The car what I bought is blue. → ___
3. My mother, that is a teacher, lives in Madrid. → ___
4. The hotel where we stayed in it was great. → ___
5. The boy which won the prize is my friend. → ___

**Respuestas:**
1. The woman **who** helped me was nice.
2. The car **that** I bought is blue.
3. My mother, **who** is a teacher, lives in Madrid.
4. The hotel **where** we stayed was great.
5. The boy **who** won the prize is my friend.
