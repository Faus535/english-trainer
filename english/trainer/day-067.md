# Dia 67 — Pronunciacion + Phrasal Verbs Viajes (25 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Dominar la asimilacion en connected speech y aprender phrasal verbs esenciales para viajar.

## Bloque 1: Assimilation en detalle (10 min)

La **asimilacion** ocurre cuando un sonido cambia para parecerse al sonido que le sigue. Esto es automatico en el habla nativa.

### /d/ + /j/ = /dʒ/ (como la J inglesa)

| Frase | Se escribe | Se pronuncia | IPA |
|---|---|---|---|
| Would you? | would you | wou-ju | /wʊdʒ.uː/ |
| Did you? | did you | di-ju | /dɪdʒ.uː/ |
| Could you? | could you | cou-ju | /kʊdʒ.uː/ |
| Had you? | had you | ha-ju | /hædʒ.uː/ |

### /t/ + /j/ = /tʃ/ (como CH)

| Frase | Se escribe | Se pronuncia | IPA |
|---|---|---|---|
| Got you | got you | gotcha | /ɡɒtʃ.uː/ |
| What's your? | what's your | whatcha | /wɒtʃ.jɔːr/ |
| Don't you? | don't you | don-chu | /doʊn.tʃuː/ |
| Won't you? | won't you | won-chu | /woʊn.tʃuː/ |
| Let you | let you | le-chu | /letʃ.uː/ |
| Meet you | meet you | mee-chu | /miː.tʃuː/ |

**Ejercicio — Lee en voz alta (forma rapida):**
1. Did you see that? → /dɪdʒ.ə siː ðæt/
2. Would you like some tea? → /wʊdʒ.ə laɪk sʌm tiː/
3. I got you a present. → /aɪ ɡɒtʃ.ə ə ˈprez.ənt/
4. Don't you think so? → /doʊn.tʃə θɪŋk soʊ/
5. What's your name? → /wɒtʃ.ər neɪm/

## Bloque 2: Phrasal Verbs — Viajes (10 min)

| Phrasal verb | Significado | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| check in | registrarse (hotel/vuelo) | We need to **check in** before 3 PM. |
| check out | dejar el hotel / mirar | We **checked out** at noon. |
| set off | salir de viaje | We **set off** early in the morning. |
| pick up | recoger (a alguien) | Can you **pick me up** at the airport? |
| drop off | dejar (a alguien) | I'll **drop you off** at the station. |
| get on | subir (transporte) | We **got on** the bus at Main Street. |
| get off | bajar (transporte) | **Get off** at the next stop. |
| take off | despegar (avion) | The plane **took off** on time. |
| touch down | aterrizar | We **touched down** in London at 8 AM. |
| stop over | hacer escala | We **stopped over** in Dubai for 4 hours. |
| see off | despedir (a alguien) | My family came to **see me off** at the airport. |

**Ejercicio — Completa con el phrasal verb correcto:**
1. The flight ___ 30 minutes late. (despegar)
2. I need to ___ at the hotel by 2 PM. (registrarse)
3. Can you ___ me ___ at the train station? (dejar)
4. We ___ the train at platform 5. (subir)
5. My parents came to ___ me ___ at the airport. (despedir)

## Bloque 3: Series sin subtitulos (5 min)

Mira 5 minutos de serie sin subtitulos. Intenta notar la asimilacion /dʒ/ y /tʃ/ en preguntas como "Did you...?" y "Don't you...?"
