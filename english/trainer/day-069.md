# Dia 69 — Repaso semanal (30 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Consolidar todo lo aprendido en la Semana 10: linking, elision, assimilation y reported speech.

## Bloque 1: Repaso activo (15 min)

### Test 1: Reported Speech — Transforma 3 citas directas

Transforma sin mirar apuntes:
1. "I'm going to the cinema tonight." → She said ___
2. "We have already finished the project." → They said ___
3. "I can't come tomorrow." → He told me ___

**Respuestas:**
1. She said she was going to the cinema that night.
2. They said they had already finished the project.
3. He told me he couldn't come the next day.

### Test 2: Pronunciacion — 5 frases enlazadas

Pronuncia estas frases aplicando linking, elision y assimilation:
1. "Did you eat?" → /dɪdʒ.uː iːt/ (assimilation)
2. "Next door" → /neks.dɔːr/ (elision de /t/)
3. "An orange" → /ən.ˈɒr.ɪndʒ/ (linking consonante-vocal)
4. "Got you" → /ɡɒtʃ.uː/ (assimilation)
5. "Last time" → /lɑːs.taɪm/ (elision de /t/)

### Test 3: Identifica elisions

Escucha 1 minuto de audio en ingles y marca donde desaparecen sonidos /t/ o /d/:
- Cuantas elisions detectaste: ___

**Si detectaste 3 o mas, excelente. Si menos de 2, repasa el Dia 64.**

### Test 4: Say vs Tell

Completa con say o tell:
1. She ___ me she was tired. (told)
2. He ___ that he would come. (said)
3. They ___ us the truth. (told)
4. I ___ I was sorry. (said)

## Bloque 2: Episodio con subtitulos en ingles (15 min)

Mira 15 minutos de un episodio con subtitulos en ingles.

**Mientras miras:**
- Nota las diferencias entre lo que lees y lo que oyes
- Identifica al menos 2 casos de reported speech en los dialogos
- Presta atencion a la assimilation en preguntas con "you"
