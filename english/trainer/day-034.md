## Bloque 2: Present Perfect — errores comunes (10 min)

Los hispanohablantes cometen errores predecibles con el Present Perfect. Aqui estan los 5 mas frecuentes.

### Tabla de errores

| # | ERROR (incorrecto) | CORRECCION | Regla |
|---|-------------------|------------|-------|
| 1 | I have seen it **yesterday**. | I **saw** it yesterday. | Con tiempo especifico → Past Simple |
| 2 | **Did** you **ever been** to Paris? | **Have** you **ever been** to Paris? | Ever/never → Present Perfect |
| 3 | I have **went** to the store. | I have **gone** to the store. | Usa el participio, no el pasado |
| 4 | She **has leave** already. | She **has left** already. | Participio de leave = left |
| 5 | I **have eat** sushi before. | I **have eaten** sushi before. | Participio de eat = eaten |

### Ejercicio: Corrige el error

1. I have went to London twice. → ___
2. Did you ever tried paella? → ___
3. He has wrote three books. → ___
4. We have saw that movie last week. → ___
5. She has already finish her homework. → ___

**Respuestas:**
1. I have **gone** to London twice.
2. **Have** you ever **tried** paella?
3. He has **written** three books.
4. We **saw** that movie last week. (last week → Past Simple)
5. She has already **finished** her homework.

### Participios irregulares problematicos

| Infinitivo | Past Simple | Past Participle |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| go | went | **gone** |
| see | saw | **seen** |
| eat | ate | **eaten** |
| write | wrote | **written** |
| do | did | **done** |
| take | took | **taken** |
| give | gave | **given** |
| speak | spoke | **spoken** |
| break | broke | **broken** |
| drive | drove | **driven** |
