# Dia 100 — Shadowing + Used to/Would Practice (30 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Shadowing con contenido casual moderno y practica intensiva de "used to" y "would" hablando de tu infancia.

## Bloque 1: Shadowing — Lenguaje Casual (15 min)

Hoy busca contenido con lenguaje **casual y moderno**: un vlog de YouTube, un podcast informal, o una entrevista relajada.

**Instrucciones:**
1. Elige un video de 5-7 minutos con lenguaje informal
2. Escucha 10-15 segundos, pausa, repite
3. Presta atencion a: contracciones ("gonna", "wanna", "kinda"), slang, y ritmo rapido
4. Repite cada fragmento 2-3 veces

**Sugerencias de contenido casual:**
- Vlogs de YouTubers nativos (elige temas que te interesen)
- Podcasts como "The Joe Rogan Experience" (fragmentos cortos)
- Entrevistas de celebridades en talk shows

**Enfoque especial:** ¿El hablante usa slang del dia 99? Marca cada termino que reconozcas.

**Ejercicio:** Despues del shadowing, habla 1 minuto en ingles sobre un tema libre. Intenta sonar **natural y casual**, no como un libro de texto.

## Bloque 2: Used to / Would — Habla de tu Infancia (15 min)

**Mision:** Habla de tu infancia usando "used to" y "would". Forma 10 oraciones.

**Guia:**

Piensa en tu ninez y completa estas frases:
1. "When I was young, I used to live in ___."
2. "I would play ___ every day after school."
3. "I used to be afraid of ___."
4. "My family would go to ___ every summer."
5. "I used to have a ___ (mascota/juguete/amigo)."
6. "We would eat ___ for dinner almost every night."
7. "I used to like ___ but now I don't."
8. "My parents would tell me ___."
9. "I didn't use to like ___ but now I love it."
10. "We would always ___ on weekends."

**Recuerda:** "Would" solo para habitos, "used to" para estados Y habitos.

**Ejercicio avanzado — Mini parrafo:**
Escribe un parrafo de 5-7 frases sobre tu infancia usando ambas formas:

> Ejemplo: "When I was a child, I used to live in a small town near the coast. I would ride my bike to school every morning. My best friend and I would play football in the park after school. I used to be very shy, so I didn't use to talk much in class. My family would go to my grandmother's house every Sunday for lunch. I used to love those Sundays — the food was amazing. We would spend the whole afternoon playing in the garden."

**Ahora escribe el tuyo:**

"When I was a child, ___..."

**Ejercicio oral:** Lee tu parrafo en voz alta 3 veces. La tercera vez, intenta decirlo sin leer.
