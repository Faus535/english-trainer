# Dia 16 — Pares minimos: /ɪ/ vs /iː/ avanzado (25 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Practicar /ɪ/ vs /iː/ en frases complejas y aprender los verbos irregulares 21-40.

## Bloque 1: /ɪ/ vs /iː/ en frases complejas (10 min)

Ya conoces los pares basicos (ship/sheep). Ahora vamos a frases donde ambos sonidos aparecen juntos.

**Frases de practica avanzada:**

1. "Did you **sit** /ɪ/ in the **seat** /iː/?"
   *(¿Te sentaste en el asiento?)*

2. "He will **live** /ɪ/ here, but she will **leave** /iː/."
   *(El vivira aqui, pero ella se ira)*

3. "**Each** /iː/ **inch** /ɪ/ matters."
   *(Cada centimetro importa)*

4. "Is this **chip** /ɪ/ **cheap** /iː/?"
   *(¿Es barata esta patata?)*

5. "I need to **fill** /ɪ/ it — I **feel** /iː/ empty."
   *(Necesito llenarlo — me siento vacio)*

6. "The **ship** /ɪ/ carried **sheep** /iː/ across the **sea** /iː/."
   *(El barco llevaba ovejas a traves del mar)*

7. "**His** /ɪ/ **feet** /iː/ **fit** /ɪ/ **these** /iː/ shoes."
   *(Sus pies caben en estos zapatos)*

8. "She **slipped** /ɪ/ while trying to **sleep** /iː/."
   *(Se resbalo intentando dormir)*

**Ejercicio:** Lee cada frase 3 veces. En la ultima, intenta decirla rapido sin perder la diferencia entre /ɪ/ y /iː/.

## Bloque 2: Verbos irregulares 21-40 (10 min)

| Base | Pasado | Participio | Español | Patron |
|------|--------|-----------|---------|--------|
| think | thought | thought | pensar | -ought |
| bring | brought | brought | traer | -ought |
| buy | bought | bought | comprar | -ought |
| begin | began | begun | empezar | i-a-u |
| sing | sang | sung | cantar | i-a-u |
| drink | drank | drunk | beber | i-a-u |
| write | wrote | written | escribir | cambio vocal |
| speak | spoke | spoken | hablar | cambio vocal |
| break | broke | broken | romper | cambio vocal |
| choose | chose | chosen | elegir | cambio vocal |
| drive | drove | driven | conducir | cambio vocal |
| eat | ate | eaten | comer | cambio vocal |
| fall | fell | fallen | caer | cambio vocal |
| grow | grew | grown | crecer | -ew/-own |
| hold | held | held | sostener | cambio vocal |
| keep | kept | kept | mantener | -ept |
| lead | led | led | liderar | cambio vocal |
| lose | lost | lost | perder | -ost |
| pay | paid | paid | pagar | -aid |
| run | ran | run | correr | cambio vocal |

**Patrones clave:**
- **-ought/-aught:** think→thought, bring→brought, buy→bought
- **i-a-u:** begin→began→begun, sing→sang→sung, drink→drank→drunk
- **-ew/-own:** grow→grew→grown, know→knew→known

Memoriza por patron, no uno a uno.
