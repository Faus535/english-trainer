### Patrones para recordar

- **Sin cambio (hit-hit-hit):** hit, hurt, set, rid, spread
- **Misma forma en past y participle:** dig-dug-dug, feed-fed-fed, hang-hung-hung, lend-lent-lent, light-lit-lit, shoot-shot-shot, slide-slid-slid, spin-spun-spun, stick-stuck-stuck
- **Tres formas diferentes:** freeze-froze-frozen, hide-hid-hidden, rise-rose-risen, shake-shook-shaken, steal-stole-stolen

### Ejercicio: Completa

1. The sun ___ (shine) brightly yesterday. → **shone**
2. She has ___ (hide) the present. → **hidden**
3. The ship ___ (sink) in 1912. → **sank**
4. He has ___ (overcome) many challenges. → **overcome**
5. They ___ (spread) the news quickly. → **spread**
