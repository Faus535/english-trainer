# Dia 93 — Shadowing + Indirect Questions Practice (30 min)

## Objetivo del dia
Practicar shadowing enfocado en entonacion y consolidar las preguntas indirectas con ejercicios intensivos.

## Bloque 1: Shadowing — Enfoque en Entonacion (15 min)

Elige un video de 5-7 minutos (TED Talk, entrevista, podcast con video).

**Instrucciones especiales para hoy:**
1. Primera escucha: solo marca mentalmente los patrones de entonacion ↗ ↘ ↗↘
2. Segunda escucha: repite imitando la entonacion EXACTA
3. Tercera escucha: repite al mismo tiempo que el hablante

**Presta atencion a:**
- Como sube la voz al final de las preguntas yes/no
- Como baja la voz al final de las afirmaciones
- Como usan fall-rise cuando dudan o son educados

**Ejercicio:** Elige 3 frases del video y repitelas 5 veces cada una, exagerando la entonacion. Luego dile normalmente.

## Bloque 2: Indirect Questions — Practica Intensiva (15 min)

**Introductores educados para preguntas indirectas:**

| Introductor | Formalidad | Ejemplo |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| Could you tell me...? | Formal | "Could you tell me where the exit is?" |
| Do you know...? | Neutro | "Do you know what time it starts?" |
| I was wondering if/whether... | Muy educado | "I was wondering if you could help me." |
| Would you mind telling me...? | Muy formal | "Would you mind telling me your name?" |
| Do you happen to know...? | Educado + casual | "Do you happen to know where she lives?" |
| Can you tell me...? | Informal | "Can you tell me how to get there?" |

**Ejercicio 1 — Transforma 10 preguntas directas:**

1. "Where is the nearest hospital?" → Could you tell me ___?
2. "What is your phone number?" → Would you mind telling me ___?
3. "Does she speak Spanish?" → Do you know ___?
4. "When did the meeting start?" → Do you happen to know ___?
5. "How long does the flight take?" → Could you tell me ___?
6. "Is there a supermarket near here?" → I was wondering ___
7. "Why was the office closed?" → Do you know ___?
8. "Who won the game?" → Do you happen to know ___?
9. "Can I bring a friend?" → I was wondering ___
10. "How do I get to the airport?" → Could you tell me ___?

> Respuestas: 1) where the nearest hospital is 2) what your phone number is 3) if she speaks Spanish 4) when the meeting started 5) how long the flight takes 6) if there's a supermarket near here 7) why the office was closed 8) who won the game 9) if I can bring a friend 10) how to get to the airport

**Error comun — Detecta el error:**
- "Could you tell me where is it?" → INCORRECTO ✗
- "Could you tell me where it is?" → CORRECTO ✓
- "Do you know what time does it open?" → INCORRECTO ✗
- "Do you know what time it opens?" → CORRECTO ✓

**Ejercicio 2:** Imagina que estas en un hotel. Escribe 3 preguntas indirectas que harias en recepcion.
